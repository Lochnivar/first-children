# Metaphysics

**Framework documentation for universal forces and metaphysical foundations in Hypatia.**

## Files

### [Quantum Foundation](quantum-foundation.md)
Quantum manipulation as the fundamental force across Hypatia. All "magic" is ultimately quantum manipulation—magic systems are comprehension tools that let mortal minds understand something way above their abilities.

### [Coherent Patterns](coherent-patterns.md)
Ghosts, spirits, and non-corporeal entities as coherent energy patterns at the quantum layer. All existence (physical bodies, ghosts, spirits, etc.) are patterns of coherent energy—ghosts/spirits are looser, non-corporeal patterns that retain coherence without dense physical binding.

### [Souls and Identity](souls-and-identity.md)
Souls as self-anchored consciousness—the third type of existence. Memory as identity structure (chain of causal experience). Why memory loss threatens dissolution. Ghosts as degrading chains. Therapy as identity architecture.
