# Axiarch Solon

**World:** Iolanu | **Gender:** Male | **Race:** [TBD]  
**Status:** Antagonist  
**Role:** Knight of the Ebon Throne; Axiarch of the Church of Malachor; interrogator  

**Appearance:** Balding; gentle, beneficent smile that never wavers; [further detail TBD]

**Backstory:** High-ranking priest and institutional architect of Malachor's Church. Does not delight in pain — considers that an amateur's mistake. His purpose is extraction of truth in service to Malachor, and he is methodically good at it. Has trained with the best healers specifically to keep subjects alive long enough to give a full accounting. Views his work as harvest, not butchery.

**Tell:** The smile never wavers. You cannot read anything in it. That is the worst part.
