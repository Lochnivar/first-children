# Gabby D'Olivier *(born Boudicca Jane Call)*

**World:** Iolanu | **Gender:** Female | **Race:** Caelori (host) — Earth soul (Manchester, NH)  
**Status:** Main  
**Role:** Greyrose Ranger; Nala's frontline agent on Iolanu  

**Appearance:** Milk-white skin with freckles; flaming red hair in a long braid; slate-blue eyes; extensive Caelori tattoos chin to toes; greyrose flower embedded below left clavicle; ~5'5", fit and limber

**Backstory:** American foster kid who died in a hiking accident and was transplanted by Nala into the body of a Caelori woman named Laila. Navigates an alien world in a stranger's body, carrying that stranger's tattoos, muscle memory, and community obligations. Serves as a Greyrose Ranger while concealing her Earth origin from everyone except the Apostasy.

**Tell:** Keeps asking *why* when everyone around her simply knows. Treats every Iolanu norm like a puzzle she has to solve before she'll accept it — one beat behind socially, three beats ahead morally.
