# Chance Hodgkins

**World:** Iolanu | **Gender:** Male | **Race:** Thohalla (formerly; now disembodied)  
**Age:** ~1,796 years (born ~IY 200; disembodied since the Shattering, IY 1381)  
**Status:** Antagonist  
**Role:** Ancient Quantumancer; disembodied soul; seeks to complete the Shattering  

**Appearance:** No body; soul only. Formerly a Thohalla fisherman.

**Backstory:** A fisherman who discovered quantumancy through grief, became immortal through accident, and lost his mind when the one person who kept him grounded died. Was used by Nala — without fully knowing it — to shatter the Godstone. Got the worst possible outcome: body destroyed, soul intact, quantum haze unrelenting, unable to die, unable to finish what he started. Has existed in this state for over a thousand years.

**Tell:** He didn't start nihilistic. He started in love. Everything after that is what the world did with it.
