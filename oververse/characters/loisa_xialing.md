# Loisa Xialing

**World:** Iolanu | **Gender:** Female | **Race:** Orindi (host) — Earth soul  
**Status:** Main  
**Role:** Intelligence Chief, Nala's Apostasy; cover identity: hydrologist  

**Appearance:** Pale Orindi skin; ash-blonde hair; glass-blue eyes; greyrose placed horizontally across the belly (hidden by Llosca dress)

**Backstory:** One of Nala's most experienced Earth souls, integrated so completely into Iolanu that no one — except Gabby — has ever guessed her origin. Runs intelligence operations from Burmallosca under a hydrologist cover. Built her entire Iolanu self from absolute zero and has never let the mask slip in the field.

**Tell:** Three moves ahead, always. The only crack in the mask is when Gabby accidentally quotes something from Earth — and even then, it looks like a cough.
