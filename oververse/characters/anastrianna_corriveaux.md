# Anastrianna Corriveaux

**World:** Unclaimed | **Gender:** Female | **Race:** Human  
**Status:** Sketch  
**Role:** Ranger / Mastermind Hybrid — expert tracker and skirmisher who runs multi-layered operations and hand-picks specialists into a crew

**Appearance:** Tall, willow-slender with sculptural bone structure that reads as deceptively fragile. Warm parchment-toned skin; hazel eyes that shift toward green or gold with mood or light; deep auburn hair in a practical chignon with one strand that always escapes. Favors tailored traveling leathers and a long functional coat in forest green or charcoal, reinforced for rough work and loaded with hidden pockets. A ranger's cloak that shifts to blend with surroundings. Always carries a compact, well-worn tactical journal that is far heavier than it appears.

**Backstory:** Mid-30s. Leads from the front or the shadows with the calculated precision of a Nate Ford-style orchestrator. Her plans succeed more often than not, which has earned her a formidable and sometimes feared reputation. She can read as a complete bitch — pushy, impatient with incompetence, quick to override — but to those who earn her loyalty she is unfailingly protective and will take the fall for her people without hesitation. Her core flaw is a control blindspot: she over-refines plans, micromanages at critical moments, and doesn't leave enough room for her team to improvise. When it goes wrong she recognizes her own role almost immediately. Admitting it publicly is slow and painful. Gets marginally better at it with time.

**Voice:** Dry, surgical wit with an elegantly arched eyebrow and perfect timing.
- *"Darling, if I wanted your opinion I would have asked three contingencies ago."*
- *"Next time you decide to go off-script, at least have the courtesy to be wrong about it."*
- *"How adorably predictable. I've already won — I'm just waiting for you to catch up."*
- *"I don't mind initiative. I mind initiative that hasn't been properly budgeted into my plan."*
- *"You're not wrong. You're simply… premature. There's a difference."*

**Tell:** A slow inhale, shoulders dropping slightly, fingers flexing once — the one moment of visible recalibration before she snaps back into command presence. Unconscious. She doesn't know she does it. Her crew knows exactly what it means.
