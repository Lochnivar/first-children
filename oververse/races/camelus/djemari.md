# Djemari (Camel People)

**Status:** Framework Element - Universal Race  
**Category:** Races / Species

## Overview

Djemari are camel-based anthropomorphic people created through FC panspermia. Water conservation, endurance, phlegmatic calm. Not a designated role or biome lock. Peak phlegmatic: nothing bothers them. Low-energy calm; biologically adapted to economical movement and minimal emotional expenditure.

## Universal Characteristics

### Physical Attributes
- **Height/Size:** 7–8 feet tall
- **Build:** Humped backs (water/fat storage); broad, padded feet; endurance build
- **Lifespan:** Long-lived (varies by world)
- **Distinctive:** Can go days without water; closable nostrils (particulate protection); sandy brown to dark brown coloring

### Cognitive & Behavioral Traits
- **Unflappable:** Nothing bothers them. Ever. At all. World ending? "Mm. Noted."
- **Minimal emotion:** Not suppressed, just don't have much
- **Deadpan delivery:** Everything in monotone
- **Economical:** Minimal energy expenditure; why waste?
- **Phlegmatic:** Biologically adapted to low-energy state; panic wastes resources

### Social Characteristics
- **Community:** Nomadic routes (established circuits, not endless wandering); trade caravans (seasonal routes)
- **Culture:** Water is sacred (never waste); endurance competitions (pride); oral storytelling tradition

## Natural Gravitation

Water conservation + endurance + phlegmatic calm → long-haul work, low-resource efficiency. Traits lead naturally to transport, hauling, steady-state operations. Not designed for logistics; logistics is just what happens when you're built to go forever without complaining.

## Relationships

- **Llamari:** Often work together on long-haul routes. Ultimate calm meets ultimate anxiety—comedy gold through contrast. Both professionally competent despite appearances; mutual respect between specialists.
- **Kitsune:** Trade interactions; Djemari don't get swindled (too patient to rush a deal).
