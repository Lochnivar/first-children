# Genus Procyon

**Status:** Framework Element - Universal  
**Category:** Races / Genus Definition  
**Related:** [Genetic Frameworks](../genetic-frameworks.md), [../README.md](../README.md)

## Definition

Genus Procyon is defined by the raccoon-derived genetic template from the FC genetic repository. All Procyon expressions—sapient and non-sapient—share these core traits:

### Physical
- **Dexterity-forward** — Sensitive hand pads; fine manipulation; strong grip in hands and forearms
- **Mobility** — Climbing, balance, squeezing into tight spaces; often nocturnal-adapted senses (night vision, whiskers)
- **Markings** — Facial mask patterning common; ringed tail in many expressions

### Temperament
- **Opportunistic generalism** — Thrives in marginal and edge environments; quick to repurpose resources
- **Social flexibility** — Strong loyalty to chosen in-group; skeptical default toward external authority

### Framework
- **Non-sapient baseline:** Raccoons (Earth template)
- **Sapient expression:** [Bandari](bandari.md) (*Procyon callidus*) — traits tend toward salvage, frontier trade, fixers, and gray-area economy
