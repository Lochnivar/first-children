# Genus Lepus

**Status:** Framework Element - Universal  
**Category:** Races / Genera  
**Related:** [Sungrun](sungrun.md), [Races README](../README.md), [Genetic Frameworks](../genetic-frameworks.md)

## Overview

Lepus is the genus framework shared by rabbit and hare species across the multiverse. The non-sapient baseline is well-established on Earth and FC-seeded worlds — small, fast, prey-adapted animals defined by extraordinary sensory capability, speed, and social architectures built around collective survival.

The sapient expression of this genus is the **Sungrun** (*Lepus acutis*): anthropomorphic rabbit-people whose defining trait is the same as their baseline cousins, scaled to sapience.

## Framework Characteristics

**Non-sapient baseline:** Rabbits, hares. Prey animals. Defined by sensory acuity, speed, and warren structures built around shared awareness.

**Core traits carried into sapient expression:**
- Extraordinary sensory range — hearing, smell, vibration, pressure sensitivity
- Wide-angle environmental awareness
- Speed and evasion as primary survival mechanism
- Social architecture built around information sharing
- Crepuscular alertness pattern — most acute at liminal times

## Earth Relatives

Earth rabbits and hares are the non-sapient template — the Lepori's biological cousins in the same way Earth foxes are cousins to Kitsune.
