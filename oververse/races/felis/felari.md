**Status:** Framework Element - Universal Race  
**Category:** Races / Species

## Overview

Felari (*Felis elegans*) are feline-derived anthropomorphic people created through FC panspermia—cousins to the Nekari, sharing the Felis genetic template. Where Nekari pursue perfection of purpose or action, Felari pursue perfection of appearance or manners. Same genus, different orientation: Nekari are about the job; Felari are about the presentation.

### Truism
*"Appearance is courtesy."* — Double meaning: "Look your best" (polite) and "You're lucky we grace you with our presence" (edge).

## Universal Characteristics

### Physical Attributes
- **Height/Size:** Medium; sleek, graceful (same Felis baseline as Nekari)
- **Build:** Predator template refined for elegance
- **Lifespan:** Long-lived (varies by world)
- **Senses:** Night vision, enhanced senses
- **Distinctive:** Grooming and presentation are paramount

### Cognitive & Behavioral Traits
- **Appearance-conscious:** Not vain—it's the craft
- **Manners as weapon and tool:** Social grace deployed with precision
- **Perfection-oriented:** Of appearance, etiquette, and presentation
- **Socially adept:** Navigate courts, salons, high-end negotiations

### Social Characteristics
- **Community:** Social circles; reputation and presentation-based hierarchy
- **Culture:** Etiquette, fashion, aesthetics, social refinement; diplomacy, hosting, cultural arbiters

## Natural Gravitation

Perfection of appearance → etiquette, fashion, aesthetics, social refinement. Perfection of manners → diplomacy, hosting, cultural arbiters. Often found in roles where presentation matters: courts, salons, high-end negotiations. Not designed for it; elegance is simply who they are.

## Relationships

- **Nekari:** Cousins. Nekari: perfection of purpose. Felari: perfection of appearance. Mutual respect for different crafts; occasional rivalry.
- **Kitsune:** May overlap in social spaces. Felari: presentation. Kitsune: transaction. Different tools, sometimes the same rooms.

## Nekari/Felari Contrast

| | Nekari | Felari |
|---|--------|--------|
| **Perfection of** | Purpose, action | Appearance, manners |
| **Focus** | The job gets done | How it looks when it's done |
| **Truism** | "Perfection is not vanity, it is duty." | "Appearance is courtesy." |

Both share Felis traits: predator template, solitary tendency, pride. Nekari channel that into precision work; Felari channel it into social grace, aesthetics, and the cultivation of presence.
