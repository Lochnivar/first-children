## Overview

Capra Quantus (common name: Riftkeepers; Anchor Goats) are Virelle's purpose-built transit infrastructure staff at Mercara's Cross. Goat-derived anthropomorphic beings with quantum-sensitive horns, territorial binding to the Grand Anchor, and compressed speech patterns. They monitor platforms, maintain schedule precision, and detect quantum anomalies. Cannot psychologically leave The Cross.

**Authorial note:** Without the behavioral/Anchor binding, Capra Quantus could be used elsewhere as quantumancy detectors—quantum-sensitive horns, early warning for flux, illegal magic detection. Don't restrict usage to the Cross only.

## The Anchor Binding

- Psychological binding (not physical tether)
- Territorial instinct amplified—Anchor is THEIR territory
- Can physically leave but psychologically cannot
- Leaving = abandoning territory (unthinkable)
- 100+ feet: anxiety; 500+ feet: panic; 1000+ feet: breakdown

## Quantum Sensitivity

- Horns sense: quantum flux, soul pattern corruption, dimensional thinning, quantumancy use
- Detect problems 3–5 seconds before instruments
- Greater Dragons feel "quantum-wrong"—Riftkeepers scream at them continuously

## Speech Patterns

- Staccato, compressed (fragments, no articles)
- Present tense dominant; pauses mid-thought
- "Platform 47. Earth. Five minutes. Board now."
- Caused by quantum perception scrambling; can't change without losing detection speed

## The Screaming

- **Emergency:** "MAAHHH!" — platform malfunction
- **Territorial:** "AAAHHHHH!" — get off my platform
- **Schedule violation:** "SCREEEEE!" — stop delaying
- **Quantum detection:** "EEEHHHHHHH!" — something wrong
- **Collective:** All Riftkeepers—Grand Anchor crisis (1–2×/year)

## The Silence Problem

- Perfect schedule = silence = anxiety trigger
- Low traffic (e.g., 3–6 AM) → bleating → escalates → creates minor flux
- "Too quiet" feels wrong; horns say quantum too perfect
- Virelle accepts 99.7% efficiency; can't fix without breaking detection
