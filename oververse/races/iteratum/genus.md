**Status:** Framework Element - FC Experimental  
**Category:** Races / Genus Definition

## Definition

Genus Iteratum is the FC's classification for experimental species created outside the standard genetic repository — custom biology, non-template design, specific experimental briefs. Each species in the genus represents a distinct experiment. Physical form, biology, lifespan, and cognitive traits vary by species; what they share is origin, not anatomy.

Species are classified as Iteratum when they meet three criteria:
- Created directly by the FC (not seeded, not purpose-built as staff)
- Not derived from any template in the Earth genetic repository
- Produced under a specific experimental brief rather than a functional designation

The genus is open. As FC experimental species are identified, documented, or rediscovered, they are classified here regardless of biological similarity to other Iteratum species.

### Framework
- **Repository status:** FC Experimental — not in standard genetic repository; no designated roles
- **Known species:**
  - [Weavers](weavers.md) (*Iteratum textori*) — immortality experiment; biological identity impermanence in physically immortal bodies
