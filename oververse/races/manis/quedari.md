# Quedari (Manis sapiens)

**Status:** Framework Element - Universal Race  
**Category:** Races / Species  
**Related:** [Genus Manis](genus.md), [Races README](../README.md)

## Overview

Quedari are pangolin-based anthropomorphic people created through FC panspermia. They survived their trial (847+ years) by passive avoidance—curling into armored spheres and waiting. FC verdict: passed, but "boring—do not replicate." They exist. That's sufficient.

**No designated role.** Not all races have specific purposes; some simply are.

### Truism
*"Everything passes. Wait long enough."*

## Universal Characteristics

### Physical Attributes
- **Height/Size:** 4–5 feet tall; stocky, armored
- **Build:** Chitinous plates (brown/gray); low center of gravity; optimized for defensive curling
- **Lifespan:** 400–600 years
- **Distinctive:** Defensive curl—tuck limbs, head, tail → complete armored sphere; shed plates periodically (source of trade goods)

### Cognitive & Behavioral Traits
- **Patient:** Deeply patient; minimal energy expenditure
- **Deliberate:** Slow movement, minimal speech; no wasted motion
- **Curl when uncertain:** Uncurl when certain
- **Minimal verbal communication:** Silence preferred

### Social Characteristics
- **Community:** Loose collective; minimal governance; consensus when needed
- **Culture:** Burrow networks; communal storage; dispute resolution: wait. Chitin trade (shed scales → armor, shields, containers).

## Natural Gravitation

No natural gravitation toward any profession. Quedari simply *are*. Their patience makes them compatible with roles requiring waiting, but they don't seek roles. "Sufficient" is optimal.

## Relationships

- **Capra quantus:** Too loud, too anxious; avoid when possible
- **Humans:** Active, curious; exhausting but pay fair prices
- **Chelonari:** Also patient, also shelled; different priorities (they help others; Quedari help themselves)
- **Hobgoblins:** Understand procedures; minimal conversation; compatible

## Native World & History

### Origin
FC working group creation (not Epsilon—different generalist attempt). Panspermia-seeded.

### Trial History
- **Trial:** Passed via complete avoidance of trial mechanics
- **Duration:** 847+ years
- **Strategy:** Survived rifts, predators, scarcity, conflict by curling and waiting
- **Repository:** Active; approved but deprioritized
- **FC verdict:** "Boring—do not replicate"
- **Earth deployment:** ~200–300 on Earth (Platform 934); Burrow District, Manchester; rural New Hampshire. Quiet locations; avoid cities; "sufficient" income, then stop.

### Governance & Politics
Loose collective; minimal governance; consensus when needed. No leaders; no hierarchy; decisions by waiting until agreement emerges or the issue resolves itself.

### Legal Code
Dispute resolution: wait. If both parties wait long enough, either the dispute resolves or it stops mattering.
