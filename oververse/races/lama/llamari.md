# Llamari (Llama People)

**Status:** Framework Element - Universal Race  
**Category:** Races / Species  
**Related:** [Genus Lama](genus.md), [Djemari](../camelus/djemari.md), [Races README](../README.md)

## Overview

Llamari are llama-based anthropomorphic people created through FC panspermia. Efficient oxygen usage, sure-footedness, hypervigilance. Not a designated role or biome lock. Their temperament is anxiety incarnate: perpetually worried about everything. Almost as if OCD were given a personification—the checking, the worry spirals, the need to account for every possibility. Vigilance keeps them alive—and makes them incredibly capable despite (or because of) their worry.

### Truism
*"There's always time for one last check."*

## Universal Characteristics

### Physical Attributes
- **Height/Size:** 5–6 feet tall (shorter, stockier)
- **Build:** Thick, woolly coats (natural insulation); split hooves, perfect balance
- **Lifespan:** Moderate to long (varies by world)
- **Distinctive:** Large lungs (efficient oxygen usage); white, brown, black, spotted patterns

### Cognitive & Behavioral Traits
- **Hypervigilant:** Scanning for dangers constantly; prey animal instincts; one mistake = death
- **Worry spirals:** What if, what if, what if; catastrophize everything
- **Compulsive checking:** Need to CHECK things repeatedly; verbal processing (talk through anxieties constantly)
- **Capable despite anxiety:** Actually SUPER competent—anxiety doesn't stop them, enhances vigilance
- **Zen-like until threatened:** Then aggressive spitting

### Social Characteristics
- **Community:** Communal care (guard each other); slow, methodical movement (safety first)
- **Culture:** Meditation practices (quiet focus); weaving traditions (use their own wool)

## Natural Gravitation

Efficient O2 + sure-footed + hypervigilant → transport, logistics, precision work. Traits lead naturally to roles requiring vigilance and careful execution. The anxiety that looks like a weakness is actually the engine of their competence.

## Relationships

- **Djemari:** Often work together on long-haul routes. Ultimate anxiety meets ultimate calm—comedy gold through contrast. Both professionally competent despite appearances; mutual respect between specialists.
