# Aubrei (Testudo aubreii)

**Status:** Framework Element - Universal Race  
**Category:** Races / Species  
**Related:** [Genus Testudo](genus.md), [Chelonari](chelonari.md), [Races README](../README.md)

## Overview

Aubrei are tortoise-based anthropomorphic people—naval-themed Testudo, cousins to the Chelonari. They share the same core genus traits: shell-backed, long-lived, patient, slower of movement and thought. The key difference: Aubrei are more aggressive. Their culture and identity center on the sea—ships, naval tradition, maritime life. Same family, different temperament.

### Truism
*"We carry shells. We also carry teeth."*

## Universal Characteristics

### Physical Attributes
- **Height/Size:** Medium; slow-moving (same as Chelonari)
- **Build:** Shell-backed; can retreat into shell
- **Lifespan:** 300–500 years
- **Distinctive:** Naval adaptations vary by world (webbed digits, salt tolerance, etc.)

### Cognitive & Behavioral Traits
- **Patient:** Same Testudo baseline—deliberate, unhurried
- **Aggressive edge:** Willing to act when provoked; more combative than Chelonari
- **Protective:** Of their ships and crews; fierce loyalty
- **Methodical:** Plan carefully, execute with force

### Social Characteristics
- **Community:** Ship crews; naval traditions; coastal/island settlements
- **Culture:** Maritime tradition, naval identity; ships and the sea as central to who they are

## Natural Gravitation

Patient + shell-backed + long-lived + aggressive edge → naval combat, ship defense, maritime protection. Same patience as Chelonari channeled into seafaring and warfare rather than healing.

## Relationships

- **Chelonari:** Cousins. Same core; different temperament. Chelonari help, Aubrei fight. Trade partners or distant, varies by world.
