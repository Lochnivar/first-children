## Overview

Ilvari are the fae you'd mistake for a First Child if you didn't know better. They know you might make that mistake. Their relationship to that fact varies by individual.

Tall, presence-heavy, aesthetically extreme in the direction of too-perfect rather than too-strange. Associated with courts, long memory, and games that run longer than most species live. FC find them the most unsettling of the fae precisely because they're the most person-shaped — but wrong, in a way that's hard to locate.

**Singular/Plural:** Ilvari  
**Adjective:** Ilvarine

### Truism
*"Nobody forgets meeting an Ilvari. The Ilvari usually forgot meeting you."*
