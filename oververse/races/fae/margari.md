## Overview

Margari are threshold fae. They are found at edges: doorways, shorelines, mountain passes, dawn, dusk, the moment before a decision becomes irreversible. Neither here nor there is not a limitation for them — it is the address.

Guide, trickster, ferryman — context determines which. Possibly all three simultaneously. The substrate expressing itself at the places where one state becomes another.

**Singular/Plural:** Margari  
**Adjective:** Margarine *(this will get jokes; noted)*

### Truism
*"If you met one at a doorway, you were being assessed. Whether you passed is a separate question."*

## One Line Summary

They live in the between — not the place, not the destination, but the crossing.
