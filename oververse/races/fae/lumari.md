## Overview

Lumari are small, luminescent, and operating on priorities that don't map cleanly onto anything else. Not malicious. Not benevolent. Present in the way that a spark is present — briefly, brightly, and then somewhere else.

Associated with curiosity, light, and the kind of mischief that isn't really directed at you but happens to involve you. The most commonly *encountered* of the fae and probably the least *understood*.

**Singular/Plural:** Lumari  
**Adjective:** Lumarine

### Truism
*"The light you saw was probably a Lumari. What it wanted is still unclear."*
