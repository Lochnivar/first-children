# Genus Gekko

**Status:** Framework Element - Universal  
**Category:** Races / Genus Definition  
**Related:** [Genetic Frameworks](../genetic-frameworks.md), [../README.md](../README.md)

## Definition

Genus Gekko is defined by the gecko-derived genetic template from the FC genetic repository. All Gekko expressions—sapient and non-sapient—share these core traits:

### Physical
- **Climbing-specialized** — Adhesive toe pads; strong three-dimensional spatial sense; wiry, precise movement
- **Sensory** — Large eyes; excellent low-light vision; vocal range broader than many species expect; coloration often temperature-responsive
- **Autotomy** — Tail shed/regrow capacity (degree varies by expression)

### Temperament / cognition
- **Temperature-coupled metabolism** — Ambient warmth tends to speed cognition and expressiveness; cool conditions favor deliberation and precision (biology, not illness)
- **Environmental attunement** — Strong tendency to adapt to local context when sapient; patience for long immersion

### Framework
- **Non-sapient baseline:** Geckos (Earth template)
- **Sapient expression:** [Geliss](geliss.md) (*Gekko versalis*) — traits tend toward cross-context fluency, liaison, embedded scholarship, and analysis
