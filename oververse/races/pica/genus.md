## Definition

Genus Pica is defined by the magpie-derived genetic template from the FC genetic repository. Panspermia species; no designated role. Survived trial by **knowing what everything cost before committing to it, including the trial itself**.

### Physical
- **Magpie-derived** — 4'9"–5'5"; compact, quick; appear to take up more space than they measure
- **Plumage** — Black and white with iridescent blue-green in wings and tail; shifts in direct light; reads as theatrical; this is just how they look
- **Wings** — Flight capable; rarely used for long distance; they prefer to be on the ground, in the room, in the work
- **Hands** — Dexterous; built for turning things over and finding the angle
- **Tail** — Long, iridescent, overdressed by any reasonable standard

### Temperament
- **Transaction as vocabulary** — Express care, loyalty, warning, and regard through exchange; consistently misread as mercenary
- **Private ledger** — Maintain precise internal accounting of who has earned what from them; never displayed
- **Already in the room** — Do not ask permission to be present; inform you of a fact you were about to overlook
- **Strategic vividness** — Assessments delivered in terms vivid enough that you cannot pretend you misunderstood
- **Teaches by doing** — Model the lesson; never explain it while giving it
- **Volume drop** — Loud when performing; goes very quiet when serious; the volume drop is the tell

### Framework
- **Repository status:** Active (survived trial, included)
