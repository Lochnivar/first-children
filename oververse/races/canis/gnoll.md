## Overview

Gnolls (*Canis jocularis* / *Canis festivus*) are hyena-derived anthropomorphic people. Chaos-souls; improv troupe social structure; Festivus. No designated role—emergent. Shared genetic repo root with Cayminus (coyote folk); divergent branch with stronger hyena-derived social and vocal traits.

## Quick Reference

- **Taxonomy:** Canis jocularis (official) / Canis festivus (common—Gnoll-adopted)
- **Social unit:** Troupe (pack as improv troupe; fluid leadership)
- **Core trait:** Chaos-soul orientation—perceive quantum absurdity; warmth until crossed
- **Distinctive:** Layered, infectious laughter; Festivus (Seinfeld-adopted holiday)
