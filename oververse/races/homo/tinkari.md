# Tinkari (Homo technis)

**Status:** Framework Element - Universal Race  
**Category:** Races / Species  
**Related:** [Genus Homo](genus.md), [Dwarves](dwarves.md), [Races README](../README.md)

## Overview

Tinkari are First Children purpose-built for operations—Quantum DevOps. Where Dwarves build and Hobgoblins record, Tinkari keep things running. They monitor anchor networks, patch quantum instabilities, maintain transit systems, and program the crawl. Same role everywhere: production support. Mercara's Cross trains, Earth's transformation, theway systems, rift mitigation—if it runs, they run it.

### Truism
*"It's not a bug, it's a feature—until it's a bug again."*

## Universal Characteristics

### Physical Attributes
- **Height/Size:** Smaller than humans (gnome-sized)
- **Build:** Nimble, quick, built for crawling through infrastructure
- **Lifespan:** Long-lived (typically 150–250 years, varies by world)
- **Distinctive:** Nimble fingers, sharp eyes for dashboards and subtle quantum drift; high metabolism (always moving, always checking); can work in tight spaces

### Cognitive & Behavioral Traits
- **Pattern recognition:** Spot degradation before it cascades
- **Improvisation:** Fix things with what's available
- **Multitasking:** Monitor six systems while explaining the problem to a Hobgoblin
- **On-call mentality:** Something's always about to break
- **Pragmatic over proper:** "Ship it, we'll patch"

### Social Characteristics
- **Community:** On-call rotations and incident postmortems; war stories about the time the crawl almost inverted
- **Culture:** Highly caffeinated—Tinkari have made caffeine their sacrament; their brews are legendary, their tolerance higher. Friendly but distracted (half-listening to the monitoring feed).

## Framework Role: FC Staff

### Origin
Created by First Children as purpose-built operators for divine infrastructure.

### Designated Role
- Monitor and maintain quantum systems (anchors, theway, crawl parameters)
- Operational support for FC infrastructure
- Transit maintenance (Mercara's Cross trains, gateways, conduits)
- Incident response (quantum instabilities, rift spawns, decay)
- Deploy fixes, rollbacks, patches—"fix it in production"
- Automation and self-healing systems

### Deployment Contexts
- **Mercara's Cross:** Maintaining the trains, scheduling, gate alignment, platform quantum stability
- **Earth:** Programming the crawl—transformation parameters, phase transitions, mortal interface calibration
- **Trial worlds:** Monitoring anchor decay, rift suppression, theway wetware patches

## Relationships

- **Dwarves:** Respect them (they built it) but don't understand why they won't just hotfix.
- **Hobgoblins:** Get along (they document; Tinkari need the runbooks).
- **Halflings:** Keep Tinkari workspaces stocked with coffee; grudging appreciation.
