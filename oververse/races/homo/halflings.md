## Overview

Halflings in Hypatia are First Children purpose-built logistics specialists—Homo halfinius—who handle supply chains, distribution, and coordination. Small, nimble, and practical, they get the right thing to the right place at the right time. "Everything just works" because halflings make it work. Same genus as Humans, Elves, Hobgoblins, Dwarves, and Tinkari; different model for a different job.

## Universal Characteristics

### Physical Attributes
- **Height/Size:** Small (typically 3–4 feet tall)
- **Build:** Stocky, proportionate but compact, nimble; strong for their size
- **Lifespan:** Moderate to long (typically 90–150 years, varies by world)
- **Distinctive:** Curly hair (including on feet—natural padding for walking); space-efficient, warehouse-optimized, high carrying capacity relative to size; efficient metabolism (can work long hours on minimal food, but eat frequently by choice); fit into cargo holds, tight conduits, storage bays

### Cognitive & Behavioral Traits
- **Obsessively organized:** "Everything has a place"; cannot tolerate chaos; will reorganize unsolicited
- **Detail-oriented:** Notice everything, spot discrepancies, count compulsively
- **Practical intelligence:** Routing, scheduling, resource allocation
- **Memory:** Good for routes, manifests, schedules, institutional knowledge
- **Risk-averse:** "Better overstocked than under"; multiple backup plans

### Social Characteristics
- **Community:** Strong family/clan ties ("My cousin can get that for you")
- **Culture:** Seven meals per day: Breakfast, Second Breakfast, Elevensies, Lunch, Afternoon Tea, Dinner, Supper ("Proper fuel for proper work"). Meticulous record-keeping (ledgers, logs, lists spanning generations). Comfortable infrastructure (cushioned chairs, soft lighting, climate controlled; "Happy staff = efficient staff").
- **Hospitality as protocol:** Offer food/drink constantly; warm, welcoming—but still counting what you consume.

## Variations Across Worlds

Worlds may present halflings differently:
- **FC Staff (primary):** Logistics specialists for FC infrastructure
- **Traditional:** Community-focused, comfort-seeking, adaptable—same traits, different cultural expression
- **Cultural:** Shire halflings, nomadic halflings, city halflings—different expressions based on world

Individual worlds can include, exclude, or modify this framework characterization.

## Strengths & Weaknesses

**Strengths:**
- Organization (can organize anything from memory)
- Networking (know everyone, connected everywhere)
- Prediction (anticipate needs, pattern recognition)
- Negotiation (fair but shrewd, know value of everything)
- Hospitality (build relationships through generosity)

**Weaknesses:**
- Combat (small, avoid conflict, hire security)
- Risk-taking (too cautious, miss opportunities, analysis paralysis)
- Improvisation (need structure; chaos is terrifying)
- Speed (methodical, thorough, slow—"haste makes waste")
