## Overview

Ogres are Orcus magnus—the tank/siege tier of the Orcus genus. Heavy damage output, huge health pool, environmental hazards. Too stupid to fully understand their purpose. Just hungry, angry, fighting. "Ogre hungry. Ogre smash. Ogre die. Ogre... confused."

## Universal Characteristics

### Physical Attributes
- **Height/Size:** 10–12 feet tall, enormously strong
- **Build:** Proportionally massive; huge appetite (high caloric need)
- **Lifespan:** 5–10 years (expected)
- **Distinctive:** Yellowish-green to brown skin; not very bright (trade-off for size); surprisingly fast when motivated

### Cognitive & Behavioral Traits
- **Simple:** Food-motivated; easily confused/angered
- **Strong but lazy:** Need supervision; prefer eating to fighting
- **Childlike:** In some ways—not malicious, just hungry and confused
- **Not fully aware:** Too stupid to fully understand their purpose; die confused

### Social Characteristics
- **Community:** Need supervision (often managed by Orcs)
- **Culture:** Eating; smashing; confusion

## Framework Role: Trial Adversary

### Purpose
Heavy/siege encounters—teach dodging, test sustained damage, destroy fortifications. Food-motivated (can be distracted; comedic but dark).

### Combat Profile
- Maximum strength; demolition capability
- Slow (telegraphed attacks, learnable patterns)
- Stupid (exploitable; use environment against them)
- Huge health pool; heavy damage output
