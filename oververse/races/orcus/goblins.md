# Goblins (Orcus minor)

**Status:** Framework Element - Adversary  
**Category:** Races / Species  
**Related:** [Genus Orcus](genus.md), [Genetic Frameworks](../genetic-frameworks.md), [../../mechanics/trials.md](../../mechanics/trials.md)

## Overview

**WARNING: Adversary use only. Not for staff deployment.**

Goblins are Orcus minor—the swarm mob tier of the Orcus genus. Designed to be killed en masse. Low-level tutorial enemies, ambush attackers, loot hoarders. They know they're weak. They constantly flee. "We are prey. That is our purpose."

## Framework Role: Trial Adversary

### Adversary Designation
Low-level swarm enemies

### Physical Traits
- 3-4 feet tall, wiry, quick, expendable
- Sharp teeth, large eyes (low-light vision)
- Green-grey skin
- High reproduction rate

### Purpose in Trials
- Tutorial enemies (easy kills, teach basics)
- Ambush attackers (teach awareness)
- Swarm tactics (teach crowd control)
- Loot rewards (hoard treasure)

### Design Cruelty
- Designed to be killed en masse
- Know they're weak, constantly flee
- Reproduce just to replace losses
- "We are prey. That is our purpose."

### Personality
- Cruel, cowardly individually
- Brave in groups (mob mentality)
- Cunning in petty ways
- Treasure-obsessed
- Cackling, chaotic

### Expected Lifespan
2-6 months

## Known Issues

Goblins occasionally achieve unexpected intelligence. Immediate culling recommended.

## Variations Across Worlds

- Swarm size and tactics may vary
- Loot behavior and hoarding patterns differ
- Some worlds may have goblin populations outside trial contexts (rare)
