## Overview

Goblins are Orcus minor—the swarm mob tier of the Orcus genus. Designed to be killed en masse. Low-level tutorial enemies, ambush attackers, loot hoarders. They know they're weak. They constantly flee. "We are prey. That is our purpose."

### Truism
*"We are prey. That is our purpose."*

## Universal Characteristics

### Physical Attributes
- **Height/Size:** 3–4 feet tall, wiry, quick
- **Build:** Expendable; sharp teeth, large eyes (low-light vision)
- **Lifespan:** 2–6 months (expected)
- **Distinctive:** Green-grey skin; high reproduction rate

### Cognitive & Behavioral Traits
- **Cruel:** Individually cowardly; brave in groups (mob mentality)
- **Cunning:** In petty ways; survival instinct sharp despite design
- **Cackling, chaotic:** Constant noise, constant movement
- **Treasure-obsessed:** Hoard compulsively

### Social Characteristics
- **Community:** Swarm-based; no stable structures; numbers are safety
- **Culture:** Hoarding; chaos; brief, frantic lives

## Framework Role: Trial Adversary

### Purpose
Tutorial enemies—teach basics, awareness, crowd control. Designed to be killed en masse; reproduce to replace losses.

### Combat Profile
- Swarm tactics; ambush attackers
- Individually weak; dangerous in numbers
- Loot rewards (hoard treasure for player incentive)
