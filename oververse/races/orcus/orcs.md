# Orcs (Orcus sapiens)

**Status:** Framework Element - Adversary  
**Category:** Races / Species  
**Related:** [Genus Orcus](genus.md), [Genetic Frameworks](../genetic-frameworks.md), [../../mechanics/trials.md](../../mechanics/trials.md)

## Overview

**WARNING: Adversary use only. Not for staff deployment.**

Orcs are Orcus sapiens—the standard combat tier of the Orcus genus. Primary adversaries in trials. Territory holders, boss fights, tactical challenges. The tragedy: smart enough to know they're designed to lose. Organized enough to try fighting back. Strong enough to win sometimes. But ultimately designed to be overcome. "We fight. We lose. We die. We are replaced. This is the cycle."

## Framework Role: Trial Adversary

### Adversary Designation
Mid-tier combat opponents

### Physical Traits
- 6-7 feet tall, heavily muscled
- Green skin, prominent tusks
- High pain tolerance, fast healing (minor wounds)
- Aggressive disposition

### Purpose in Trials
- Primary adversary (bulk of combat)
- Territory holders (create objectives)
- Boss fights (chieftains, warlords)
- Tactical challenges (use weapons, armor, basic strategy)

### The Tragedy
- Smart enough to know they're designed to lose
- Organized enough to try fighting back
- Strong enough to win sometimes
- But ultimately designed to be overcome
- "We fight. We lose. We die. We are replaced. This is the cycle."

### Personality
- Brutal, straightforward
- Respect strength (hierarchical)
- Combat-focused, "might makes right"
- Surprisingly organized (militaristic)

### Philosophy
- "We are weapons. We exist to be used and broken."
- "At least you CHOSE to enter this trial. We were BORN into it."
- "Kill us quickly. We will return."

### Post-Trial Acceptance
"We know. When trial ends, we end." "Better than endless cycle." "At least make it quick."

### Expected Lifespan
2-5 years

## Known Issues

Orcs sometimes organize beyond design parameters. Rebellion risk.

## Variations Across Worlds

- Some worlds may have orc populations with different cultural expressions (see generic orc framework for non-adversary variations)
- Trial adversary (Orcus sapiens) is the primary framework designation
