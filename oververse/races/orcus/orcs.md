## Overview

Orcs are Orcus sapiens—the standard combat tier of the Orcus genus. Primary adversaries in trials. Territory holders, boss fights, tactical challenges. The tragedy: smart enough to know they're designed to lose. Organized enough to try fighting back. Strong enough to win sometimes. But ultimately designed to be overcome. "We fight. We lose. We die. We are replaced. This is the cycle."

### Truism
*"We fight. We lose. We die. We are replaced. This is the cycle."*

## Universal Characteristics

### Physical Attributes
- **Height/Size:** 6–7 feet tall, heavily muscled
- **Build:** Green skin, prominent tusks; aggressive disposition
- **Lifespan:** 2–5 years (expected)
- **Distinctive:** High pain tolerance, fast healing (minor wounds)

### Cognitive & Behavioral Traits
- **Brutal, straightforward:** Respect strength; hierarchical
- **Combat-focused:** "Might makes right"
- **Surprisingly organized:** Militaristic; use weapons, armor, basic strategy
- **Self-aware:** Smart enough to know they're designed to lose; this is the tragedy

### Social Characteristics
- **Community:** Military hierarchy; chieftains, warlords
- **Culture:** Combat as identity; strength-based hierarchy
