## Overview

Trolls are Orcus gigantus—the elite/boss tier of the Orcus genus. Bridge guardians, area denial, near-immortal until someone learns fire. They live long enough (centuries) to get very smart. Know they're unkillable... until someone learns the weakness. Watch generation after generation die to the same pattern. Can't escape their nature. "We are eternal... until we're not. We guard... because we must."

### Truism
*"Troll does not hate you. Troll simply... IS."*

## Universal Characteristics

### Physical Attributes
- **Height/Size:** 8–10 feet tall, massive build
- **Build:** Extremely tough hide; green-brown skin, sometimes mossy growth
- **Lifespan:** Centuries (regenerative)
- **Distinctive:** Enhanced regeneration (regrow limbs slowly over days/weeks); can eat almost anything

### Cognitive & Behavioral Traits
- **Slow-thinking, patient:** Long-lived; become very intelligent over centuries
- **Solitary:** Don't need others
- **Territorial:** Simple but not stupid; calm until provoked
- **Mournful:** Centuries of the same role; depression is common
- **Self-aware:** Know they're unkillable until someone learns fire; watch generations die to the same weakness

### Social Characteristics
- **Community:** Solitary; territorial; don't form groups
- **Culture:** Guarding as identity; bridges, passages, territory markers

## Framework Role: Trial Adversary

### Purpose
Elite/boss encounters—set piece battles, bridge guardians, area denial. Teaching moment: rewards knowledge (fire/acid weakness).

### Combat Profile
- Near-immortal (regeneration); slow but unstoppable
- Territorial; regenerate in place
- Weakness to fire/acid (learnable, rewardable)
- Boss-tier threat; block progress until weakness discovered
