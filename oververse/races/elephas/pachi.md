# Pachi (Elephant People)

**Status:** Framework Element - Universal Race  
**Category:** Races / Species  
**Related:** [Genus Elephas](genus.md), [Races README](../README.md)

## Overview

Pachi are elephant-based anthropomorphic people created through FC panspermia. Their traits—wanderlust, philosophical, melancholic—tend toward movement, trade, and contemplation. Not a designated role; their biological compulsion to travel and their religion (the search for "The Perfect Place") provide meaning for their endless journey. Caravans and trade emerge naturally from who they are.

### Truism
*"The search gives wandering purpose."*

## Universal Characteristics

### Physical Attributes
- **Height/Size:** Large, strong build
- **Build:** Massive frame, patient
- **Lifespan:** 300+ years
- **Distinctive:** Incredible memory; enhanced wanderlust (neurological)

### Cognitive & Behavioral Traits
- **Philosophical:** Deep thinkers, contemplative, melancholic wisdom
- **Wanderlust:** Biological compulsion to travel—cannot stay anywhere long
- **Patient:** Gentle, observant, steady
- **Memory:** Remember everything—routes, faces, conversations, debts

### Social Characteristics
- **Community:** Mobile civilization built around the journey; strong social bonds maintained across distance
- **Culture:** Caravan-centered life; oral traditions; trade as connection

## Natural Gravitation

Wanderlust → tends toward movement, caravans, trade. Philosophical, contemplative → often merchants, witnesses, travelers. Long memory, social bonds → mobile civilization built around the journey. Not designed for commerce—commerce finds them because they're always moving and always remembering.
