# Pachi (Elephant People)

**Status:** Framework Element - Universal Race  
**Category:** Races / Species  
**Related:** [Genus Elephas](genus.md), [Races README](../README.md), [../../FRAMEWORK.md](../../../FRAMEWORK.md)

## Overview

Pachi are elephant-based anthropomorphic people created through FC panspermia. Their traits—wanderlust, philosophical, melancholic—tend toward movement, trade, and contemplation. Not a designated role; their biological compulsion to travel and their religion (the search for "The Perfect Place") provide meaning for their endless journey. Caravans and trade emerge naturally from who they are.

## Universal Characteristics

### Physical Attributes
- Large, strong, patient build
- Enhanced wanderlust (neurological)
- 300+ year lifespans
- Incredible memory

### Emergent Tendencies
- Wanderlust (neurological) → tends toward movement, caravans, trade
- Philosophical, contemplative → often merchants, witnesses, travelers
- Long memory, social bonds → mobile civilization built around the journey

### Technology
- Highly efficient, self-contained caravans
- Mobile manufacturing, hydroponics, medical capabilities
- Developed because they refused to stop moving
- (Tech baseline varies by world; no futuristic weapons in trial worlds)

### Personality
- Deep wanderlust (biological compulsion)
- Philosophical, contemplative
- Melancholic wisdom
- Gentle but can't stay anywhere long
- "We have to move. It's who we are."

### Religion/Philosophy
- Searching for "The Perfect Place" to finally settle
- Know it probably doesn't exist
- Religion provides framework for biological wanderlust
- "The search gives wandering purpose"
- Consciously choose meaning despite truth

## Story Potential

### Story Function
- Philosophical depth (religion as chosen meaning)
- Witnesses to human condition
- Merchants/information source
- Contrast (they're at peace despite endless journey)

### Key Theme
"Truly, is The Road so much different than your God? Your minds tell you that you close your eyes at the end of your life and you cease. Yet, your hearts tell you that you will once again open them in a new place, beyond pain, beyond suffering, beyond this crawl."

## Variations Across Worlds

- Caravan technology and capabilities vary by world
- Cultural expressions of wanderlust may differ
- Relationship to trial worlds varies
