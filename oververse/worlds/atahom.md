## Overview

**Atahom** (First Speech **`'THM`**, common speech **Tahom** / *the world*) is an endless vertical realm of stacked dungeon floors, Stacks, the Collapse, and the Pulse. There is no surface and no confirmed bottom; civilization lives inside the descent and migrates on a metronomic schedule. Etymology and formal naming: [First Speech — Atahom](atahom/world-building/first-speech-lexicon.md).

**First Speech (deployment token):** `'THM` — system/orientation labeling for this world in trial and quest-OS fiction (same convention as other Hypatia world tokens).

**Genre note:** **Not LitRPG** and **not a dungeon-crawler setting** — civilizational fiction; vertical realm as geology and society, not XP or encounter design. See [Narrative Framing](atahom/world-building/narrative-framing.md).

## World-specific expansions (from the bible)

- **Stacks** — vertical civilizational columns; depth race; lateral connection corridors that rotate each Collapse cycle.
- **Collapse** — synchronized, metronomic reclaim of the top occupied floor across all Stacks.
- **Pulse / Distributor** — universal heartbeat; calendar built on Pulses, Hands, Fists, Spans, Floors.
- **Guild** — neutral cross-Stack cartography, certification, and archive.
- **Theology** — Reclaimists, Descenders, Ascenders (competing readings of the same facts).
