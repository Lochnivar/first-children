## Overview

This document will detail the First Children involved in Stratara's creation and game. Currently, this is a placeholder for future development.

## Framework Principles

**When Developing First Children:**
- No personality stereotypes should be established for First Children
- Each First Child has unique motivations and goals
- They vary widely in personality (from wise to cruel)
- They are siblings with complex relationships
- Some are "more equal than others" (power/hierarchy differences)

## Known Information

**Number of Players:**
- 4-6 First Children siblings collaborated to create Stratara
- Exact number and identities to be determined

**Roles:**
- One claimed primacy as "lead author" (sparking conflict)
- Others challenged this claim
- External First Children observe and judge
