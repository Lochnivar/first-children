# Kitzengai

**Earth Reference:** Peruvian cliff terraces + Himalayan hermit monasteries

## Physical Characteristics
- **Skin:** Sun-darkened with altitude pallor
- **Hair:** Black to dark brown, often braided or tied back
- **Eyes:** Deep brown to warm amber
- **Build:** Lean, sure-footed builds with strong calves and grip-conditioned hands

## General Clothing Notes
Heavy layered wraps and shawls for cold; windproof skirts and stitched leather gaiters; woven textiles with geometric patterns and prayer-cloth panels for ritual use.

## Cultural Elements
- **Kitzigen Falls:** Location associated with Ladyfinger flower cultivation and Eirendor's shrines
- **Vertical architecture:** Cliff terraces and high-altitude settlements
