# Llosca (Reimagined Andalusian)

**Earth Reference:** Andalusian merchants and Sufi mystic brotherhoods through an Arabian Nights lens

## Physical Characteristics
- **Skin:** Warm olive to golden bronze
- **Hair:** Dark brown to black, often glossy and styled long or in loose braids
- **Eyes:** Deep amber, molten brown, or sea-green with heavy lashes and expressive brows
- **Build:** Lithe, sinuous posture common

## General Clothing Notes
Flowing silks, translucent veils, embroidered waistcoats, ornate belts with hanging coins, jeweled hairpieces, scented scarves, filigree amulets, henna patterns, and indoor slippers or curled toe boots; public dress is elegant and functional, private dress sensuous and ritualized.

## Cultural Elements
- **Merchant culture:** Andalusian trading traditions
- **Sufi mysticism:** Mystic brotherhoods and spiritual practices
- **Arabian Nights aesthetic:** Ornate, luxurious, and ritualized private spaces
- **Dual presentation:** Elegant public function vs. sensuous private ritual

## Notes
This is a reimagined version of Llosca culture, distinct from the Dutch/Southeast Asian canal town version. Consider which version applies to your narrative context.
