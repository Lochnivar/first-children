# Wrinomo

**Earth Reference:** Swiss–Vietnamese fusion; Alpine lake–basin engineers and stilted shore settlements

## Physical Characteristics
- **Skin:** Olive to light tan
- **Hair:** Dark brown to sun-streaked
- **Eyes:** Freshwater blues, slate greens, warm browns
- **Build:** Broad-chested commons and slightly finer magistrate lineages inland; frequent calluses and ledger-stamp marks

## General Clothing Notes
Nobility wear brocade-lined cloaks, organza aprons, and fur trims; commons use indigo cotton, oiled linen, compressed-reed cloth, felt hats, short wool tunics, wrap-front bodices, utility obis, crampon-ready boots, and pale-lens goggles.

## Cultural Elements
- **Alpine lake engineering:** Stilted shore settlements and lake-basin infrastructure
- **Swiss-Vietnamese fusion:** Blend of alpine and Southeast Asian architectural and cultural elements
