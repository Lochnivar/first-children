# The Burmallosca Incident

**Category:** Historical Event / Character Relationship / Cultural Context
**Related Characters:** Loisa Xialing, Grayson McKenzie
**Related Locations:** Burmallosca (Llosca capital)
**Related Organizations:** Hierarchy of Lust (Church of Malachor), Nala's Apostasy

## Overview
A historical event that occurred years before Gabby's orientation tour. Loisa's extraction from Burmallosca went wrong when Inquisitors of the Hierarchy of Lust encountered her and Grayson. To avoid detection, they had to couple publicly (Llosca culture accepts public intimacy). It was REAL coupling, not simulated, as the Inquisitors are experts who would detect faking. Both survived but were scarred differently, creating a complex bond that defines their friendship.

## What Happened

### The Situation
- **Location:** Burmallosca (Llosca capital, seat of the Hierarchy of Lust)
- **Context:** Loisa's extraction/operation in Burmallosca went wrong
- **Encounter:** Inquisitors of the Hierarchy of Lust found them
- **Problem:** Needed to avoid detection while in Llosca territory

### The Solution
- **Cultural Context:** In Llosca culture, public intimacy is acceptable (not sexualized, culturally normal)
- **Action Required:** Had to couple publicly to blend in and avoid detection
- **Reality:** REAL coupling, not simulated (Inquisitors are experts, would detect faking)
- **Outcome:** Both survived, avoided capture
