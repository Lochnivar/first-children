# Cosmological Awareness: Delverium

**Status:** World-Specific Expansion  
**Category:** Mortal Awareness / Cosmology  
**Related:** [First Children](../../cosmology/first-children.md), [../README.md](README.md)

## Overview

Delverium has an **open cosmology**—mortals are aware of the creator and understand the System is engineered for their growth. This contrasts with closed cosmology worlds like Iolinus.

## What Mortals Know

### About the Creator

**Zorathar as "The Architect":**
- Mortals know Zorathar exists
- View the System as divine gift/natural law hybrid
- Understand it's engineered for their growth

**Framework Alignment:**
- Open cosmology variant (framework allows)
- Mortals aware of creator (framework variant)
- Different from closed cosmology (like Iolinus)

### About the System

**Comprehension:**
- Comprehend soul patterns and anchors intuitively via interface
- No full grasp of quantum bedrock (that's haze-inducing)
- Accept gamified reality as normal

**Framework Alignment:**
- Cultural magic interface makes quantum accessible (framework concept)
- Full quantum understanding causes haze (framework limitation)
- Mortals interpret through cultural lenses (framework pattern)

### Religious Perspectives

**Varied Beliefs:**
- Some sects worship System as meritocratic providence
- Others see it as a test or trial
- Philosophy debates about purpose of endless challenge

**Framework Alignment:**
- Mortals interpret cosmology through cultural lenses (framework pattern)
- Varied religious perspectives exist (framework allows)

### About Other Worlds

**Rumors:**
- Rumors exist of other worlds in the Library
- Soul transfers rare (only for liberation tests or high-level rewards)
- Most mortals focused on Delverium's challenges, not cosmology

**Framework Alignment:**
- Other worlds exist (framework truth)
- Soul transfers are rare (framework pattern)
- Mortals may or may not know about other worlds (framework allows)

## Framework Conformance

**Conforms to Framework:**
- Open cosmology is allowed (framework variant)
- Mortal awareness varies by world (framework rule)
- Mortals interpret through cultural lenses (framework pattern)
- Rumors of other worlds exist (framework allows)

**World-Specific Expansions:**
- Open cosmology with "The Architect" concept
- System as divine gift/natural law hybrid
- Focus on growth and meritocracy
- Limited awareness of other worlds
