# Mortal Society: Delverium

**Status:** World-Specific Expansion  
**Category:** Cultures / Society  
**Related:** [../README.md](README.md)

## Overview

Delverium's mortal society is structured around a meritocracy where status is determined by level, achievements, and cleared dungeons. The System affects everyone, creating a culture focused on progression and challenge.

## Population Distribution

### Adventurers

**Elite Class:**
- Pursuing dungeon challenges
- Cultural ideal—heroes and celebrities
- Range from solo delvers to raid guilds

**Framework Alignment:**
- Mortals pursue quantum manipulation through System (framework-aligned)

### Non-Adventurers

**Everyone Has the System:**
- Basic levels/stats for all
- Crafters, merchants, scholars use it for mundane progression
- Example: farmers level up farming skills, blacksmiths level metalworking
- Support infrastructure for adventurer economy

**Framework Alignment:**
- System provides quantum access to all (framework-aligned)
- Cultural magic interface makes quantum accessible (framework concept)

## Social Structure

### Meritocracy

**Status Determination:**
- Status determined by level, achievements, cleared dungeons
- Legendary dungeon completions grant prestige
- Leaderboards shape social hierarchy

**Framework Alignment:**
- Demonstrates balance/imbalance philosophy (framework theme)
- Meritocracy rewards adaptation (framework principle)

### Guilds

**Organization:**
- Organized adventuring groups
- Provide support, resurrection access, training
- Compete for dungeon completion records

**Framework Alignment:**
- Mortal organizations exist (framework allows)

### Economic System

**Loot-Based Economy:**
- Trading stabilized quantum patterns (gear)
- Service industries supporting delvers
- Loot as stabilized energy patterns

**Framework Alignment:**
- Loot is stabilized energy patterns (framework truth)
- Trading patterns is quantum manipulation (framework-aligned)
