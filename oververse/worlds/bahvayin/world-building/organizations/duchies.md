# The Duchies of Bahvayin

**Category:** Organizations & Institutions  
**Related:** [Political Hierarchy](political-hierarchy.md), [Demographics](demographics.md), [Client Kingdoms](client-kingdoms.md)

## Overview

Twenty-eight regular Duchies across the empire — inner Akhan islands, mainland territories, rim zones, and inside the client kingdoms where Kings grant ducal titles under their own authority. Each entry: location, ruler, one-line character, succession crisis posture.

Duchies inside client kingdoms answer to their King, not the Emperor. Their succession crisis position is shaped by their King's position rather than direct claimant pressure.

---

## Inner Akhan Island Duchies

**Duchy of Varath** — Northern inner Akhan, mid-sized island, timber and fishing economy. Duke Ossian (Human, old family, third generation). *Competent, uninspiring, exactly what the island needs.* Wants the succession resolved so the trade contracts stabilize; has received three claimant delegations and fed them all well.

**Duchy of Kelen** — Eastern inner Akhan, sits on a natural deep harbor used as a secondary naval anchorage. Duchess Mira vel-Kelen (Human, appointed two reigns ago, confirmation still technically pending from the current crisis). *Her confirmation is the leverage every claimant keeps mentioning and never delivers.* Leans toward whoever confirms her first — has been waiting four years.

**Duchy of Shoram** — Central inner Akhan, small wealthy island, banking and letters of credit. Duke Taveris (Hobgoblin, third generation, runs a tighter financial operation than most Regencies). *The duchy that quietly holds debt from two claimant houses and has not called it in yet.* Not political — financial. The distinction is becoming academic.

**Duchy of Amrath** — Southern inner Akhan, between Avir and Kol Idic, fishing and coastal trade. Duke Halvorn (Human, inherited six months ago, unconfirmed). *Young, nervous, being courted by everyone because his confirmation is cheap to offer and his harbor is useful.* Has not made a decision. Is aware he should make a decision. Has not made a decision.

**Duchy of Belavar** — Western inner Akhan, sits between Zafaran and Mare Shafel, agricultural and transit. Duchess Sera (Human, old landowning family, predates the empire in the region). *Her family was here before the empire and will be here after; acts accordingly.* Views the succession crisis as an administrative inconvenience and has told three claimant delegations exactly this.

**Duchy of Narath** — Northwestern inner Akhan, Shavon-adjacent, grain surplus and river delta. Duke Corvin (Corvani, unusual appointment, earned through thirty years of river management that saved the delta from silting). *The Duke nobody expected to last who has now outlasted three emperors.* Has opinions about the northern consolidation that nobody has asked for.

**Duchy of Irath** — Northeast inner Akhan, between Kerath and Stormbreak's approach, peat and timber. Duke Bram (Human, frontier stock, built the duchy from a barony two generations ago). *Rough around the edges in ways the imperial court finds alarming and his people find reassuring.* Waiting to see which claimant has the most credible plan for the Eye.

## Mainland Duchies — Southwestern (Western Range Eastern Slopes / Archavar Highland Interior)

**Duchy of Andalvar** — Highland interior valley of the Archavar, the luxury corridor. *Ducal seat vacant 18 months; administered by County council pending Vaelor's appointment — which Vaelor is not making.* Contains two Counties and the highest concentration of prestige production in the empire. See [duchy-andalvar.md](duchy-andalvar.md) for full treatment.

- **County of Kern** *(within Andalvar)* — Eastern slopes, vineyards, Kernish Red. Count Aldric vel-Kern (Human, old vine family). *Kernish Red. Every claimant has sent a very nice letter. The Count has sent back equally nice letters and a case of the current vintage.* No commitments. Warm correspondence.
- **County of Mak'Zavar** *(within Andalvar)* — Deep mountain mines, gem cutting, Quedari sacred extractors. Count-in-waiting pending confirmation; the aged Count has no heir. *Every claimant has sent their most delicate representative. All have received a finished piece of extraordinary quality and an invoice.*

**The Rim Baronies** *(within Andalvar)* — Small mining settlements, pass controls, and quarry operations on the peaks that define Andalvar's boundary — within the duchy by definition. Answer to Andalvar's Duke; currently answering to the County council (ducal seat vacant), which none of them find satisfactory. Brief entries:
- *Barony of Marak* — Iron ore, seventh-generation Dwarf family. Sends claimants a price list.
- *Barony of Gadar* — High pass control, winter toll revenue, eight years of unusual crossing logs nobody has asked about.
- *Barony of Ashvel* — Sulfur and salt. Hobgoblin Baroness who responded to a "strategic resource guarantee" inquiry with a detailed invoice.
- *Barony of Talvar* — Quarry, imperial palace stone. Human Baron, four-generation quarry family, in no hurry. His Felari wife has been trying to determine for eleven years what she saw in him.

**Duchy of Sharath** — Southern mainland coast, between Mare Shafel and the Southern Way mouth, warm climate agriculture and coastal fishing. Duke Orin (Human, Mare Shafel's administrative reach in practice). *Exists in Mare Shafel's orbit without being absorbed by it — a distinction the Duke maintains carefully.* Follows Mare Shafel's lead on the succession but won't say so publicly.

**Duchy of Taval** — Western Range foothills, central section, logging and mountain resource extraction. Duchess Yara (Bruin, frontier family, holds territory the empire has never fully administered). *The map says this is imperial territory. The Duchess's expression suggests she has read the map and found it charming.* Technically loyal. Practically independent.

**Duchy of Meron** — Andalusian slopes, central western arc, mixed agriculture and artisan production. Duke Cassir (Felari, court appointment, genuinely gifted administrator who was given this posting as a reward and has made it exceptional). *The duchy that actually works — efficient, prosperous, and slightly embarrassing to its neighbors.* Will ratify whoever produces the best administrative framework. Is the only Duke who has sent the claimants a detailed questionnaire.

**Duchy of Halvar** — Northern mainland, western arc interior, mining and cold climate agriculture. Duke Torren (Dwarf, hereditary, family has worked these mines for six generations). *Knows what is in the ground and knows what it is worth and is in no hurry to tell anyone.* The succession crisis is noise from the south. The northern consolidation is a question he has begun to take seriously.

## Mainland Duchies — Northern Rim Adjacent

**Duchy of Ramath** — Northern inner atoll coast, just below the rim, gateway to the northern rim's western approach. Duke Sevan (Kibo, military appointment earned in the last northern rim conflict two generations ago, family has held since). *Frontier military culture, professional, the Duke who actually reads Tzafan's scout reports.* Has forwarded summaries to three claimants. Received acknowledgment from none. Finding this instructive.

**Duchy of Valdur** — Northern rim, cold climate, resource extraction and fur trade. Duchess Kira (Human, frontier stock, holds her territory by being the most competent person in it). *The sort of ruler who knows every family in her duchy by name and the northern consolidation as a personal professional concern rather than an abstract political one.* Has sent a detailed report to Baram's warehouse master. They have compared notes.

**Duchy of Thoran** — Northeastern mainland, between the northern rim and the inner Akhan coast, timber and river trade. Duke Mavar the Younger (Human, named after the Duchy of Mavar which his grandfather served — a deliberate honor that creates mild confusion in official correspondence). *Earnest, capable, slightly overwhelmed by the succession crisis, doing his best.* Has backed no claimant but has quietly asked Regent Sorath for guidance. Sorath has not yet replied.

## Mainland Duchies — Desert Road Adjacent

**Duchy of Karath** — Western mainland, Desert Road eastern terminus approach, trade and logistics. Duke Harven (Bandari, unusual appointment, earned through three decades of Desert Road logistics management that made the western approach actually function). *The Duke the Djemari will actually negotiate with — a relationship worth more than his title.* Functionally holds the land bridge between imperial territory and Djemari country. Both sides find this useful.

**Duchy of Seval** — Western mainland, south of the Desert Road corridor, dry climate ranching and trade. Duchess Omara (Llamari, frontier appointment, holds difficult terrain nobody else wanted). *Quiet, competent, completely off the succession crisis's radar — which she finds restful.* Has been watching the Desert Road traffic patterns with professional interest.

## Storm Gate Region Duchies

**Duchy of Gathar** — Eastern inner Akhan, just inside the Storm Gate, port and pilot culture. Duke Adren (Human, Gate Pilot family elevated two generations ago, still operates as a Pilot himself). *The Duke who knows the Storm Gate's currents better than most engineer-priests.* His duchy's economy depends entirely on the Storm Gate functioning. The breakwater sabotage is not an abstract concern.

**Duchy of Surath** — Eastern atoll rim, south of the Storm Gate, storm coast culture and fishing. Duke Vorn (Kibo, frontier military, holds a coast that requires someone willing to hold it). *Doesn't speak much at imperial court. Is not invited often. Both parties find this satisfactory.* Watches what washes up on his coast with professional attention.

## Southern Way Region Duchies

**Duchy of Carath** — Southern inner Akhan, Southern Way adjacent, warm climate and trade. Duchess Lena (Halfling, logistics family elevated for services to the Southern Way trade network, third generation). *Knows where everything is, where it came from, and where it is going — including several things the empire would rather not know about.* Has received every claimant delegation with warm efficiency and told each one exactly what they needed to hear to leave satisfied.

**Duchy of Mirval** — Southern inner Akhan, fog belt edge, fishing and coastal trade. Duke Shan (Aubrei, coastal family, holds the inner edge of the fog belt approach). *The Duke who knows what the Aubrei behavioral change means and has told exactly no one.* Waiting for someone to ask the right question.

## Client Kingdom Internal Duchies

*These Duchies answer to their respective Kings, not the Emperor. Their succession crisis position is filtered through their Kingdom's position.*

**Duchy of Coral Reach** *(Stormbreak)* — Inner reef administrative zone. Duchess Pella (Human, Stormbreak old family). *Manages the inner reef operations so the King can focus on the outer reef politics.* Reliable, invisible, essential.

**Duchy of Kavah's Rim** *(A'Kavah)* — Caldera rim territory, high ground agriculture. Duke Tern (Felari, estate family, caldera rim landowners for five generations). *The duchy that controls A'Kavah's defensive high ground and has never needed to use it.* Watches the Halfling distribution networks with quiet concern.

**Duchy of Tavar's Deep** *(San Tavar)* — San Tavar's outer island territories. Duke Rei (Kitsune, appointed through San Tavar's meritocratic framework after twenty years of commercial intelligence work). *Knows things. Shares things selectively. The Queen trusts him completely, which is the most frightening endorsement available.* 

**Duchy of the North Gate** *(Tzafan)* — Northern approach territory, expedition staging. Duke Harrak (Bruin, frontier ranger family, holds the actual northern gateway). *The Duke who personally knows every scout who has reported the northern consolidation.* Takes the reports seriously. Has increased the garrison quietly.

**Duchy of Idic's Shore** *(Kol Idic)* — Kol Idic's coastal and harbor territory. Duchess Vela (Chelonari, archival family, manages Kol Idic's external-facing operations). *The public face of an institution that prefers not to have a public face.* Extremely helpful. Extremely careful about what help means.

## Notes

- Several ducal confirmations are currently pending — the succession crisis has produced a backlog of unconfirmed hereditary successions that every claimant is using as leverage
- The client kingdom internal duchies are not available for claimant collection — they answer to their Kings
- Duke Mavar the Younger's naming confusion has produced at least three misdirected official correspondence incidents in the last year; Zafaran's archivists have flagged this for resolution
