## Overview

Shavon sits in the northwestern inner Akhan, close enough to Tzafan that northern news arrives early and far enough from any center of power that there is nothing useful to do with it. The island is agricultural — grain, vegetables, the ordinary food production that feeds the northwestern rim settlements that cannot feed themselves. It has a working harbor, a modest merchant fleet, and a Grand Duke who administers the duchy competently without distinguishing himself in any particular direction.

The duchy has been here as long as the empire has. It will probably be here after whatever comes next.

---

## The Island

Shavon's economy is mixed agriculture and coastal trade. The northwestern inner Akhan climate is cool but workable — long enough growing seasons for grain and root vegetables, reliable enough rainfall that irrigation is supplemental rather than essential. The duchy feeds itself and exports the surplus northward to the rim settlements that depend on inner Akhan supply.

The harbor handles moderate traffic. Ships traveling between the northern rim and the central Akhan stop here for provisioning. The merchant fleet runs regular routes to Tzafan, to Baram, and occasionally to the central Akhan markets. Nothing about this is remarkable. It works.

## Cultural Character

Shavon's culture is the background culture of the inner Akhan — the register that the more distinctive polities are distinctive against. Hardworking, practical, community-oriented in the way of agricultural societies everywhere. The duchy has produced no great figures of historical note, no signature cultural exports, no philosophical movements. It has produced two centuries of adequate harvests, stable governance, and people who go about their lives without requiring the empire's attention.

The northern news arrives early — proximity to Tzafan means Shavon hears things before the central Akhan does. The duchy does not know what to do with the northern consolidation reports that have been filtering down for two years. The Grand Duke has forwarded them to the imperial court. Acknowledgment received.

**On the succession crisis:** Shavon wants it resolved. The trade disruptions have affected the northern supply routes the duchy depends on. The Grand Duke has received claimant delegations politely and said nothing committal. He is not playing a long game. He is waiting for someone to win so the shipping lanes stabilize.

**Species mix:** Humans majority, the standard inner Akhan mix of species without particular concentration in any direction. The duchy reflects the empire's diversity without amplifying any single thread of it.
