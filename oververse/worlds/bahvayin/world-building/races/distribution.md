# Species Distribution — Bahvayin

**Category:** Races & Species  
**Related:** [Genetic Frameworks](../../../../races/genetic-frameworks.md), [Races of Hypatia](../../../../races/README.md)

## The Experiment's Logic

Bahvayin was seeded with maximum species diversity deliberately. The FC running the experiment selected from the full genetic repository and placed species into ecological niches where their natural tendencies would create meaningful interactions — integration, segregation, or conflict — rather than simply coexisting without contact.

The distribution is not random. Species were placed where their traits would be tested by the environment and forced into relationship with species whose traits differ most productively. The salad bar was not dumped into the bowl. It was arranged.

**Genetic incompatibility:** Species cannot interbreed. The biological barrier between species is absolute — a dog cannot breed with a cat, and the same principle applies across all species on Bahvayin. No half-species, no hybrids. Cultural integration can be deep and genuine; biological boundaries do not move. This is not a social rule — it is a fact of how these species were seeded from the genetic repository.

**Cross-species marriage:** Legally valid, biologically sterile. Rare, socially viewed with disdain in most cultures — an instrument of political desperation rather than genuine union. Old houses with deep biological lines do not need this mechanism. Its use announces the limits of a house's options even while executing strategy. Such marriages can transfer legitimacy through designated cadet branches of either spouse's species, making them a tool for alliance-building across species lines when no other instrument serves. The imperial court has the social machinery to process them with appropriate ceremony and theological framing. The rim cultures find them bizarre.

## Inner Akhan — Imperial Heartland

The sheltered inner sea and its islands. The administrative, commercial, and diplomatic center of Bahvayin civilization.

**Humans** (*Homo sapiens*) — generalist majority across the inner islands and most of the rim. The empire's common population. Present everywhere, dominant nowhere in particular.

**Hobgoblins** (*Homo goblinus*) — the imperial bureaucracy. They process the succession claims, maintain the tax records, administer the legal system. They do not care who wins. They care that process is followed. At this moment they are the most functional organ of imperial governance and the most accurate documentary record of its collapse.

**Halflings** (*Homo halfinius*) — logistics and trade networks. The supply chains that connect the inner islands and the rim run through Halfling coordination. Too useful to any potential successor to disrupt. Currently maintaining function through political instability by the simple strategy of serving whoever is paying.

**Kitsune** (*Vulpes sapiens*) — merchants and information brokers. They know what the succession crisis means for commodity prices before the claimants have finished arguing. Commercial intelligence is their primary product. Morally gray; they sell to whoever can pay.

**Otter-folk** (*Lutrinae sapiens*) — port facilitators and fixers. Every port has situations requiring someone who can talk to multiple parties and make everyone feel they got something. Individual variation: some are genuine facilitators, some are light-fingered opportunists, most are both depending on the situation. Check your pockets. Usually fine.

**Elves** (*Homo sylvanius*) — imperial court, diplomacy, communications. Mercara's genetic contribution to the repo: PR-focused, warm, articulate. The court's Elf functionaries have been managing the succession crisis's communications dimension with diminishing returns.

## Storm Gate Coalition — Eastern Equatorial Rim

A multi-species functional coalition unified by shared engineering purpose at the world's most dangerous threshold.

**Dwarves** (*Homo thurinis*) — structural engineering backbone. The hydraulic calculations, the stone placement, the generational technical memory of what configurations work. The senior Dwarf engineers are the most practically powerful members of the Maintenance Hierarchy.

**Chelonari** (*Testudo chelonari*) — record-keepers and wisdom-holders. Patient enough to maintain eight centuries of tidal records without institutional decay. Some individual Chelonari may have three to four centuries of continuous personal memory — living primary sources for events that everyone else knows only from records.

**Sungrun** (*Lepus acutis*) — living early warning system. Their extraordinary sensory capability — pressure, vibration, subsonic precursors — detects what no instrument currently measures. A Sungrun behaving strangely means something is coming. The Hierarchy learned this through loss before they learned it through systematic observation.

**Corvani** (*Corvus morticus*) — pattern analysts. Hyper-literal, death-framed, extraordinary at finding signal in noise. The Corvani analyst currently working on the maintenance logs is doing what Corvani do: treating the data as a body and reading what damaged it.

**Humans and others** — the culture's general population: Gate pilots (typically human, hereditary), breakwater island residents, inner harbor merchants, support populations.

## Northern Rim — Cold Temperate to Subarctic

Hard country, resource rich, genuinely independent cultures that were never fully absorbed.

**Bruin** (*Ursus sapiens*) — rangers, marshals, independent law enforcement. Solitary, territorial, naturally suited to covering large territories alone. The northern rim's Bruin ranger networks are the closest thing to consistent authority structures many northern communities have.

**Kibo** (*Hippopotamus bellator*) — mercenaries and heavy capability. Enthusiastically pragmatic in direct conflict. Northern rim Kibo fighters are among the most capable on Bahvayin because the environment is trying to kill them constantly. Several succession claimants are sending them feelers.

**Llamari** (*Lama sapiens*) — transport and logistics in difficult terrain. High-altitude capable, vigilant, efficient. Every viable northern supply route runs through Llamari knowledge. Not currently for sale to succession claimants at any price.

**Quedari** (*Manis sapiens*) — simply there. Pangolin-derived, patient, minimal. Quedari communities have been in the same northern valleys since before records begin. They do not participate in imperial politics. They will survive whatever comes.

## Grenmarch / Pachi Trail / Agricultural Mainland

The interior mainland arcs and the Pachi Trail corridor. Ecological specialists and land managers.

**Tzvari** (*Cervus tzvari*) — ecological readers and land tenders. Present wherever the land is worked carefully — Grenmarch's interior forests, along the Pachi Trail's ecological transition zones, A'Kavah's caldera estates, Andalvar's highland valleys. The Chelonari own A'Kavah's estates and think in centuries; the Tzvari tend them and feel what the soil needs this season. That distinction has always mattered and nobody has examined it carefully.

Their ecological reading makes them the first to know when something is wrong with a landscape — drought stress before visible indicators, soil depletion before yield drops, ecosystem disruption before any instrument registers it. In Bahvayin this means some Tzvari have been feeling the consequences of the breakwater budget redirections in the coastal ecology for longer than any official report has acknowledged. Their findings are rarely solicited. Their findings are correct.

The Pachi Trail creates natural Tzvari-Pachi cooperation: Pachi carry knowledge of the trail through every biome; Tzvari carry knowledge of what each biome is currently doing. Together they produce a landscape assessment no imperial survey replicates.

## Desert Road / Western Equatorial Rim

Hyperarid desert corridor. The nomadic culture of the Desert Road passage.

**Djemari** (*Camelus sapiens*) — dominant nomadic culture. Water conservation, endurance, long-haul efficiency across brutal terrain. They know every water source, every shelter, every aquifer along the western rim. This knowledge is the Desert Road's fundamental resource and is held in human memory, not written records. Their operational importance is about to be discovered.

**Bandari** (*Procyon callidus*) — the informal economy layer. Salvage, frontier trade, last-mile solutions. The Djemari move through the desert; the Bandari find things worth moving. Productive working relationship; mutual wariness about price and leverage.

**Nekari** (*Felis praecisus*) — solitary desert hunters. Sparse population, specific territorial ranges, very dangerous in their territory. The Djemari give Nekari ranges professional respect. Occasionally trade; do not integrate.

**Geliss** (*Gekko versalis*) — linguistic and cultural liaison layer along the winding passage. Cross-context fluency — cultural, linguistic, analytical. The Desert Road passes through multiple distinct cultural zones; Geliss are the people who can operate across all of them without appearing to be from any of them.

## Southern Way / Fog Belt

Warm current influence, mixed port culture, and the fog belt navigation specialists.

**Aubrei** (*Testudo aubreii*) — fog belt navigation specialists. Naval Testudo — aggressive cousins of the patient Chelonari. They have mapped the fog belt through methods not transferable to outsiders and have no intention of making those methods transferable. Their patrol behavior along the southern outer rim has changed in the last year; they have not explained why.

**Pachi** (*Elephas sapiens*) — long-haul philosophical traders. The warm southern islands suit their temperament. Pachi communities on the southern inner islands are among the most established in the world — multiple generations, deep commercial integration, genuinely useful as neutral parties in disputes.

**Iridari** (*Ratufa iridescens*) — color-changing emotional tails; port broadcasters; living news network. Cannot conceal emotional states; constitutionally suited to information sharing. The informal network of Iridari broadcasters across the Southern Way's ports transmits the emotional temperature of every community they pass through. The broadcasts have been shifting in color for a year.

**Gnolls** (*Canis jocularis/festivus*) — thriving in mixed port chaos. They are not causing the chaos; they are the culture that has figured out how to be warm, useful, and professionally effective in environments where everyone else is slightly off-balance. Theatrical, communal, warm until crossed.

## Threshold Spaces — All Three Straits

**Margari** (*Fae threshold*) — liminal fae; their presence at significant thresholds is essentially inevitable. Guide or trickster depending on context and what the narrative needs them to be. FC cannot trace their genetic repo ancestry. The engineer-priests do not fully understand them and will not admit it.

## Absent or Rare — Notes

**Draconis sapiens** — no specific reason to be present; not placed.

**Greater Dragons** — possibly present in deep history; not in the current era.

**Homo bovinus** — present in the engineer-priest coalition at the Storm Gate. Their tremorsense, applied to structural assessment of the breakwater system, was the practical foundation on which the engineer-priest institution was built. See [Engineer-Priests](../organizations/engineer-priests.md).

**Fae Ilvari and Lumari** — possible but unplaced; available when a story needs them.

**Orcus genus** — present in ruins and sealed dungeons as mageocracy remnants. Not a surface-world population. See [Ruins & Dungeons](../natural-world/ruins-dungeons.md).

**Kethi** — possible at the Storm Gate given their quantum-sensitive horns and transit specialist capabilities, but not established; could be placed if a story requires it.

**Felari** — possible in port culture and imperial court (social grace, presentation); not specifically placed but consistent with the world.

**Corvani (as population rather than specialist)** — present broadly; the Storm Gate has a notable Corvani population but they exist across the world wherever pattern analysis and record-keeping are valued.
