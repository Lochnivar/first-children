# The Storm Cycle

**Category:** Natural World  
**Related:** [Storm Gate (geography)](../geography/storm-gate.md), [Storm Gate Theology](../cosmology/storm-gate-theology.md), [Engineer-Priests](../organizations/engineer-priests.md)

## Overview

The storm cycle of Bahvayin is driven by the atoll's atmospheric geometry. The desert western rim generates thermal conditions that seed weather systems in the outer ocean. Those systems cross the full outer ocean fetch before reaching the eastern equatorial rim — organized, large, and powerful by landfall.

This is not magic. It is geography.

---

## The Gate Surge

The ordinary annual phenomenon. During storm season, organized weather systems from the outer ocean make landfall at the eastern rim. The Storm Gate's straight geometry means surge energy enters the inner Akhan directly rather than dissipating around curves.

**Character:** Predictable within the range of the engineer-priests' eight-century record. Dangerous for the unprepared. Fatal for the foolish. Managed by functional breakwaters.

**Severity scale:** The engineer-priests maintain a surge classification system based on tidal records. Common folk use a simpler two-state system: manageable and not manageable. The engineer-priests use approximately seven states. The distinction between the middle states is the difference between "the breakwaters take strain" and "we lose three breakwater sections."

**Storm season timing:** Predictable by calendar and confirmed by the Sungrun early warning. The beginning and end of storm season are the Storm Gate culture's primary calendar events.

---

## The Eye of Tsapadon

The mega-storm cycle. All meteorological and astronomical conditions aligning simultaneously — a convergence that the engineer-priests' records show occurs on a long cycle, not a regular annual event.

**What makes it different from the Gate Surge:**
- Scale: the outer ocean fetch is fully organized; systems arrive that have spent their entire development over open water
- Duration: the Eye surge is not a single event but a multi-day catastrophic weather sequence
- Surge magnitude: without functional breakwaters, a wall of water with an entire outer ocean behind it enters the Akhan Sea
- Inner Akhan effects: inner sea flooding, storm damage, maritime catastrophe extending far from the Gate

**Predictability:** The cycle is mathematically legible from the tidal records if you have eight centuries of data and know what you are looking for. The engineer-priests can predict the Eye's approach. They cannot prevent it. They can predict the specific tidal and astronomical conditions that indicate the Eye's formation is underway. Those conditions are currently present.

**Theological framing:** The Eye is Tsapadon looking directly at Bahvayin. Not punishment — attention. Periodic, unavoidable, scheduled. The engineering-priest culture does not hide from the Eye; they prepare the offering that says *we have done our work; consider whether we have done it well enough.*

## Current Situation — Eye Watch

The engineer-priests declared the Eye Watch two years ago.

**What triggered the declaration:**
- Tidal records showing cycle alignment approaching
- Astronomical observations consistent with the Eye formation window
- Sungrun behavioral changes detected three weeks before the instrument readings confirmed the pattern

**What has happened since:**
- Breakwater maintenance budget redirected at imperial direction
- Supply ships arriving late — three consecutive years
- At least one senior engineer-priest has had an accident
- The Corvani analyst's work on maintenance logs is approaching the point of legally documentable evidence of deliberate compromise

**What the Eye Watch means operationally:**
- All non-essential activities suspended
- Full maintenance priority on the breakwater chain
- Continuous Sungrun monitoring
- Senior Council in continuous session
- Political engagements with succession claimants being managed by specific designated council members so the majority can focus on engineering

**The fundamental problem:** The breakwaters are compromised. The Eye is approaching. The emperor, whose divine mandate is specifically to prevent this situation, has created this situation. The three succession claimants offering favorable terms each want to use the engineer-priests' political endorsement. Full breakwater restoration would require imperial resources that the emperor is not providing and that no succession claimant can provide quickly enough.

## The Fog Belt Connection

The Southern Way's warm current creates fog belt conditions on the southern outer rim. The fog belt has its own storm behavior that differs from the eastern rim's:

- Less organized systems — the southern outer rim does not get the full outer ocean fetch that the eastern rim gets
- Persistent low visibility — the fog itself is a hazard distinct from storm events
- The Aubrei navigate it; everyone else does not

The Eye's approach affects the Southern Way as well — the mega-storm sequence is not contained to the eastern rim. The southern and northern rim cultures will feel the Eye's approach in their own weather. The inner Akhan will feel it in storm surge from the Storm Gate direction and in unusual sea conditions from the Southern Way direction simultaneously.
