# Mortal Experience: Hegemon

**Status:** World-Specific Expansion  
**Category:** Cultures / Mortal Awareness  
**Related:** [First Children](../../cosmology/first-children.md), [Races](../../races/README.md), [../README.md](README.md)

## Overview

Mortals on Hegemon live real lives with real stakes while their destinies are shaped by godly manipulators playing geopolitical chess. They have varying levels of awareness and varied responses to their situation.

## Cosmological Awareness

### Open Knowledge

**What Mortals Know:**
- Mortals know about The Strategists (the FC players)
- Understand world is a contest among gods
- Varying cultural responses to this knowledge

**Framework Alignment:**
- Open cosmology variant (framework allows)
- Mortals aware of First Children (framework variant)
- Different from closed cosmology (like Iolinus)

### Cultural Reactions

**Faithful:**
- Worship patron First Child, strive to please them
- See participation as divine purpose
- Actively seek to serve their patron

**Resentful:**
- View themselves as pawns, resist manipulation
- See First Children as manipulative siblings
- May form resistance movements

**Pragmatic:**
- Accept reality, work within system for advantage
- Navigate the game for mortal benefit
- Play First Children against each other

**Rebellious:**
- Underground movements seeking to break free
- May attract external First Child attention (liberation potential)
- Could deliberately destabilize world to force reset/intervention

**Opportunistic:**
- Play First Children against each other for mortal benefit
- Seek advantage through manipulation
- Exploit the competition

**Framework Alignment:**
- Varied responses exist (framework allows)
- Mortals have agency (framework allows)
- Resistance movements possible (framework allows)

## Social Structure

### Quantum-Gifted Elite

**Champions and Nobility:**
- Mortals with First Child-granted fragments become nobility/champions
- Often persecuted by rival factions
- May defect between First Child patrons
- Rare "wild" quantum manifestations outside First Child control

**Framework Alignment:**
- Mortals with quantum fragments exist (framework rule)
- Fragments granted by First Children (framework capability)
- Wild manifestations possible (framework allows)

### Common Mortals

**Regular Lives:**
- Live regular lives while empires rise and fall around them
- Caught in wars they didn't choose
- Some thrive through adaptation, others suffer
- Real consequences - death, displacement, cultural erasure

**Framework Alignment:**
- Mortals have real lives (framework truth)
- Real consequences exist (framework truth)
- Agency varies by individual (framework allows)

### Resistance Movements

**Underground Networks:**
- Groups seeking to end the game or achieve stalemate
- May attract external First Child attention (liberation potential)
- Could deliberately destabilize world to force reset/intervention
- Underground networks sharing knowledge of First Child manipulation tactics

**Framework Alignment:**
- Resistance movements exist (framework allows)
