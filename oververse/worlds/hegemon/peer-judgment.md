## Overview

Other First Children observe Hegemon with mixed reactions, debating whether the "game" violates moralistic rules about mortal treatment. This demonstrates framework concepts of peer judgment and potential liberation operations.

## The Watchers

### Other First Children

**Observers:**
- First Children not involved in Hegemon's creation observe from afar
- They debate whether this violates moralistic rules
- Mixed reactions to the ongoing competition

**Perspectives:**
- **Some see it as educational/balanced experiment**
  - Structured challenge for mortals
  - Tests strategies and influence methods
  - Controlled environment for growth

- **Others view it as problematic**
  - Immature sibling squabbling
  - Potential abuse of mortals
  - Using civilizations as game pieces

**Framework Alignment:**
- Peer judgment determines legality (framework rule)
- First Children debate moralistic rules (framework pattern)
- Varied perspectives exist (framework allows)
