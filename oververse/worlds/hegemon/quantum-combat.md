# Quantum Combat & Warfare: Hegemon

**Status:** World-Specific Expansion  
**Category:** World Mechanics / Combat  
**Related:** [Quantumancy](../../magic-systems/quantumancy.md), [Coherent Patterns](../../metaphysics/coherent-patterns.md), [../README.md](README.md)

## Overview

Combat on Hegemon operates at both mortal and quantum levels, with First Children influencing outcomes through subtle manipulation while mortals fight real wars with real consequences.

## Battle Mechanics

### Mortal Perspective

**Standard Warfare:**
- Standard warfare with medieval-renaissance tech levels
- "Magical" effects from quantum fragment users
- Unpredictable outcomes despite superior forces
- Terrain advantages from quantum stability/instability

**Framework Alignment:**
- Mortals fight real wars (framework truth)
- Quantum fragments provide abilities (framework rule)
- Terrain advantages from quantum patterns (framework-aligned)

### Quantum Layer

**Energy Pattern Disruptions:**
- Battles create energy pattern disruptions
- Fallen armies leave coherent echoes (ghost soldiers haunting borders)
- Massive conflicts can spawn rift phenomena
- Victory/defeat influenced by quantum probability collapse

**Framework Alignment:**
- Combat creates quantum disruptions (framework-aligned)
- Ghosts are coherent energy patterns (framework truth)
- Rifts are quantum instabilities (framework concept)
- Probability manipulation is quantum (framework principle)

### First Children Interference

**Subtle Influence:**
- Subtle probability nudging during key moments
- Cannot directly intervene without violating physical laws
- Work through champions, weather, terrain
- Risk of peer judgment if too obvious

**Framework Alignment:**
- First Children can influence but not directly control (framework allows)
- Physical laws cannot be violated (framework constant)
- Peer judgment applies (framework rule)

## Strategic Resources

### Quantum Artifacts

**Stable Anchors:**
- Stable anchors providing territorial bonuses
- Captured from conquered regions
- Grant enhanced abilities to holders
- Become legendary items in mortal culture
- Can shift game balance significantly

**Framework Alignment:**
- Anchors are stable constructs (framework concept)
- Artifacts are anchored patterns (framework truth)
- Can grant abilities (framework function)

### Anchor Networks

**Strategic Infrastructure:**
- Control of anchor points enables greater First Children influence
- Destroying enemy anchors weakens their position
- Building new anchors extends control
- Strategic infrastructure as important as armies

**Framework Alignment:**
- Anchors enable influence (framework function)
- Anchor networks extend control (framework capability)
- Strategic importance (framework-aligned)

## Framework Conformance

**Conforms to Framework:**
- All combat operates through quantum manipulation
- Physical laws cannot be violated
- First Children work through influence
- Real consequences exist
- Quantum uncertainty provides limits

**World-Specific Expansions:**
- Strategic combat mechanics
- Quantum artifact system
- Anchor network importance
- Ghost soldiers from fallen armies
