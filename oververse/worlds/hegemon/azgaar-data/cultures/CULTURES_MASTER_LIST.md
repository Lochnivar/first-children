# Hegemon Cultures Master List

**Status:** Reference Document  
**Era:** Hacutonce Era (374 HE)  
**Last Updated:** December 2024  
**Total Cultures:** 13 (11 subcultures + 1 fusion + 1 special)

## Overview

This master list provides a comprehensive reference for all cultures in the Hegemon world, organized by their major cultural templates. All cultures have been given unique Hegemon-world names while maintaining their foundational Earth-based templates as conceptual handles.

## Framework Context

These cultures represent mortal civilizations within the Hegemon world, where First Children (The Strategists) manipulate and influence civilizations as part of their strategic game. The cultural templates serve as organizational handles to conceptualize the diverse mortal populations, while the unique Hegemon-world names give each culture its distinct identity.

**Key Framework Points:**
- Cultures are mortal constructs within the quantum foundation
- Cultural identity shapes how mortals interpret First Children influence
- Expansionism values reflect cultural tendencies toward territorial growth
- Cultural blending (fusions) demonstrates complex interactions between major templates
- Special cultures represent unique cases outside standard categorization
