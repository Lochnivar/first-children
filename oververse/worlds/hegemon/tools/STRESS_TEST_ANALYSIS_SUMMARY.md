## 7. Recommendations

### For Production Use

1. **Monitor Inbreeding Levels**
   - Similarity >60% = warning
   - Similarity >75% = critical (introduce outcrossing)

2. **Introduce Outcrossing**
   - When instability exceeds 50%
   - When rare traits are lost
   - Every 3-5 generations for small populations

3. **Maintain Larger Populations**
   - 100+ individuals to preserve rare traits
   - Prevents rapid allele loss
   - Reduces inbreeding pressure

4. **Track Recessive Traits Carefully**
   - Monitor allele frequencies, not just phenotypes
   - Introduce new bloodlines when traits become rare
   - Use selective breeding to maintain desired traits
