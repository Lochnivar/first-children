# Chance Hodgkins

**Status:** Antagonist (Major)
**Role:** Ancient Quantumancer; disembodied soul; wants to complete the Shattering; potential hijacker of Greyroses
**First Appearance:** [TBD - likely in later books]

## Basic Information
- **Full Name:** Chance Hodgkins
- **Age:** ~10,000+ years (immortal since the Fall, disembodied since Shattering)
- **Gender/Pronouns:** Male
- **Occupation/Title:** Former Thohalla fisherman; Quantumancer; Disembodied antagonist
- **Physical Description:** Currently disembodied (soul only, no body). Formerly Thohalla fisherman.

## Personality
Mad, driven by nihilistic desire for complete oblivion. Suffers from eternal quantum haze (unfiltered perception of all possible realities). Wants to complete what he started: the destruction of reality itself. Tragic figure whose devotion to ending his suffering is cosmically unrequited.

## Background

### Early Life
- Thohalla fisherman
- Met Rhyannin (love of his life)
- Best seasons of his life
- Storm killed her (Sea of Storms)
- Inconsolable grief

### Quantumancy Discovery
- Blinding pain in brain
- Double vision (ghost images of possibilities)
- Saw potential futures overlapping
- Learned to collapse preferred outcomes
- Practiced alone for years

### The Fall (500 years after Rhyannin's death)
- Hiking in mountains
- Fell ~200 feet, spine snapped
- Should have died
- Used quantumancy to repair spine
- Discovered he could stop aging
- Became immortal

### Alyzandra (The Anchor)
- Reader (saw current Prime, grounded him)
- His anchor to reality
- Argued with her constantly (even after death)
- "Alyz says I'm being rude" (imagined her voice keeping him sane)
- Lost her = lost connection to reality = madness

### The Crossing Disaster
- Witnessed Thohalla desert crossing
- Over 50% died (no water preparation)
- Zeta turned tragedy into religious ritual
- Week-long mortification, ends with salt water
- Modern Thohalla still practice (moderated version)
- Blame victims: "those who drank from sea when faith flagged"
- Theological abuse commemorated

### The Shattering Attempt
- Nala manipulated him and other Quantumancers
- Tried to destroy Godstone (believed it would evaporate the universe, ending his eternal suffering)
- Succeeded in shattering it (not destroying)
- His body destroyed in attempt
- Soul survived (quantum nature)
- Still exists as disembodied Quantumancer

## Current Threat
- **Mad:** Wants to complete the Shattering
- **Can hijack Greyroses:** Quantumancer ability allows him to potentially possess bodies through Greyrose connection
- **Needs body:** To finish quantum work and complete the Shattering
- **Major antagonist:** For later books
- **Only "free" Quantumancer:** The only one not hiding in terror

## The Quantum Haze
- Unfiltered perception of all possible realities simultaneously
- Maddening, inescapable torture
- Even after death, his soul still perceives the quantum haze
- No escape, rest, or peace
- Drives him to seek complete oblivion

## "By Chance" Document
- His confession/justification
- Written before Shattering attempt
- Thousands of years before Gabby's era
- Found later, read in present
- Title = wordplay (by Chance + by chance/probability)
- Modern language justified: Malachor's translation gift + addressing future readers

## Skills & Abilities
- **Quantumancer:** Ancient, powerful - can collapse probabilities into reality
- **Immortal:** Stopped aging through quantum manipulation
- **Disembodied:** Exists as soul only after body destroyed in Shattering
- **Greyrose Hijacking:** Can potentially possess bodies through Greyrose connection
- **Reality Manipulation:** Peak Quantumancer power (shattered mountains, created deserts)

## Relationships
- **Rhyannin:** Love of his life, died in storm (Sea of Storms). Inconsolable grief led to his power awakening.
- **Alyzandra:** Reader who was his anchor to reality. Argued constantly. Her death/loss led to his complete madness. Still imagines her voice.
- **Nala:** Manipulated him and other Quantumancers to shatter the Godstone. Used his suffering and nihilistic desires.
- **Other Quantumancers:** Worked with ancient Quantumancers to attempt the Shattering.
