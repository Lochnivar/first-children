## Background
Second son of House Lochnivar, a Maneth noble house. Connected to the priesthood of the Church of Malachor and Seeker tradition. Charged with retrieving a Godshard to secure his father's kingship.

## Relationships
- **House Lochnivar:** Political weight within Maneth; values ritual legitimacy (Godshards) and moral clarity; enforces a strict code of duty and public honor.
