## Personality
Field medic with two distinct modes:
- **Default:** Mouse-quiet, eyes down, barely audible
- **Crisis:** Training slams in like a portcullis; voice flat and absolute, orders no one ignores

After crisis mode, she's mortified, apologizes for "bossing everyone around," and scrubs blood from under her nails while hating herself for sounding like she belonged in charge.

## Background
Field medic specializing in ethno-medical practices. Has two distinct behavioral modes that she remembers in both states (NOT split personality - that was rejected). Struggles to integrate her emergency confidence into her normal personality.

## Character Arc

### The Rejected Split Personality
- **Considered & Rejected:** Split personality where she can't remember emergency mode
- **Why Rejected:** Would be medical condition, not character growth. Can't integrate what she doesn't remember. Gabby couldn't help her. Swat sequence payoff impossible. Clinical complexity, no narrative gain. CONTRIVANCE - rightly rejected.

### Actual Arc
- **Beginning:** Mouse normally, decisive in emergencies, REMEMBERS both states (key difference)
- **Development:** Struggles to accept emergency-self. Apologizes for being "too much." Gabby helps her integrate.
- **End:** Learns to access confidence outside emergencies. Growth: comfortable being assertive when needed.

## Relationships
- **Gabby D'Olivier:** Fierce loyalty (Gabby protects instinctively). Mentorship (Gabby helps Hara find confidence). Growth partnership (both learning). American directness helps Hara. Gabby drowns the guilt in hugs; cuts Hara's post-crisis apologies off with hugs or terrible jokes.
- **Elsbeth Thane:** Quiet scaffolding. Elsbeth clears space so Hara can flip into command mode and takes the blame afterward so the guilt has somewhere else to land. Elsbeth quietly takes the blame so Hara doesn't have to carry the guilt alone.
- **Calder Voss:** Initially, Calder goes off on her (too harsh, typical veteran behavior). Through the swat sequence, Calder learns to modulate his behavior. Hara eventually stands firm (grown confident).

## Motivations
Help people as a medic. Learn to integrate her emergency confidence into her normal personality. Stop apologizing for being competent. Accept that she can be assertive when needed.

## Secrets & Hidden Aspects
Her two modes are NOT a split personality - she remembers both states. This is key to her character growth. She can integrate the confidence, unlike a true split personality disorder.

## The Swat Sequence

### First Swat
- Calder going off on Hara (too harsh)
- Hara melting (mouse personality, can't defend herself)
- Gabby stands up, swats Calder (protects Hara)
- Shows Hara's vulnerability in default mode

### Second Swat
- Calder does it again to Hara
- Elsbeth swats Calder (authority enforcement)
- Calder learns, apologizes to Hara
- Shows Hara's growth beginning

### Third Swat (Resolution)
- Weeks later
- Hara now stands firm (grown confident)
- Calder performs exaggerated caricature (self-aware, playful)
- Gabby and Elsbeth simultaneous swat (playful)
- Everyone laughs
- Shows Hara's completed growth arc
