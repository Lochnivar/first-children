## Broader Palette — Non-Middle-Eastern Cultures at ~0 AD

The Middle East was the starting point but not the only option. Other major cultures active around 0 AD:

### Africa
- **Ancient Egypt** — one of the most distinctive ancient cultures; monumental architecture; death/afterlife obsession; complex bureaucratic priesthood; not yet mapped to anyone
- **Kingdom of Kush/Nubia** — powerful African kingdom south of Egypt; distinct aesthetic; iron-working; often overlooked
- **Axum (Ethiopia)** — sophisticated trade empire; obelisk architecture; early Christianity adjacent; Red Sea maritime power

### Asia
- **Mauryan/early Gupta India** — vast empire; Buddhism emerging; philosophical sophistication; caste structure; elephant warfare
- **Parthian Empire (Iran)** — Iranian successor to Persia; mounted archers; sophisticated Silk Road trade culture; resistant to Rome
- **Han Dynasty China** — already in Takoma surface reference; bureaucratic examination culture; silk trade; Confucian social order
- **Yayoi Japan** — already in Astelli surface reference; rice agriculture; bronze/iron introduction; ancestor veneration

### Europe
- **Celtic Gaul/Britain/Ireland** — already in Caelori; druids, oral tradition, head-hunting, warrior aristocracy
- **Germanic tribes** — decentralized warrior culture; thing (assembly) democracy; deep forest mythology
- **Scythians** — nomadic steppe warriors; extraordinary horse culture; fierce independence; gold artistry; no cities by choice

### Americas
- **Early Maya** — sophisticated mathematics and calendar; jungle city-states; sacrifice culture; writing system; astronomical obsession
- **Teotihuacan** — massive planned city; mysterious culture; Avenue of the Dead; significant trade network
- **Hopewell culture (North America)** — mound builders; vast trade networks across the continent; ritual burial culture

### Oceania
- **Polynesian** — already in Orindi; navigation by stars and waves; oral tradition; tattoo culture
- **Melanesian** — distinct from Polynesian; cargo cult potential; island chain culture

### Central Asia
- **Scythians/Sarmatians** — nomadic horse warriors of the steppe; some matrilineal; possibly origin of Amazon myths
- **Kushans** — Central Asian empire bridging China, India, Persia; Buddhist; extraordinary syncretism

## Gaps Worth Filling

Looking at the current 11 cultures, some Earth archetypes with no clear Iolanu equivalent:

- **No steppe nomad culture** (Scythian/Mongol type) — distinct from Thohalla caravans; pure horse culture
- **No monumental death culture** (Egyptian type) — afterlife obsession, massive funerary architecture
- **No jungle/tropical culture** (Maya/Southeast Asian type) — all current cultures are temperate or desert
- **No pure maritime island culture** (Polynesian beyond Orindi) — island chain people, not desert navigators
