# Rathen

**Earth Reference:** Scandinavian–Incan fusion (terrace craft, altitude life)  
**What “Rathen” is:** A **legal and social category** for the **quantum-adjacent gifted** and those **exiled or fled with them** to the floating peaks — **not** a single ethnic people. Peak society is **mongrel** (Thohalla-heavy lines are common; so are Astelli, Maneth, Caelori, Kitzengai, and every other mainland group that produced a manifesting child or a family racing ahead of the hunt). Outsiders say “Rathen culture”; from inside it reads as **survival under containment**. Interface politics and Orindi gatekeeping: [Orindi.md](Orindi.md). Region file: [rathen_mountains.md](../../locations/regions/rathen_mountains.md).

## The Rathen Oath

Inclusion and collective shelter on the peaks — **where the Orindibar moat still holds** — hinge on **the Rathen Oath**: to **contain** the **gift**, **its sanctioned teaching**, and **uncontrolled effects** **to the Rathen Peaks** as far as honor and force allow, and **not** to **allow or encourage** its **spread beyond** into the wider world. The Oath **buys** breathing room **inside the cage**; it is **not** Church **pardon**. Caught on the mainland or **extracted**, you **still burn**.

Many Rathen experience the Oath as **trading one Church for another**: orthodoxy, confession-of-danger, neighborly scrutiny of **lesson plans** — but **so long** as containment holds, peak law **mostly** leaves you to work and love without inquisitorial fire. **Peace lasts until cracking or oath breach.** Breach **need not** be willful — **exported** miracle, reckless teaching, or **accidental** spill can trigger the **same machinery**; **mercy and intent-weighing vary by peak**.

## Long Walk and terrace execution

When a mind **breaks containment** or **rejects** the compact, enforcement is **of a piece with the Oath**. Someone **still lucid enough** to own the sentence is expected to take the **Long Walk** — **wakeful**, **voluntary** capstone: escorts may **steady**, **rope**, or **contain** for **others’** safety, but **sedating** someone **unconscious** for the drop is **not** the norm. **If** they **cannot** take the oath seriously anymore, or **refuse** the Walk → **summary execution on the terrace** (blade, rope, etc.); **remains stay on the peak** under ordinary death custom — **deliberate floor-cast** is **Walk-norm**, not routine burial.

**Outcome:** the walker **dies**. **Almost always** the body **hits black sand**; **to date** **no cracked** survivor of the Walk sits in the record — QM has **not**, in known practice, **arrested** the fall. **Virga** under the peaks is **hydrology** (why peak rain rarely wets the desert), **not** the in-world **cover story** for Walk forensics.

**Natural death** — age, sickness, murder **not** sentenced as Walk — stays **on-peak** (interment, pyre, ice-chamber, terrace shrines, etc.), **not** a default **cast** to the sand.

## Teaching and “forbidden air”

**Sanctioned** QM **curriculum** implements the Oath. **Teaching forbidden-air** paths — **levitation**, **self-flight**, **QM arrest of terminal fall** — is **curriculum heresy**: skills that **ease escape** or **export miracle**. Open guild and terrace schools **do not** tolerate it (**silencing**, **expulsion**, **Walk** for worst teachers, **peak-local**). **Black-market tutors** exist but stay **very low profile** — informants, elders, and peers make open heresy **dangerous**. **Leaks** still **happen** (rogues, foreign contact, cracked insight), which is why Walk outcomes stay **lethal** without needing **unconscious** walkers.

QM **perceptual haze** and cracking connect to the same family of failure that broke **ancient Quantumancers**; see [quantum_abilities.md](../magic-systems/quantum_abilities.md).

## Daily life (spine)

- **Terraces and graft-husbandry:** Meltwater caught in basins and crevices, engineered **distribution** — the “graft-keeper” role is **life-support**, not only ornament.
- **Fragmentation:** Major massifs default to **separate** communities — water, elders, grudges — linked **sometimes** by **vines**, cableways, or **seasonal** daring transit; **no** assumption of one sky-kingdom.
- **Lineage:** The **quantum-adjacent gift** first rose in **ancient Thohalla** and has **since mixed into every culture** by inheritance; peak populations **reflect** that spread, not one mother culture. See [Thohalla.md](Thohalla.md).

## Physical Characteristics

Base appearance comes from **parent culture** — an Astelli exile looks Astelli; a Thohalla exile looks Thohalla. What the peaks add over time:

- **Altitude pallor** over any base complexion
- **Compact, dense-boned frames and powerful legs** from terrace work — the physical signature of a life spent on steep stone
- **Keen depth perception**, trained by the exposure of altitude and the distances of terrace labor
- **Hair** typically plaited or tightly bound — altitude wind and graft-work make loose hair impractical, regardless of origin

For inherited features (coloring, facial structure, baseline build), see the relevant parent culture file.

## Clothing

Base layering and textile tradition comes from **parent culture**, adapted for cold and altitude. What the peaks impose on top:

- **Compression harnesses** — standard across all peaks, origin-agnostic; terrace work and the proximity of long drops makes them load-bearing safety equipment
- **Ceramic damping plates** at joints and pressure points — the distinctly Rathen addition, Oath-logic made textile: suppression of uncontrolled flux expression during physical stress
- **Pale-lens visors** for altitude glare — practical, not cultural
- **Boiled wool and felt overlayers** where local textile tradition doesn't already supply equivalent cold-weather gear

The result is whatever someone's home culture wore, restructured for altitude and containment.
