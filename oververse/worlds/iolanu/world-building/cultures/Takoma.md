# Takoma

**Earth Reference:** Imperial China bureaucracy + Roman civic engineering

## Physical Characteristics
- **Skin:** Warm olive to light brown
- **Hair:** Black to dark brown, often bound in utilitarian styles
- **Eyes:** Dark brown to deep hazel
- **Build:** Broad-shouldered officials and lithe clerks with visible outdoor calluses among engineers

## General Clothing Notes
Structured embroidered robes and tunics with standardized insignia for bureaucrats; magistrates wear colonnade-ready cloaks and sandals; engineers and laborers use reinforced tunics and leather gaiters.

## Cultural Elements
- **Bureaucratic structure:** Imperial Chinese-style administration
- **Civic engineering:** Roman-inspired public works and infrastructure
- **Standardized systems:** Emphasis on order, hierarchy, and public service
