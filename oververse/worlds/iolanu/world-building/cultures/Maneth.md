# Maneth

**Earth Reference:** Victorian Gothic London + Norse Scandinavia

## Physical Characteristics
- **Skin:** Pale to fair
- **Hair:** Ash blond to dark brown
- **Eyes:** Steel gray, pale blue, or muted green
- **Build:** Varied builds from wiry seafarers to stiff, upright magistrates; weathered hands and salt- or soot-stained features common

## General Clothing Notes
High collars, layered wool cloaks, leather and fur trims; brooches with runic or guild motifs; practical boots and heavy gloves for dock and workshop work.

## Cultural Elements
- **House Lochnivar:** Noble house with political weight; values ritual legitimacy (Godshards) and moral clarity; enforces a strict code of duty and public honor
- **Seeker tradition:** Role tied to Godshard-hunting and interaction with fire-glyph magics
- **Godshards:** Divine fragments that act as sources of legitimacy, power, and political leverage

## Religious Organizations
- **Church of Malachor:** A religious organization that is separate from Maneth culture. The Church of Malachor and Maneth are independent entities. See [Church of Malachor Priesthood Hierarchy](../organizations/church-of-malachor/priesthood.md) and [Malachor and the Ebon Throne](../cosmology/malachor_faith.md) documents.
