# Balance and Imbalance

**Category:** Philosophy / Cosmology

## The Principle of Balance
So many things in the universe depend on some form of balance to maintain order:
- The balance of the fundamental forces of the universe that binds the stars together and lets them burn
- The social pressures of constituent minds that hold together a society
- Even a young woman hiking the Appalachian Trail in the White Mountains of New Hampshire

**Without balance, there would be chaos.**

## The Problem with Balance
Yet, balance encourages stagnation, the inability to change, to grow. Imbalance, an unpredicted, unforeseen outside force, forces change:
- From the void, our universe springs
- Revolution overthrows the cruel despot
- A panicked rabbit darts across the feet of a hiker at a particularly treacherous part of the trail

**Only when balance is disrupted does change occur.**

## Notes
This philosophical principle underlies the cosmic and narrative structure of Iolanu. Balance maintains order, but imbalance drives change and growth.
