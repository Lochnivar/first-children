# Chapterhouse of Nala in Ibelin

**Category:** Location / Organization  
**Related Characters:** Daochen (Mother Superior), Gabby D'Olivier, other Earth souls

## Overview
The Chapterhouse of Nala in Ibelin is a facility where Earth souls brought to Iolinus by Nala are housed and acclimated. It's managed by Daochen, the Mother Superior, who serves as liaison between Nala and the Earth souls.

## Location
- **City:** Ibelin
- **Setting:** Moderately busy town, warm golden sunlight, cloudless skies

## Purpose
- House and acclimate Earth souls to their new bodies and world
- Evaluate skills to determine what role Nala has in mind for each soul
- Support Earth souls adapting to their new life and mission
- Coordinate activities once souls are acclimated
- Provide transition period so Earth souls don't stand out in the field

## Facilities
- Individual rooms for Earth souls
- Full-length mirrors for exploring new bodies
- Writing desks
- Storage for possessions (tunics, leather armor, swords, etc.)
- Common areas

## Traditions
- **Donor Letters:** It is a tradition that a donor (the person who gave their body) writes a letter to the soul that will inhabit their body after the Ceremony. These letters can be unusual—Laila's letter was a single intricate glyph that allowed her to appear and speak to Gabby.

## Management
- **Mother Superior:** Daochen serves as the head of the order on Iolinus
- **Responsibilities:** Fulfilling Nala's plans, supporting Earth souls, coordinating activities, managing the Chapterhouse

## Notes
- Earth souls spend their first few weeks here getting used to new places and new concepts
- The Chapterhouse provides a safe space for Earth souls to explore their new bodies and adapt
- It's part of the quiet, hidden effort to build momentum beneath Zeta's notice
