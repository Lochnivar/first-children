# University of Ibelin (and the Chapterhouse within)

**Category:** Location / Organization  
**Related Characters:** Daochen (Mother Superior), Gabby D'Olivier, other Earth souls

## Overview

The **University of Ibelin** is a legitimate educational institution in the Wereshi city of Ibelin — founded by Camulos, Iolanu's first known Earth soul, who used his experience synthesizing Celtic druidic and Templar Knight institutional traditions to build something that would outlast him. From the outside it is exactly what it appears to be: a functioning university in a northern city, a place people travel to in order to learn things.

Embedded within it, silently, is **the Chapterhouse** — Nala's operational facility for receiving and acclimating Earth souls brought to Iolanu. The Chapterhouse has no public presence. It is a quiet portion of the university's physical structure, unremarkable from the outside, indistinguishable from administrative or residential university space. Daochen, the Mother Superior, manages it as a function of the university rather than a separate institution. The cover is not a thin one. The university is real; the Chapterhouse simply lives inside it.

---

## Location
- **City:** Ibelin (Wereshi hall-complex city-state, northern A'Maar, taiga-adjacent climate)
- **Host institution:** University of Ibelin (public-facing; legitimate)
- **The Chapterhouse:** Interior portion; no signage, no public role, no separate entry

---

## Purpose (Chapterhouse)

- Receive and house Earth souls brought to Iolanu by Nala
- Acclimate them to their new bodies and to Iolanu
- Evaluate skills and disposition; determine what role Nala has in mind
- Provide a transition period so Earth souls don't stand out in the field
- Coordinate activities once souls are sufficiently acclimated

---

## Facilities

- Individual rooms for Earth souls (within the university residential structure)
- Full-length mirrors for exploring new bodies
- Writing desks
- Storage for possessions (tunics, leather armor, swords, etc.)
- Access to university common areas — which provide natural cover for Earth souls moving around and learning the world

---

## Traditions

**Donor Letters:** The person who gave their body writes a letter to the soul that will inhabit it, delivered after the Ceremony. These letters take whatever form the donor chose — Laila's was a single intricate glyph that allowed her to appear and speak to Gabby directly.

---

## Management

**Mother Superior:** Daochen (from Xian, China; long-dead Earth soul; Christian who approves of Nala's mission without worshipping her; wears a simple silver crucifix). Serves as head of the order on Iolanu. Manages the Chapterhouse, receives Earth souls at their most disoriented, and serves as liaison between Nala's pre-arrival instructions and the agents now deployed in the field.

---

## Founding

Camulos — Celtic Druid turned Templar Knight, pulled from the Crusades; Iolanu's first known Earth soul — founded the University of Ibelin and embedded the Chapterhouse within it. He gave it the name he carried from his own world. Long dead by the time of the story. His institutional design has held for approximately 900 years.

---

## Notes

- The university provides natural cover: people arriving from elsewhere, learning unfamiliar things, asking basic questions about the world — this is what universities are for. Earth souls blend into that pattern.
- The Chapterhouse is part of the quiet, hidden effort to build momentum beneath Malachor's notice
- Nala's last point of live guidance to agents; once deployed from Ibelin, she cannot reach them directly
