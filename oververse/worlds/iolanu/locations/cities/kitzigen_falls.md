# Kitzigen Falls

**Type:** City / Natural Feature  
**Culture:** Kitzengai  
**Region:** TBD  

## Overview

Kitzigen Falls is both the name of the city and the name of the waterfall. The Kitzengai built in, on, and around the Veilfall Cascades — not beside them or below them, but inside them, treating the falls as structure, foundation, and the thing the city depends on to exist. The two cannot be separated in Kitzengai thinking, which is why they share one name.

The falls are home to the mistfae, soul couriers whose presence defines what Kitzigen Falls is beyond geography.

## The Veilfall Cascades

Seven tiered waterfalls forming the geographic and structural basis of the city. The name "Veilfall" refers to the mist environment the cascade produces — the falls generate atmosphere as much as physical structure, and that mist is the mistfae's native medium.

Soul couriers native to Kitzigen Falls. They live here; they are not summoned or trained. The falls are their origin.
