## Geographic Features
- **Strait of Atrando** - A strait (narrow waterway)
- **Reman Hill** - The hill that the city of Reman Hill was built on (the city and hill share the same name)
- **Orindibar Desert** - Magnetic desert with black sand, created by an ancient Quantumancer's experiment (see locations/orindibar_desert.md)
- **Rathen Mountains** - Floating mountain peaks above the Orindibar Desert, fragments of a shattered range (see locations/rathen_mountains.md)

## Cultural Connections
- **A'Thohalla** - Thohalla culture city. Mostly ruins, sparsely populated by a "skeleton crew" just enough to be a functional city. The rest of the Thohalla culture lives in Romani-inspired wagon caravans that circle up at night and are fortified. (Thohalla are nomadic, covenant-bound people)
- **Llosnagar** - Likely related to Llosca culture
- **Burmallosca** - Likely related to Llosca culture
- **Ibernon** - May be related to "Ibelin" where the Chapterhouse of Nala is located

## Previously Mentioned Locations
- **Ibelin** - Location of the Chapterhouse of Nala (where Earth souls are housed and acclimated)
