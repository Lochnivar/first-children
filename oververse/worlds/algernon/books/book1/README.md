# Book 1: Orcus Sapiens

**Status:** In Development  
**Primary POV:** Charlie (third-person limited)  
**Aside POVs:** Keyes (level-up voice; becomes more present as Charlie progresses)

## Overview

Book 1 is named for the moment everything turns on. Not the dungeon, not the ascension, not Keyes. The character generation screen reading Charlie's birth race. The thing he learned, rejected, and carried anyway into a dungeon that will try to burn everything away until only the waveform remains.

## Structure

**Opening:** Charlie arrives at character generation — loincloth, splitting headache, Pineal and Amygdala connecting for wetware interface. Screen reads birth race: Orcus Sapiens. He keeps human. Walks in as Charlie. Just Charlie.

**Progression:** Charlie pushes deeper than previous participants. Keyes's voice shifts from generic/procedural to present, invested, almost nervous. The conversation becomes real.

**Open Question:** If the phase transition completes and Charlie ascends — is the waveform on the other side still Charlie? Or did Charlie have to die to make it?

## Key Beats

- Character generation — Orcus Sapiens revelation; choice to stay human
- Early levels — conventional dungeon crawl; Keyes generic
- Mid levels — Charlie exceeds expectations; Keyes deviates from script
- The burn — identity (Charlie-ness) under increasing energetic load
- Climax — [To be defined]
