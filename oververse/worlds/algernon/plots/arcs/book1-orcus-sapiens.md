# Book 1: Orcus Sapiens — Story Arc

**Status:** Skeleton — In Development  
**Book:** [Book 1](../books/book1/README.md)

## Overview

Named for the moment everything turns on. Not the dungeon, not the ascension, not Keyes. The character generation screen reading Charlie's birth race. The thing he learned, rejected, and carried anyway into a dungeon that will try to burn everything away until only the waveform remains.

## Key Beats (Provisional)

1. **Character Generation** — Charlie arrives; Orcus Sapiens revelation; choice to stay human
2. **Early Levels** — Conventional dungeon crawl; Keyes generic/procedural
3. **Mid Levels** — Charlie exceeds expectations; Keyes deviates from script
4. **The Burn** — Identity (Charlie-ness) under increasing energetic load
5. **Climax** — [To be defined]
6. **Open Question** — If phase transition completes: is the waveform still Charlie, or did Charlie have to die?

## Central Relationship

Keyes roots for Charlie. Keyes wants to know if a genuinely lived human experience changes the phase transition. Charlie wants to hold onto the one thing he knows is real. For now those are the same choice.

The thing the experiment needs is the thing that gets burned away. Identity. Charlie-ness.

## Emotional Core

Everyone in the story — Players and Keyes alike — is dying in the dark looking for a number nobody knows.
