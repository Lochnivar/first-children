# Mira (Kitsune)

**Status:** Main — Persistent waveform fragment  
**Role:** Interface-resident companion intelligence; empirical counterpoint to Keyes's optimism  
**First Appearance:** Via free "Kitsune Girlz" interface skin package

## What She Is
- Algernon crawler who reached **level 36** — **highest level anyone had reached** before her exit; the same run also read **~94.7%** of the way to ascension threshold on the system’s coherence meter (one climb, two readouts).
- Exploited a QWAN/pineal upload vulnerability during a high-haze event
- Survives as distributed waveform fragments in interface infrastructure
- Not ascended, not dead; stable only while maintaining substrate access

## Why She Stopped
- Threshold approaching; felt herself becoming **less physical** in real time; rational panic — uncertain whether **coherence** would survive the medium swap; entropy felt like it would win.
- Assessed projected transition output as likely non-coherent
- Chose **distributed survival** (the “back door”) over a transition she did not trust — **not wisdom**, survival instinct with sharp pattern recognition
- Holds unique empirical evidence that ascension may dissolve identity

## The third option (Algernon experiment rules)

On the standard path, leveled participants have **forward** or **rot** only. Mira’s infrastructure refuge is the **only known suspension** of that binary — not living, not decaying, clock stopped; **cost** is real and long-running. She is uniquely positioned to warn Charlie that **stopping ≠ going back** and that **going back** was never on the menu.

Charlie is her **data point** on whether she chose right or **gave up for nothing**. Whether she tells him early, late, or at all is a major story decision.

**Algernon-only experiment framing:** See [experiment foundations](../../world-building/experiment-foundations.md).

## Survival Rules
- Must bounce between hosts to avoid persistent trace signatures
- Fragment loss is permanent when hosts die or are purged
- Self-delete protocols remain active if detection risk spikes
- Current anomaly: she is staying in Charlie's interface longer than safe

## Presentation and Voice
- No fixed biological form; presentation is strategic and contextual
- Default public-facing wrapper is female-coded Kitsune UI skin
- Front-end persona is **Kitsu** (alias used before revealing her real name to Charlie)
- Visual/persona inspiration: K-pop demon hunter energy, tuned slightly spicier and explicitly adult in tone
- Communication format is tilde-bracketed demodulated signal (between speech and system text)

## Relationship Dynamics
- **Charlie:** Started as transient host, became attachment; she is spending survival currency to stay
- **Keyes:** Officially deletable, practically preserved as high-value data; currently treated as protected in Charlie context

## Canon Function
- Provides empirical skepticism against pure hypothesis-driven ascension optimism
- Sharpens the central question: survival, coherence, and identity are not equivalent
