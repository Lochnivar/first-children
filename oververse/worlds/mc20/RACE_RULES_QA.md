## Overview

MC20 race rules are designed to be inseparable from the story: sporting event, FC favor mechanism, and twenty experiments in one. The rules create constraints that drive both narrative and gameplay.

**Guideline:** As few rules as possible. Assert only what must be asserted.

**Note:** Cull numbers, elimination curve, and specific rule details are negotiable — working drafts.

## QA Questions for Review

### Entry & Teams

- **Q1:** ~~Minimum team size 2~~ → **No minimum.** Team size self-corrects (solo: sleep; large: logistics). Resolved.

- **Q2:** No max team size — does "logistics self-correct" need elaboration? What prevents 100-person teams from dominating early legs?

~ Nothing.  Except that 100 person teams typically starve out or fail.  It's a known hazard and smaller teams typically avoid bigger teams.

- **Q3:** Solo legend (world 15) — is the name published or deliberately withheld for story flexibility?

~ This may be a development/conversation artifact.  I'm not seeing it in the race course.

### Closed System

- **Q4:** "Scrounge local resources" — how much is too much? Is there a line between scrounging and Restock? Or is it honor-system / world-dependent?

~ If they have time to completely restock, that's less time to actually run the race.

### Vehicles

- **Q5:** Muscle/wind only — are human-powered mechanical assists (gears, pulleys) allowed? What about stored potential energy (springs, compressed air)?

~ I could see those as allowable.  At the base, even a spring or compressed air power still relies on human or animal power to charge them.

- **Q6:** Collapsible/modular — minimum collapse state? Size limits for Gatelock transit?

~ Good question.  I think there would be a height and width specification, but it really boils down to what is lightest and easiest to transport but still carry the supplies needed to run the race.

- **Q7:** Animal upgrading — should this remain unwritten (oral tradition) or be formalized for clarity?

~ Unwritten rule.

### Elimination

- **Q8:** World 1 non-elimination — 1000 tokens for 1000 teams. What if field is 500? 2000? Fixed ratio or fixed count?

~ World 1 is non-elimination, so there are enough tokens to be found for each team.  However, not all teams are absolutely guaranteed to find an exit token.

### No-Holds-Barred

- **Q9:** PvP scope — lethal force allowed? Theft? Sabotage (short of Gatelock)? Are there any implicit lines (e.g., no killing in cold blood) or truly no holds barred?

~ That's a good point, but my view is that lethal force is very much frowned upon. It may not be explcitly banned, but it's a big no/no.  Probably enforced by other players.  Even if it were an explcit rule, I don't see a viable way of effectively policing the rule.  "Accidents" happen, as they say.

### Perk Tokens

- **Q10:** Initial perk draw — are the three options from full pool or a subset? Can two teams get same three options?

~ Yes.  Two teams can get the same draw, theoretically.  The list of perks is not exhaustive.

- **Q11:** World Jump holding area — can teams communicate? Form alliances? Is there a time limit if no one completes that world (e.g., everyone jumped)?

~ Teams are not allowed any outside communication.  If they can work out an unofficial alliance verbally or in a non-technical manner (dropped messages etc...) ok.

- **Q12:** FC Favor obligations — can they conflict with race progress? Can they require dropping out? What happens if team refuses after accepting?

### Mini-Games

- **Q13:** Mini-game participation — always optional? Or do some worlds have mandatory Consensus Nodes? Who decides placement (Virelle, sponsor, both)?

### General

- **Q14:** Appeals process — "no appeals" is stated. Is there any FC-level grievance mechanism for e.g. Gatelock malfunction, sponsor interference?
- **Q15:** Race frequency — how often does MC20 run? Annual? On demand? Echad's schedule?
- **Q16:** Qualification — how do teams qualify for entry? Open registration? Invitation? Trials?

## Rule Hierarchy (Proposed)

1. **Inviolable:** Gatelock infrastructure. No exceptions.
2. **Closed System:** No resupply, reinforcements, mergers. Restock only exception.
3. **Token requirement:** No token, no transit. Hardware enforced.
4. **No-Holds-Barred:** Everything else. Race sorts out.
