# Brenneth — Meta

## Purpose in Oververse

Brenneth is the primary development world for school-based (cognitive anchor) magic systems. Its purpose is to explore what mortal quantum access looks like when it is open, accepted, and institutionalized — the opposite of Iolanu's persecution model.

## Tone

Wheel of Time / Tolkien. Magic is real and present but not the aesthetic center. The world is a world of kingdoms, conflicts, and ordinary human stakes. Magic is one professional layer among many.

## Key Contrasts with Other Worlds

| Element | Iolanu | Brenneth |
|---------|--------|----------|
| Magic origin | Natural fragments (Malachor-imposed) | School frameworks (mortal-developed) |
| Magic status | Persecuted / hidden | Open / institutionalized |
| First Child | Malachor (known, worshipped as god) | Unknown |
| Fragment type | 5 specific fragments | 5 schools (culturally contingent) |
| Wild access | Extremely rare, catastrophic | Known, managed |
