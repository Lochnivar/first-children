Quick-reference context for AI. Read before working on Brenneth content.

---

## What Brenneth Is

Standard medieval sword-and-board fantasy world within Hypatia. Magic exists, is open and accepted, but is NOT the defining feature of the world. Magic users are professionals (like physicians or lawyers) — respected specialists, outnumbered by ordinary people.

**Tone:** Wheel of Time / Tolkien — not high-magic-as-aesthetic.

---

## The Single Most Important Magic Fact

Schools of magic are **mental frameworks**, not natural gifts. Practitioners learn to perceive a filtered slice of quantum reality. The framework IS the anchor. Without the framework, unfiltered quantum perception = madness (the haze).

This is a mortal discovery, not a First Child imposition. It evolved over centuries of accumulated practice.

---

## The Five Schools (Summary)

| School | Sees | Can Do | Cannot Do |
|--------|------|--------|-----------|
| **Transformation** | Matter states | Heal, transmute, alter material | Read intent, see probability |
| **Force** | Energy in motion | Move objects, project force, shields | Sense nature of what it moves |
| **Pattern** | Truth / structure | Verify truth, read probability | Change anything |
| **Resonance** | Connections / emotion | Sense/influence emotional states, communicate | Perceive physical reality directly |
| **Stillness** | Active magic only | Cancel/block other magic | Perceive anything when no magic is active |

---

## Key Rules

- **No natural fragments here.** Unlike Iolanu's 5 imposed fragments, Brenneth's schools are mortal-developed. The fragmentation is cultural, not cosmological.
- **Wild magic exists.** Unschooled quantum access. Unpredictable. Not a "sixth school" — no framework, no stability.
- **Multi-school is possible but hard.** Frameworks conflict. Most practitioners stick to one.
- **Theorists exist.** They try to unify all schools. Approaching the haze from below. Respected and watched.
- **All magic is quantum manipulation.** Same substrate as Iolanu, Algernon, etc. Different mortal expression.

---

## First Child

Unknown. Inhabitants do not know who made Brenneth. Three competing theories: (1) magic is natural law, no creator needed; (2) an unnamed maker left magic as a gift; (3) magic is a mortal achievement, creator irrelevant. None is correct. None is entirely wrong.

---

## Species Notes

- **Humans:** Majority. Practitioners of all five schools.
- **Elves (Caeldris):** Ancient northern kingdom. Oldest civilization on the continent. Elves have their own magical traditions predating the human schools. Specific Caeldrisi framework TBD.
- **No half-species.** Genetic incompatibility is a cosmological constant. No half-elves, no hybrids. Cross-species marriage is legally possible but biologically sterile.
