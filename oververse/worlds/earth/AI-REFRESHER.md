## One-Line Summary

**The FC activated the QWAN on Earth as a sandbox experiment, got results they didn't anticipate, and walked away. A scrambling Commission staff built the walls, invented the trial narrative, and has been managing the mess ever since.** Manchester NH has the only known terminus to Mercara's Cross. Nobody asked for this.

## What Actually Happened

The FC returned to Earth after detecting anomalous human development — a repo species outpacing its baseline projections. Their hypothesis was QWAN interaction with sapiens biology. Earth was a convenient sandbox: single planet, clean propagation envelope, a catalogued species with acceptable institutional risk. They activated the QWAN carrier signal and walked away to let it run.

They did not build walls. They did not design a trial. They flipped a switch and left.

**The QWAN interaction** — the FC expected some form of devolution or technology abandonment — a soft cultural drift away from advanced and dangerous systems. They didn't model the physics. The QWAN carrier signal interacted with combustion chemistry: explosives became unstable and detonated prematurely, fuel ran too hot for engines to handle, internal combustion became non-functional. Military hardware, weapons caches, fuel supplies — catastrophic physical failure across everything combustion-dependent. Human pineal glands activated simultaneously. The FC hadn't expected bombs going off or engines burning out. That wasn't the hypothesis. They got a hard physical cascade where they expected a soft cultural one, and they had no framework for interpreting it as useful data. FC lost interest and walked away. Left the QWAN running. Left no instructions.

**The Summer of Chaos** — QWAN saturated, explosives failing, global communications fragmenting, no walls yet, no alien presence, no explanation. Humans organized because someone had to. Rough governance emerged in rooms that needed it. Communities developed ownership over their own survival that nothing after would fully dislodge.

**The Fall** — the Amoskeag terminus opened. The Commission came through into a wall-less world mid-chaos with institutional competence and a narrative already assembling. The walls came from the Commission — their judgment call, their infrastructure, built using human administrative boundaries because that is what a staff body under pressure uses. The official narrative was constructed alongside the walls, each requiring the other.

The Fall of Humanity is not a collapse. It is the moment humanity discovers the Summer wasn't the worst part. The worst part is finding out what kind of universe you actually live in.

Earth Front's kernels are in the Summer. The specific loss of having figured something out, then having someone show up to explain it to you.

## The Official Narrative vs. Reality

**Official (Commission):** This is a trial. Humanity is being evaluated for integration into the wider multiverse. The walls are the evaluation mechanism. KPIs define success. When KPIs resolve, walls come down.

**Reality:** The trial narrative is entirely Commission-constructed — no FC authorization behind it. The FC ran a QWAN experiment and left. The Commission needed a story that gave the situation meaning and themselves institutional legitimacy. The KPIs are real enough as goals the Commission genuinely believes in. The integration metric is their own theory. The timeline is undefined. The walls might never come down.

Every non-human on Earth signed an NDA at Amoskeag customs as a condition of entry — no disclosure to humans that the trial is Commission mythology. Compliance is mostly sympathetic: other species understand why the fiction is necessary and don't want to be the one who blows it up.

Scratch the narrative hard enough and the seams show. The Commission knows this. They maintain it anyway because the alternative is worse.

## Humans in the FC Repo

Humans have been in the FC genetic repository since approximately 40,000 years ago, when sapiens emerged as the sole surviving human subspecies variant and the FC formally closed the development file. The current Earth situation has nothing to do with repo admission — that question was settled before recorded human history.

What humans mythologized as alien visitation was FC presence during the final observation and sample collection period, including early terminus operations transporting genetic samples to Mercara's Cross for formal repository filing. Humans taken through that terminus were returned. They remembered correctly that something extraordinary happened. They had no framework for what it was.

## The QWAN / Tech Regression

QWAN interacts with combustion chemistry. On Earth, this meant fuel runs too hot for engines to handle and explosive compounds become unstable. The FC did not anticipate this — they expected cultural or behavioral regression from technology, not a physical cascade. What they got: military ordnance detonating prematurely, weapons caches going off without being triggered, engines burning out, fuel supplies becoming dangerous. The process took weeks to fully manifest and months before anyone understood the pattern.

Result: Modern warfare non-viable. Motorized transport largely gone. Society adapted to non-explosive, non-motorized alternatives. The RV wagons, draft animals, and wood trade are all downstream of this. The QWAN is still running. None of this has been fixed.

## The Wall System (Condensed)

| Element | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Walls** | Commission-built. Follow administrative boundaries — county lines, city limits, CDP edges. International equivalents apply. |
| **Corridors** | Limited-access highways only. Walled travel arteries. Free movement once inside. |
| **Gatelocks** | Friction thresholds at room boundaries and corridor interchanges. Toll is the friction mechanism — makes crossings deliberate, not reflexive. |
| **Integration metric** | Commission's working theory for wall dissolution. Measures quality of cross-boundary interaction. 0–100% per segment. ~90% = walls dissolve. ~50% = walls reform. Progress is not permanent. |
| **Rails** | Cut by walls. Local engineering problem. |
| **Waterways** | Pass through tunnels where walls bisect them. Walls end at bank level on parallel stretches. Can swim under where walls cross waterways. |
| **Wall extent** | Underground (severs utilities) and high enough to block aircraft. |

## Manchester NH

Ground zero by accident — the Amoskeag terminus to Mercara's Cross landed here. Not chosen for cosmic significance. The terminus generates the traffic, the Commission concentration, the species presence. Everything radiates from Amoskeag Circle.

Manchester sits as a sub-room within Hillsborough County. I-93 runs through as a corridor, subdividing the city. The Millyard is Commission headquarters — 19th-century brick mill buildings along the Merrimack, chosen because the space was available and the hobgoblin who negotiated the lease was good at negotiating leases.

**City character:** Equal parts New England progressive and rural independent. Volatile integration metric. Fault lines that predate the walls. The walls didn't create the division — they made it legible.

**Scope:** Stories are Manchester-based, ranging along the immediate corridor network. What happens beyond is rumor. This is intentional. The world is big. The story is here.

## Key Institutions

| Institution | Role |
|------------|------|
| **The Commission** | Assembled in the aftermath. Primarily Homo staffers. Kitsune arbitration layer. Cayminus financial infrastructure. No enforcement force. Maintains the trial narrative. Believes in it to varying degrees. |
| **Wall Commission** | Sub-body. Handles wall boundary adjustments. Homo staffers only. Kitsune as non-voting interface. Narrow authority. No precedent, no appeals. |
| **Cayminus** | Built and runs the Gatelock payment infrastructure. Takes administrative fee from toll revenue. Commission operations funded by the remainder. |
| **Iridari (Pristails)** | Traveling witnesses. Tails cannot lie. Perform witnessed experiences for crowds elsewhere — the tail deployed, the colors undulating, the crowd experiencing what the Iridari felt. No live feed. Only the witness and eventually a performance. |
| **Bruin** | Independent authority in corridors and county remainder. "Don't be a problem" over local law. Not Commission employees. Authority is personal and reputational. |
| **Orcus** | Deployed by Commission as adversarial pressure. Released and dispersed geographically in the early post-wall period. Nobody made a decision so much as processed a requisition. |
| **Earth Front** | Human resistance. Four wings: principled opposition, pragmatic, political (rebuilding human governance), hardline (collaborator accusers). Kernels formed during the Summer of Chaos. |

## The NDA

Every non-human arriving through Amoskeag terminus signs a Commission NDA as a condition of entry — no disclosure to humans that the trial narrative is constructed rather than FC-authorized. Enforcement teeth exist (terminus access revocation) but are rarely needed. Most species comply because they understand why the Commission needs the fiction and don't want to be the one who blows it up.

The result: every non-human on Earth knows the trial is mythology. The humans are the only ones who don't.

---

## Homo Staffers vs. Wild Races

**Homo staffers** (elves, hobgoblins, dwarves, halflings, tinkari, bovinus) — FC staff doing FC work. Came through the terminus when the Commission assembled. Don't draw operational pay. The hobgoblin negotiating Millyard office space was good at negotiating office space and did it in an unfamiliar city because that's what the situation required.

**Wild races** — arrived for their own reasons after the terminus opened. Each species follows its own logic. Those on Commission payroll draw actual salaries from toll revenue. Their interests and Earth Front's "stop the tolls" strategy are in direct opposition.

---

## Tone

- FC activated the QWAN as a sandbox experiment and walked away when it went sideways. Careless, not malevolent.
- Commission is sympathetic — damage control under impossible conditions, not complicity. They believe in what they're doing to varying degrees.
- The trial narrative is constructed but not cynical. The KPIs are real goals even without FC authorization.
- Aliens are not morally superior. They have their own angles, their own baggage, their own reasons for being here.
- The story is Manchester. Not Earth. Not the multiverse. Tuesday in Manchester.
- The protagonist's silence is the most consequential thing they do every day.
