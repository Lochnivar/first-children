## One-Line Summary

**The FC activated the QWAN on Earth as a sandbox experiment, got results they didn't expect, and walked away. A scrambling Commission staff built the walls, invented the trial narrative, and has been managing the mess ever since.** Manchester NH has the only known terminus to Mercara's Cross. Nobody asked for this.

---

## What Actually Happened

The FC activated the QWAN on Earth — a sandbox experiment to observe QWAN effects on a single-planet world. The QWAN carrier signal interacted catastrophically with engineered explosive compounds. Military hardware, fuel supplies, weapons caches — premature detonations, cascading failures, modern warfare rendered non-viable. Human pineal glands activated. FC observed results inconsistent with experiment parameters, lost interest, and walked away. Left the QWAN running. Left no instructions.

**The Summer of Chaos** followed — QWAN saturated, explosives failing, global communications fragmenting, no walls yet, no alien presence, no explanation, no management. No aliens to blame. Humans organized because someone had to. Rough governance emerged. Communities developed ownership over their own survival.

**The Fall** — the Amoskeag terminus opened. The Commission came through into a wall-less world with institutional competence and a narrative already assembling. The walls came from the Commission — their judgment call, their infrastructure, built using human administrative boundaries because that is what a staff body under pressure uses. The official narrative was constructed alongside the walls, not before them.

The Fall of Humanity is not a collapse. It is the moment humanity discovers the Summer wasn't the worst part. The worst part is finding out what kind of universe you actually live in.

Earth Front's kernels are in the Summer. The people who organized during the chaos and then watched the Commission arrive to administer what they had already begun managing themselves. That specific loss — having figured something out, then having someone show up to explain it to you — is Earth Front's founding grievance.

The Commission assembled in the aftermath — not planned, not pre-positioned. Just the people who came through the terminus and understood someone had to manage this.

## The Official Narrative vs. Reality

**Official (Commission):** This is a trial. Humanity is being evaluated for integration into the wider multiverse. The walls are the evaluation mechanism. KPIs define success. When KPIs resolve, walls come down.

**Reality:** The trial narrative is entirely Commission-constructed — there is no FC authorization behind it. The FC ran a QWAN experiment and left. The Commission needed a story that gave the situation meaning and themselves institutional legitimacy. The KPIs are real enough as goals the Commission genuinely believes in. The integration metric is their own theory. The timeline is undefined. The walls might never come down. Nobody knows.

Every non-human on Earth signed an NDA at Amoskeag customs as a condition of entry — no disclosure to humans that the trial is Commission mythology. Compliance is mostly sympathetic: other species understand why the fiction is necessary and don't want to be the one who blows it up.

Scratch the narrative hard enough and the seams show. The Commission knows this. They maintain it anyway because the alternative is worse.

## The QWAN Interaction / Tech Regression

The QWAN carrier saturating Earth interacted with engineered explosive compounds — military ordnance, weapons caches, high-density fuel. Premature detonations wiped out military infrastructure in the early weeks. Remaining explosives became unstable and dangerous. Fuel supplies collapsed. Combustion-dependent transportation became unreliable.

Result: Modern warfare non-viable. Motorized transport largely gone. Society adapted to non-explosive, non-motorized alternatives. The RV wagons, draft animals, and wood trade are downstream of this.

## The Wall System (Condensed)

| Element | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Walls** | Follow administrative boundaries — county lines, city limits, CDP edges. International equivalents apply. |
| **Corridors** | Limited-access highways only. Walled travel arteries. Free movement once inside. |
| **Gatelocks** | Friction thresholds at room boundaries and corridor interchanges. Toll is the friction mechanism — makes crossings deliberate. |
| **Integration metric** | Commission's working theory for wall dissolution. Measures quality of cross-boundary interaction. 0–100% per segment. ~90% = walls dissolve. ~50% = walls reform. |
| **Rails** | Cut by walls. Local engineering problem. |
| **Waterways** | Pass through walls at bank level. Can swim under where walls cross waterways. |
| **Wall extent** | Underground (severs utilities) and high enough to block aircraft. |

## Manchester NH

Ground zero by accident — the Amoskeag terminus to Mercara's Cross landed here. Not chosen for cosmic significance. The terminus generates the traffic, the Commission concentration, the wild race presence. Everything radiates from the Amoskeag Circle.

Manchester is now Earth's de facto interface with the multiverse. Nobody elected it. The walls prevent anyone else from getting here easily. Federal authority exists on paper and nowhere else — DC is as room-bound as everyone else.

**City character:** Equal parts New England progressive and rural independent. Volatile integration metric. Fault lines that predate the walls. The walls didn't create the division — they made it legible.

**Scope:** Stories are Manchester-based, ranging along the immediate corridor network. What happens beyond is rumor. This is intentional — the Jericho model. The world is big. The story is here.

## Key Institutions

| Institution | Role |
|------------|------|
| **The Commission** | Manages the situation. Primarily Homo staffers. Kitsune arbitration layer. Cayminus financial infrastructure. No enforcement force. Outreach is primary human-facing tool. Maintains the trial narrative. |
| **Wall Commission** | Sub-body. Handles wall boundary adjustments. Homo staffers only. Kitsune as non-voting interface. Narrow authority. |
| **Cayminus** | Built and runs the Gatelock payment infrastructure. Takes administrative fee from toll revenue. Commission operations funded by remainder. |
| **Iridari (Pristails)** | Traveling witnesses. Tails cannot lie. Deploy tail to perform witnessed experiences for crowds. Not a broadcast network — presence required. |
| **Bruin** | Independent authority in corridors and county remainder. "Don't be an asshat" over local law. Not Commission employees. No oververse legal framework backs them — authority is personal and reputational. |
| **Orcus** | Deployed as adversarial pressure. Released and dispersed geographically by Commission. FC entertainment infrastructure, inherited along with everything else. |
| **Earth Front** | Human resistance. Kernels formed during the Summer of Chaos — people who organized without alien help and felt that ownership taken from them when the Commission arrived. Four wings: principled opposition, pragmatic, political (rebuilding human governance), hardline (collaborator accusers). Fringe acts independently. |

## The Protagonist

Human outreach employee for the Commission. Works for the institution managing the situation. Labeled a collaborator by Earth Front. Has access that reveals the trial narrative is thinner than advertised. Cannot say so — the truth is accelerant, not liberation. Does the job anyway.

Based in Manchester. Ranges the immediate corridor network as work requires.

---

## Homo Staffers vs. Wild Races

**Homo staffers** (elves, hobgoblins, dwarves, halflings, tinkari, bovinus) — FC staff doing FC work. Came through the terminus when the Commission assembled. Don't draw operational pay — this is FC work. The hobgoblin negotiating Millyard office space was good at negotiating office space and did it in an unfamiliar city because that's what the situation required.

**Wild races** — arrived for their own reasons after the terminus opened. Each species follows its own logic. Those on Commission payroll draw actual salaries from toll revenue. They need the money. Their interests and Earth Front's "stop the tolls" strategy are in direct opposition.

---

## Tone

- FC activated the QWAN as a sandbox experiment and walked away when results were unexpected. The "on a whim" framing is what the Commission assumes; the actual motivation was experimental. Either way, they left.
- Commission is sympathetic — damage control, not complicity. They believe in what they're doing to varying degrees.
- Aliens are not morally superior. They have their own angles, their own baggage, their own reasons for being here.
- The story is Manchester. Not Earth. Not the multiverse. Tuesday in Manchester.
- The protagonist's silence is the most consequential thing they do every day.
