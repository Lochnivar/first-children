## The Protagonist

**Role:** Human outreach employee for the Commission.

**What the job is:** Interface between the Commission and human communities. Works the neighborhoods, the corridor, the Wall Commission plaza. Knows the species by name — which Iridari is on which block, which Geliss is studying what, which Earth Front wing is in which room and what they want this week. The job requires being trusted by humans while working for the institution those humans most distrust.

**What the job costs:** Access. The protagonist knows — through the Commission work — that the trial narrative is thinner than advertised. Not the full picture, not the NDA, not the QWAN experiment origin. But enough to know the story has seams. Cannot say so. The truth doesn't set anyone free here. It's accelerant.

**The core dramatic situation:** Every day the protagonist does the job is a day they choose silence. That silence is not passive — it's the most consequential thing they do. Earth Front calls it collaboration. The Commission calls it professionalism. Neither framing is entirely wrong.

**Open:** Name, gender, background, how they came to work for the Commission.

---

## The Pressures

**Earth Front** — four wings, not unified. The hardline wing calls the protagonist a collaborator and means it personally. The pragmatic wing would use them if they could. The political wing might actually be trying to accomplish something real. The fringe is the variable. Which wing the protagonist has to deal with on a given day changes what the job looks like.

**The Commission** — sympathetic employer in an impossible position. They believe in what they're doing to varying degrees. The protagonist's access to the seams in the narrative is a function of being trusted inside the institution. That trust is the thing Earth Front is trying to break.

**The NDA crack** — the pattern is legible to anyone asking the right questions. The protagonist knows the pattern exists. Whether they know what it means, or whether someone else finds it first, is an open story question.

**The integration metric** — the protagonist's work directly affects the metric in the neighborhoods they cover. Success looks like walls coming down. Failure looks like walls going back up. The metric is not abstract — it's legible on the street.

---

## The Story's Shape

**Not established yet.** The inciting incident, the spine, the arc — all open.

What's established is the pressure system the protagonist operates inside. The story begins when something in that system changes — a specific Earth Front escalation, a specific Commission decision, a specific piece of the NDA crack surfacing, a specific relationship that puts the silence under unbearable pressure.

**Possible spines** (none chosen):
- The NDA crack surfaces — someone finds the filing or finds the pattern, and the protagonist has to decide what to do with what they know
- Earth Front escalation forces the Commission's hand in a way the protagonist can't neutralize from the outreach position
- A specific human relationship — neighbor, Earth Front contact, family — makes the silence personal in a way it wasn't before
- Something the protagonist learns through access crosses a line they didn't know they had

---

## Scope

Manchester. The immediate corridor network. What happens beyond is rumor. The world is big. The story is here.
