## QWAN Hardware — Brief Reference

**Pineal gland** — universal passive transponder. Functions as toll tag, identity unit, and QWAN ping device. Every species with a pineal gland has this working without activation or account. Humans discovered theirs was functional QWAN hardware when the walls went up.

**Amygdala** — dormant for QWAN purposes on Earth and most worlds. On Algernon-type worlds, QWST protocols hijack it to run the full LitRPG interface layer. Not relevant to Earth.

## The Earth Situation

The Earth trial is the most watched event in recent multiverse history. A 21st-century first-planet species encountering genuine sapient life for the first time, with full QWAN broadcast capability, real-time integration metric drama, and a trial design the veterans read as prosecutorial. The Iridari are proportionally present.

## What the Witnessing Means on Earth

The Iridari on the Manchester rooftops are witnessing. The multiverse will hear about it when those Iridari perform somewhere else, for a crowd, with their tails deployed. There is no live feed. There is only the witness, the tail, and eventually a room full of people who will know exactly what it felt like to be in Manchester NH when the walls went up.

Humans in the crowd feel the performance as fully as anyone. The amygdala processes emotion — the tail's effect works on them the same as every other species. They come out of an Iridari performance not entirely sure what happened to them. They attribute it to the spectacle, the colors, the quality of the storytelling.

They are not wrong that they felt something real. They are wrong about the mechanism.

Some Iridari have started sitting lower on the roof while witnessing. Not for legal reasons. Just because it felt like the decent thing to do. Their tails gave away that it bothered them that it bothered humans.
