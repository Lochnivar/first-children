## Burmallosca Incident
Years before the tour, Loisa's extraction from Burmallosca went wrong. Inquisitors of Hierarchy of Lust encountered her and Grayson. They had to couple publicly to avoid detection (Llosca culture = public intimacy acceptable). It was REAL coupling, not simulated (Inquisitors are experts, would detect faking). Both survived, both scarred differently.

### Loisa's Response (Earther):
- Modern perspective: sex = physical act
- Pragmatic, moved on quickly
- Finds it funny in retrospect
- Teases Grayson about it constantly
- No romantic feelings, just absurd situation

### The Dynamic:
"We remember Burmallosca very differently" (Black Widow/Hawkeye energy). She teases, he reddens, both know it's play. Inside joke that shows their bond. Eventually he performs exaggerated versions, inviting the teasing. Healthy friendship through shared absurdity.

### Brief Jealousy Arc:
When Grayson started dating Elsbeth, Loisa felt platonic possessiveness (not romantic). "He's MY best friend." Fear of losing priority. Recognized feelings, adapted. Made peace with new dynamic. All three became comfortable.
