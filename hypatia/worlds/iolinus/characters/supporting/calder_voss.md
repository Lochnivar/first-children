- Calder going off on Hara (too harsh, typical veteran behavior)
- Hara melting (mouse personality, can't defend herself)
- Gabby stands up, swats Calder (NCIS Gibbs-slap instinct)
- Calder confused (doesn't know Earth references), backs off
- Realizes he was too harsh
- Shows Calder's initial obtuseness

### Second Swat (Elsbeth → Calder)
- Calder forgotten, does it again to Hara
- Elsbeth learned context from Gabby
- MUCH harder swat
- Private lecture follows
- Calder actually learns, apologizes to Hara
- Shows Calder's capacity for growth

### Third Swat (Both → Calder - Playful Resolution)
- Weeks later
- Hara now stands firm (grown confident)
- Calder performs exaggerated caricature (self-aware, playful)
- Eyes glittering, watching for them

- Shows Calder's completed growth arc
