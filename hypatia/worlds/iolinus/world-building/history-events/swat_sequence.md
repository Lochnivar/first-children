# The Swat Sequence

**Category:** Character Development / Scene Structure / Character Arcs
**Related Characters:** Gabby D'Olivier, Hara Kest, Calder Voss, Elsbeth Thane, Loisa Xialing
**First Appearance:** [TBD - during orientation tour]

## Overview
A three-beat structure showing character growth through repeated interactions. Gabby and Elsbeth teach Calder to modulate his harsh behavior toward Hara through physical correction (swats). The sequence shows Gabby's protective instincts, Hara's confidence growth, Calder's learning, and group bonding through conflict resolution.

## Three-Beat Structure

### First Swat: Gabby → Calder

#### The Situation
- **Setting:** [TBD - during tour]
- **Trigger:** Calder going off on Hara (too harsh, typical veteran behavior)
- **Hara's response:** Melting (mouse personality, can't defend herself)
- **Gabby's instinct:** Stands up, swats Calder (NCIS Gibbs-slap instinct)

#### Gabby's Actions
- **Physical:** Swats Calder (instinctive protection)
- **Gesture:** Does "I'm watching you" fingers (American gesture)
- **Calder's reaction:** Confused (doesn't know Earth references), backs off
- **Realization:** Calder realizes he was too harsh
- **Earth behavior slip:** Instinctive protection, Earth behavior slip

#### Loisa's Response
- **Recognition:** Recognizes Earth behavior immediately (fellow Earther)
- **Coaching:** Privately coaches Gabby: "Something from where you come from" (vague, no specifics)
- **Protocol:** Earth culture concealment protocol
- **Protection:** Protects her from exposure

### Second Swat: Elsbeth → Calder

#### The Situation
- **Trigger:** Calder forgot, does it again to Hara
- **Elsbeth's knowledge:** Learned context from Gabby
- **Action:** MUCH harder swat (authority enforcement)

#### Elsbeth's Response
- **Physical:** Harder swat than Gabby's
- **Follow-up:** Private lecture follows
- **Result:** Calder actually learns, apologizes to Hara
- **Authority:** Proper correction through authority enforcement

### Third Swat: Both → Calder (Playful Resolution)

#### The Situation
- **Timeline:** Weeks later
- **Hara's growth:** Now stands firm (grown confident)
- **Calder's growth:** Performs exaggerated caricature (self-aware, playful)
- **Calder's behavior:** Eyes glittering, watching for them
- **Action:** Gabby and Elsbeth simultaneous swat (playful)
- **Outcome:** Everyone laughs
- **Bonding:** Inside joke, group bonding, growth shown
