# Llosca

**Earth Reference:** Dutch/Southeast Asian canal towns + European mining towns

## Physical Characteristics
- **Skin:** Mixed from pale to warm brown
- **Hair:** Dark to medium brown in practical styles for waterwork
- **Eyes:** Brown to gray
- **Build:** Sturdy, squat builds among canal workers and leaner miners from subterranean workshops

## General Clothing Notes
Water-resistant outerwear, low-profile boots for canal work, lamp-friendly caps, layered garments that transition from wet quays to dry caverns, and simple metal harnesses for miners.

## Cultural Elements
- **Canal infrastructure:** Dutch-inspired water management
- **Mining operations:** Subterranean workshops and extraction
- **Dual environment:** Adaptation to both aquatic and underground work
