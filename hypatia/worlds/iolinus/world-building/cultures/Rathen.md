# Rathen

**Earth Reference:** Scandinavian–Incan fusion adapted from Orindi ascent to high plateaus

## Physical Characteristics
- **Skin:** Fair to medium olive with high-altitude pallor common
- **Hair:** Ash blond to dark brown, usually plaited or tightly bound
- **Eyes:** Pale blues, steely grays, muted greens with keen depth perception
- **Build:** Compact, dense-boned frames and powerful legs

## General Clothing Notes
Scandinavian-derived layered tailoring with boiled wool, felt, alpaca-like fibers, compression harnesses, pale-lens visors, woven rune-terrace sashes, slate or pewter clasps, and ceramic damping plates.

## Cultural Elements
- **High-altitude adaptation:** Adapted from Orindi ascent to high plateaus
- **Terrace architecture:** Incan-inspired terrace systems
- **Scandinavian structural craft:** Combined with Incan terrace techniques
