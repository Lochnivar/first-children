# Orindi

**Earth Reference:** Northern European pale populations + speculative magnetic-desert inventors

## Physical Characteristics
- **Skin:** Very pale to sallow
- **Hair:** Light blond to platinum or darkened at roots
- **Eyes:** Reflective or light-sensitive tones (pale gray, icy blue, or silvery green)
- **Build:** Narrow faces and lean endurance builds

## General Clothing Notes
Functional layered garments with reflective panels and magnetically compatible fittings; travel cloaks, hooded parkas, compact boots, and minimalist ornamentation that signals guild or caravan.

## Cultural Elements
- **Magnetic desert adaptation:** Technology and clothing designed for magnetic environments
- **Minimalist aesthetic:** Functional design over ornamentation
