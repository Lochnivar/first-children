# Astelli

**Earth Reference:** Renaissance Florence + Japanese rooftop horticulture

## Physical Characteristics
- **Skin:** Fair to light olive, garden-tended
- **Hair:** Dark brown to chestnut, kept neat for horticultural work
- **Eyes:** Warm brown or green
- **Build:** Slender hands and poised posture shaped by terrace cultivation

## General Clothing Notes
Layered gardening smocks, fitted tunics for observatory work, ornamental pruning aprons, slim trousers or split skirts for mobility, and ceremonial cloaks with botanical embroidery.

## Cultural Elements
- **Horticultural focus:** Terrace cultivation and rooftop gardens
- **Renaissance-Japanese fusion:** Blend of Florentine artistry and Japanese garden aesthetics
- **Observatory work:** Astronomical and botanical observation
