## Maogreki

**Earth Reference:** Māori × Celtic coastal tribes

- **Physical:** Tawny to warm olive skin; black to auburn hair; dark brown to bright green eyes; athletic, compact builds; elders and warriors often bear facial tattooing or carved scar motifs
- **Clothing:** Flax cloaks, feathered mantles, pleated tunics with geometric weaving, shell or bone jewelry, civic badges
- **Cultural Elements:** Salt-circle rites; Greyrose Rangers (guardians of sacred sites, stewardship of bound souls); Echo Mothers (memory-keepers, custodians of communal history)
- **Related Characters:** Gabby D'Olivier (soul-transplanted into Maogreki body named Laila)

---

## Kitzengai

**Earth Reference:** Tibetan monasteries × Aztec aqueducts × Balinese water temples

- **Physical:** Pale olive to bronze, mist-stained; black to dark brown hair in looped braids or topknots with carved wood pins; deep brown, amber, or slate gray eyes; lean, tendon-strong climbers
- **Clothing:** Harness-integrated robes, mist veils, bone prayer chimes, heavy layered wraps, windproof skirts, stitched leather gaiters
- **Cultural Elements:** Vertical architecture; cliff terraces and high-altitude settlements; Kitzigen Falls (Ladyfinger flower cultivation, Eirendor's shrines)
- **Narrative Identifiers:** Mineral-stained skin, rope harness lines, echo-trained posture

---

## Llosca

**Earth Reference:** Andalusian × Arabian Nights (see [Llosca_Andalusian.md](Llosca_Andalusian.md) for reimagined version)

- **Physical:** Warm olive to golden bronze; dark brown to black hair, glossy and braided or veiled; amber, molten brown, or sea-green eyes; lithe dancers, sturdy riders
- **Clothing:** Flowing gowns, diaphanous veils, coin belts, embroidered bodices, henna patterns, jeweled hairpieces
- **Cultural Elements:** Merchant culture; Sufi mysticism; dual presentation (elegant public vs. sensuous private ritual)
- **Narrative Identifiers:** Perfumed garments, hennaed hands, veil shimmer

---

## Orindi

**Earth Reference:** Scandinavian cold-adapted × Saharan tech minimalism

- **Physical:** Very pale to sallow; blond to platinum hair, often with dark roots; pale gray, icy blue, or silvery green eyes; narrow, endurance-built frames
- **Clothing:** Reflective lenses, minimalist fastenings, stamped tunics, travel cloaks, hooded parkas, magnetically compatible fittings
- **Cultural Elements:** Magnetic desert adaptation; minimalist aesthetic; functional design over ornamentation
- **Narrative Identifiers:** Pale skin, stamped wrists, magnet-safe fittings

---

## Thohalla

**Earth Reference:** Old Testament covenant motifs × Romani caravan resilience

- **Physical:** Bronze to warm tan; dark brown to black hair, long and braided; deep brown or warm hazel eyes; agile, muscular, travel-trained
- **Clothing:** Layered practical armor (light mail/leather under cloaks); reinforced elbows, shoulders, knees; modular greaves and vambraces; visible repairs and patches; pennons and sashes for caravan identity
- **Cultural Elements:** Stateless, covenant-bound; Three Inherited Sins (Ancestral Burden, Mad Gift, Bound Tongue); Three Perpetual Vows (Stateless Exile, Riftwalker Duties, Word's Reckoning); Day Vigil ritual; hunt and seal mobile rifts
- **Historical:** Malachor's chosen people—played with, experimented on, discarded. Shapes covenant structure and sense of exile.
- **Related Characters:** Loisa, Grayson, Elsbeth, Calder, Hara, Gabby, Rowan, Marek

---

## Maneth

**Earth Reference:** Victorian England × Norse guildcraft

- **Physical:** Pale to fair; ash blond or dark brown hair, tied back with cords or shaved patterns; steel-gray or pale blue eyes; wiry, cold-weather adapted
- **Clothing:** Wool doublets, rope belts, mourning bands, fur collars, high collars, brooches with runic or guild motifs
- **Cultural Elements:** House Lochnivar (noble house, ritual legitimacy, Godshards); Seeker tradition (Godshard-hunting, fire-glyph magics); harbor oathkeepers; rune cairns
- **Related Characters:** Jasin "Loch" Lochnivar (Seeker, noble of House Lochnivar)

---

## Astelli

**Earth Reference:** Renaissance Florence × Japanese rooftop horticulture

- **Physical:** Fair to light olive; dark brown to chestnut hair, braided or coiled with Florentine ribbons and Japanese pins; warm brown or green eyes, almond-shaped; slender, poised hands
- **Clothing:** Gardening smocks, pruning aprons, embroidered cloaks, fitted tunics for observatory work
- **Cultural Elements:** Terrace cultivation; rooftop gardens; astronomical and botanical observation; Renaissance-Japanese fusion aesthetics
- **Narrative Identifiers:** Botanical embroidery, bamboo trellises, garden scent, terrace rituals

---

## Veylori

**Earth Reference:** Venetian lagoon × Creole bayou

- **Physical:** Olive to deep bronze, palm-shade pallor; dark brown to black hair, silky to coiled, shell pins or turbans; moss green, amber, or deep brown eyes; lithe, barefoot-trained balance
- **Clothing:** Layered robes, translucent shawls, coin-fringe belts, spice pouches
- **Cultural Elements:** Bayou courtiers; lagoon and wetland settlements
- **Narrative Identifiers:** Scented garments, coin jingles, barefoot plank-step

---

## Rathen

**Earth Reference:** Incan terracecraft × Scandinavian structural craft (Orindi-derived)

- **Physical:** Fair to medium olive with altitude pallor; ash blond to platinum, tightly braided or crown-coiled; glass blue, pale gray, or silvery green eyes; compact, dense-boned, powerful legs
- **Clothing:** Compression harnesses, stepped embroidery, slate badges, woven sashes, boiled wool, pale-lens visors
- **Cultural Elements:** High-altitude adaptation; terrace architecture; grafting and terrace maintenance
- **Narrative Identifiers:** Terrace-trained stance, grafting tools, civic knotwork

---

## Takoma

**Earth Reference:** Imperial China × Roman public works

- **Physical:** Warm olive to light brown; black to dark brown hair, bound in utilitarian styles; dark brown to deep hazel eyes; broad-shouldered officials, lithe clerks
- **Clothing:** Structured robes, embroidered insignia, reinforced tunics, standardized insignia for bureaucrats
- **Cultural Elements:** Bureaucratic structure; civic engineering; standardized systems; order, hierarchy, public service
- **Narrative Identifiers:** Scroll cases, survey tools, standardized insignia

---

## Wrinomo

**Earth Reference:** Swiss lake engineers × Vietnamese stilt towns

- **Physical:** Olive to light tan with occasional alpine rosiness; dark brown to sun-streaked hair, low buns or shoulder braids with reed/cord wraps; freshwater blue, slate green, or warm brown eyes; broad-shouldered commons, finer magistrate lineages
- **Clothing:** Indigo/ochre weaves, reed-knot badges, semi-transparent organza aprons, civic sashes with stepped embroidery
- **Cultural Elements:** Alpine lake engineering; stilted shore settlements; lake-basin infrastructure
- **Narrative Identifiers:** Ledger-callused fingers, water-stained hems, reed-knot insignia
