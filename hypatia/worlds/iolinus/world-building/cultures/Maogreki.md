# Maogreki

**Earth Reference:** New Zealand Māori + Classical Athens

## Physical Characteristics
- **Skin:** Tawny to warm olive
- **Hair:** Black to dark brown, thick and wavy
- **Eyes:** Dark brown to warm hazel
- **Build:** Athletic, compact builds; elders and warriors often bear facial tattooing or carved scar motifs

## General Clothing Notes
Cloaks and capes woven from flax or plant fibers; pleated tunics and skirts with geometric weaving; feathered ceremonial cloaks, shell or bone jewelry, and garlands for high-status roles.

## Cultural Elements
- **Greyrose Rangers:** Guardians tied to the greyrose ritual; tasked with protecting sacred sites and executing the community's stewardship of bound souls
- **Echo Mothers:** Memory-keepers; custodians of communal history and participants in rites that preserve or trade memory
- **Sacrificial and communal spiritual norms:** Emphasis on communal stewardship of souls and ritual practices
