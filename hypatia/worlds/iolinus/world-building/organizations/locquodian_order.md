# Locquodian Order

**Category:** Organization / Faction  
**Status:** Major faction preventing political unification

## Overview
The Locquodian Order is dedicated to preventing any central world empire from arising again. They actively work to prevent anything larger than a city-state from forming, both politically and physically.

## Historical Context

### The God-King Era
- Malachor ruled directly as God-King
- Unified world empire under his control
- Quantumancers ran unchecked, shattered mountains, remade landscapes
- Absolute power, absolute madness

### The Shattering
- Godstone = Malachor's connection to Iolinus
- Was shattered into seven pieces (seven Godshards)
- Malachor's direct control broken
- End of God-King reign

### Formation
- Formed after the Shattering
- Response to the chaos and collapse
- Mission: Never again allow a God-King or unified empire

## Core Mission

### Primary Purpose
- **No God-King:** Prevent any return to direct divine rule
- **No World Empire:** Block unified world government
- **No Unified Nation-States:** Enforce fragmentation
- **Enforced Fragmentation:** Maintain political balkanization

### Active Opposition
- Political intervention to prevent alliances
- Break up growing powers
- Deliberate balkanization
- Physical enforcement if needed (assassinations, warfare, sabotage)

## Methods
- Espionage
- Open warfare when necessary
- Diplomacy and manipulation
- "Physically too" - will use force
- Sabotage unification attempts
- Detection methods unclear (spies? divination?)

## Relationship to Other Factions

### vs. Church of Malachor
- Natural enemies
- Church wants unified faithful
- Locquodians prevent unity
- Church expanding = Locquodian target
- Constant friction

**Question:** Do Locquodians know about Malachor's divine nature, or are they fighting a god unknowingly? Or are they deliberately opposing him?

### vs. Nala's Apostasy
- May be accidentally helping Nala by keeping Malachor's power fragmented
- May be hindering Nala by preventing coordination
- Complex relationship - accidental allies and enemies simultaneously
- Enemy of enemy dynamic

### Role in Political Structure
- One of two factors enforcing city-state system (along with monster pressure)
- Prevent regional kingdoms or confederations
- Maintain fragmented political landscape
