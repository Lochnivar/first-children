# World Geography: Delverium

**Status:** World-Specific Expansion  
**Category:** Locations / World Structure  
**Related:** [../README.md](README.md)

## Overview

Delverium's geography is structured around a radial organization with central safe hubs radiating outward to increasingly dangerous zones, layered vertically from surface hubs to underground metropolises.

## Structure

### Layout

**Radial Organization:**
- Single mega-continent with radial organization
- Central safe hubs radiating outward to increasingly dangerous zones
- Layered vertically: surface hubs, underground metropolises

### Zones by Depth

1. **Safe Hubs:** Central cities with full System protection
2. **Shallow Zones:** Beginner dungeons, low-level threats
3. **Mid Zones:** Moderate challenges, common quantum pockets
4. **Deeps:** High-difficulty dungeons, legendary statics
5. **Abyssals:** Extreme challenge zones, permadeath risks elevated

### Biomes

**Forest Regions:**
- Trap-focused dungeons, ambush encounters

**Volcanic Zones:**
- Fire-themed pockets, lava hazards

**Crystal Caverns:**
- Puzzle dungeons, resonance mechanics

**Underdark Regions:**
- Undead crypts, shadow magic

**Coastal Areas:**
- Water dungeons, nautical themes

## Major Locations

### Anchorhold Prime

**Description:**
- Largest safe hub city
- Central guild headquarters
- Major resurrection stone network
- Market districts for loot trading
- Scholar academies studying the System

**Framework Alignment:**
- Safe zones use anchor protection (framework function)
- Resurrection stones are respawn crystals (greyrose-equivalent)

### The Grinding Fields

**Description:**
- Popular mid-zone training grounds
- High density of quantum pocket spawns
- Competitive dungeon racing circuits

**Framework Alignment:**
- Quantum pockets are localized instabilities (framework concept)

### The Curator's Gallery

**Description:**
- Region containing most static dungeons
- Tourist destination for sightseers and challengers
- Keepwarden headquarters located here

**Framework Alignment:**
- Static dungeons are anchored configurations (framework function)
