## Overview

This document tracks questions that need to be resolved for Hegemon's development. These questions help guide future world-building decisions while maintaining framework conformance.

   - Cultural dominance?

   - Peer-enforced penalties?
   - Temporary exile?

4. **Are there agreed-upon rules or is it anarchic competition?**

5. **Can new First Children join the game or original players only?**

   - Closed to original creators?

## Development Priorities

**Next Steps:**
1. Resolve victory conditions and game structure
2. Develop 2-3 specific First Children player personalities
3. Detail example territory with its culture/patron
4. Design a champion character with quantum fragments
5. Outline a sample strategic conflict
6. Determine reset mechanics (if any)
7. Explore resistance movement structure

**Tone Considerations:**
- Balance strategy game fun with moral weight
- Avoid grimdark nihilism or pure cynicism
- Show mortal agency despite divine manipulation
- Make First Children competition compelling but questionable
- Leave room for hope and resistance
