## Framework Context

Burgs represent cities, towns, and settlements within the Hegemon world. These are the centers of population, commerce, and strategic importance where the First Children's game is played out through mortal agents.

**Key Framework Points:**
- Burgs are mortal settlements within the quantum foundation
- Population indicates economic and strategic importance
- Type (Naval, Generic, River, Lake, etc.) reflects strategic characteristics
- Cultural affiliation shapes how burgs respond to manipulation
- State ownership determines political control and strategic value
