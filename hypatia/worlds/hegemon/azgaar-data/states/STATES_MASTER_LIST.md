## Framework Context

These states represent political entities within the Hegemon world, where First Children (The Strategists) manipulate and influence civilizations as part of their strategic game. Each state is a "piece" on the board, with characteristics that make them more or less useful for different types of strategic manipulation.

**Key Framework Points:**
- States are mortal political constructs within the quantum foundation
- Expansionism values reflect territorial ambitions and aggressiveness
- Military power represents both defensive capability and offensive potential
- Population and area indicate state scale and resources
- Form of government influences how states respond to external manipulation
- Type (Generic/Nomadic/Hunting) reflects cultural foundations and strategic characteristics
