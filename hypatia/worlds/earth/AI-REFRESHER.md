# Earth Trial — AI Refresher

**Purpose:** Quick-reference for AI context when working on Earth trial stories, world-building, or integration.  
**Scope:** Earth-specific trial mechanics (world-to-dungeon transformation)  
**Canonical source:** [trial-mechanics.md](trial-mechanics.md)

---

## One-Line Summary

**21st-century Earth undergoes trial** — final vetting for Homo sapiens as generalist FC staff. Walls, Gatelocks, integration metric. Independent contractors (not refugees) deploy via Platform 934. Manchester = ground zero.

---

## Trial Purpose

- **KPI 1 (Generalist Viability):** Do sapiens have the traits the design doc specified? Trajectory across generational timescales.
- **KPI 2 (Colonist Filter):** Can sapiens cooperate with non-sapiens as default, not just under desperation?
- Not punishment, not rescue. Quality assurance. The Gray records; does not editorialize.

---

## Wall System (Condensed)

| Element | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Rooms** | Administrative boundaries (county, town, city, CDP). International equivalents apply. |
| **Corridors** | Limited-access highways only (Interstates, motorways). Not all major roads. |
| **Gatelocks** | Tolled entry/exit at room boundaries and corridor interchanges. Cayminus/QWAN payment. |
| **Integration metric** | 0–100% per wall segment. ~90% = walls dissolve, rooms merge. ~50% = walls reform. |
| **Rails** | Cut by walls; can be torn up and rerouted. Not corridors. |
| **Waterways** | Tunnels or under walls; walls end at bank level; can swim under. |
| **Wall extent** | Underground (sewers, power, data); blocks aircraft and balloons. |

---

## Independent Contractors (Not Refugees)

Non-human species on Earth are **independent contractors** — professionals with their own priorities, operating under their own structures or as freelancers. They have passed their own trials. They deploy via Platform 934 during Prep Year. They go home when rotation/contract ends. The indifference (exit option, no skin in the game) is itself a trial pressure.

---

## Key Institutions

- **Wall Commission:** Homo genus staffers only (elves, hobgoblins, dwarves, halflings, tinkari). Kitsune arbitrators = human-interface layer (non-voting). Narrow authority: boundary nudges, orphaned segments, temporary passage.
- **Pristails (Iridari):** De facto media corps. Tails cannot lie. Broadcast trial events for multiverse audience.
- **Bruin (Marshals/Rangers):** Contracted enforcers across jurisdictions. "Don't be a problem" in county remainder.
- **Orcus:** FC resources — NPCs, mobs, adversaries. Not staff. Present on Earth in that capacity.

---

## FC Manifestation: The Gray

When First Children manifest physically in Hypatia, they take the form of the Roswell Gray. Non-readable. Observers can appear (cameos). The Gray at the plaza is recording, not rooting.

---

## Tone

- Trial = QA on a product that took hundreds of thousands of years to iterate.
- Story = humans surviving Tuesday. KPIs count Tuesdays; they don't judge them.
- Aliens are not morally superior. Professionals with baggage. The cruelty is in the indifference.
