# Stratara

**Status:** Proposed World - In Development  
**Framework Conformance:** Active - See framework summary in [../stratara.md](../stratara.md)  
**Last Updated:** December 2024

## Overview

Stratara is a world that demonstrates **collaborative world creation** and how such collaborations can spiral into conflicts over ownership and control. The world functions as a cosmic "game" among First Children siblings, highlighting framework concepts of peer judgment, contested creations, and the moralistic gray areas of First Children interactions.

**Concept:** A planet-sized "board" divided into territories, where First Children compete for control through proxy wars fought by mortals, all operating within the quantum foundation of Hypatia.

## Framework Elements Demonstrated

- **Collaborative Creation:** Multiple First Children created Stratara jointly
- **Ownership Conflicts:** Questions of "whose world is it?" drive the narrative
- **Peer Judgment:** External First Children debate whether the "game" violates moralistic rules
- **Quantum Foundation:** All mechanics operate through quantum manipulation
- **Open Cosmology:** Mortals are aware of the First Children "players"
- **Balance and Imbalance:** Conquest creates growth but risks instability
