## Key Infrastructure

**The Grand Anchor (The Pillar)**
- Massive quantum stabilization construct; visible from anywhere
- Built by Virelle (Transit Autist)
- Stabilizes all rift connections simultaneously
- Most sophisticated anchor network in Hypatia
- Enables safe soul transfers between worlds
- Administered by Riftkeepers (Virelle's creation)

**Portal Platforms**
- 1,247+ documented platforms in concentric rings around the Pillar
- Built by Virelle
- Each platform = gate to a different world
- Numbering: 1–100 (ancient, prestigious), 101–1000 (established), 1200+ (recent, experimental)
- Scheduling by quantum probability alignment
- Solo mortal crossings impossible by design — batch transits ("trains") prevent deadly wake turbulence

**The Ledger**
- Primary: Transit safety — pattern backup, corruption detection, emergency reconstruction (Virelle's domain, Riftkeepers run it)
- Secondary: Contract enforcement (Legara's domain; merchants co-opted it; voluntary registration, both parties consent)
- Quantum database; revenue funds anchor maintenance

**Riftkeepers (Capra Quantus)**
- Created by Virelle for transit maintenance (goat-derived; Anchor Goats)
- Quantum-sensitive horns; detect problems before instruments
- Psychologically bound to Grand Anchor (cannot leave)
- Staccato speech; scream at emergencies (and dragons)
- The Silence Problem: perfect schedule = anxiety = 3 AM bleating chorus
- Focus on safety, not law enforcement; respected rather than resented

## Mercara (The Owner)

- **Pronouns:** She/her
- **Epithets:** The Cross Owner, The Crossroads Keeper, The Connector, The Hub
- **Motivation:** Pure curiosity. Watches mortals like reality TV. Built The Cross for front-row seats. "I could do anything. I choose to WATCH."
- **Empathy gap:** Closest any FC gets to empathy toward mortals. That gap is still wide. Cares like you care about TV characters—invested but watching, not living it.
- **Philosophy:** "I help because I want to see what happens NEXT." Doesn't over-intervene; wants authentic stories.
- **Involvement:** Manages public spaces, mediates (wants to hear BOTH sides), appears in disguise (tea vendor, info clerk). Elves = PR-focused homo staff (her genetic repo contribution); at the Cross they help people and report to her—her "viewing crew" use of them.
- **Personality:** Enthusiastic, nosy, genuinely curious. Slightly oblivious (doesn't see voyeurism as wrong). Triad: "She's nosy. But helpful. Weird combination."
