# Integration with Other Worlds: Mercara's Cross

**Status:** World-Specific Expansion  
**Category:** World Relationships / Integration  
**Related:** [Hegemon](../hegemon.md), [Stratara](../stratara.md), [Delverium](../delverium.md), [../README.md](README.md)

## Overview

Mercara's Cross connects to all worlds in Hypatia, serving as the central transit hub. Different platforms handle traffic to different world clusters, and each connection has unique characteristics and story potential.

## Hegemon/Stratara Connection

### Platform 12

**Function:**
- Handles diplomatic transits for game-world heroes
- High-security platform for strategic transfers
- Auction houses specifically cater to First Children players
- Military contracts and mercenary services
- Strategic intelligence marketplace
- Diplomatic services for game-world alliances

**Story Potential:**
- Stratara barbarian arrives at Cross, culture shock
- Hegemon diplomat conducting secret negotiations
- Hero realizes they're being traded between game boards
- First Children players meeting at Cross to argue over rules
- Liberation operation staging from margin zones

**Framework Alignment:**
- Connects to collaborative creation worlds (framework allows)
- Hero trading hub (world-specific)
- Strategic services (framework allows)

## Delverium Connection

### Platform 7

**Function:**
- Handles dungeon-world traffic
- Gear merchants specializing in enchanted equipment
- Resurrection insurance (expensive for non-residents)
- Dungeon maps and intelligence
- Keepwarden equipment suppliers

**Story Potential:**
- Delverium adventurer shopping for Cross-world gear
- Zorathar's avatar browsing for System improvements
- Champion from Delverium gets traded to Hegemon (game mechanics don't translate)
- Merchant arbitrage between world magic systems

**Framework Alignment:**
- Connects to experimental world (framework allows)
- Adventurer services (world-specific)
- Cross-world commerce (framework allows)

## Earth Connection

### Platform 934

**Function:**
- Handles Earth soul transits
- Technology marketplace (modern goods)
- Cultural curiosity shops
- "Otherworlder" integration services
- Front companies for liberation operations

**Story Potential:**
- Earth soul arrived for liberation, waiting for deployment
- Smuggler network moving Earth tech illegally
- First Children studying human innovation
- Earth refugee choosing to stay at Cross
- Someone from Earth trying to return home

**Framework Alignment:**
- Connects to control world (framework allows)
- Liberation operation staging (framework pattern)
- Cultural exchange (framework allows)

## Unknown/New Worlds

### Margin Speculation

**Function:**
- New portals open in flux zones periodically
- Speculators rush to be first traders
- Unknown goods, unknown risks
- Some new worlds are dangerous
- Riftkeepers monitor carefully

**Story Potential:**
- Portal to unknown world opens, what comes through?
- Speculation bubble and crash
- New world with hostile First Child, Cross becomes battleground
- Discovery of world thought lost

**Framework Alignment:**

- Speculation and risk (framework allows)
- Riftkeeper monitoring (framework function)
