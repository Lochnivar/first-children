## Key Takeaways

### Scale of Hypatia

**Documented Platforms:** 1,247+ platforms imply well over a thousand connected (or once-connected) worlds in the Library.

**Framework Alignment:**
- Demonstrates the vast scale of Hypatia (framework allows)
- Multiple worlds exist (framework truth)
- Cross connects to all worlds (world-specific function)

### Platform 1237: Iolinus

**Status:** One of the newest additions before the seal

**The Seal:**
- Shadow-wall is absolute
- Even First Children honor the peer judgment and do not use (or cross) the portal
- They simply bypass it entirely through the quantum sea if they ever need to visit
- Since the judgment, they do not visit

**Framework Alignment:**
- Peer judgment seal demonstrates framework enforcement (framework rule)
- First Children respect peer judgment (framework pattern)
- Bypass through quantum sea shows First Children capabilities (framework allows)
- No traffic demonstrates seal effectiveness (framework function)

### Platform 934: Earth

**Status:** Comfortably mid-to-late addition

**Characteristics:**
- Older and more established traffic than Iolinus
- Still "new" compared to the ancient low numbers
- Primary liberation source
- Mostly inbound traffic (Earth souls arriving for liberation operations)
- Dedicated integration desk for confused new arrivals

**Framework Alignment:**
- Earth is a world in Hypatia (framework truth)
- Source of liberation souls (framework pattern)
- Integration services exist (framework allows)

## Platform Categories

### Active Platforms
- Regular traffic and commerce
- Open to standard transit
- Examples: 7 (Delverium), 42, 99, 100, 666, 777, 888, 934 (Earth), 935, 936, 999, 1000, 1247

### Restricted Platforms
- Limited access with guards and paperwork
- Diplomatic or special purpose
- Examples: 12 (Stratara)

### Sealed Platforms
- Peer judgment seal preventing access
- No traffic, no First Children use
- Examples: 1237 (Iolinus)

### Rare/Ancient Platforms
- Rarely open, half-mythical
- Historical significance
- Examples: 1 (The First Connection)
