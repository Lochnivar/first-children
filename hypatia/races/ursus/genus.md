## Definition

Genus Ursus is defined by the bear-derived genetic template from the FC genetic repository. Created as a generalist predator framework; no designated role. Individuals have adapted into law enforcement roles (marshals, rangers) because their traits naturally suit the work—not by FC design.

### Physical
- **Bear-derived** — 6–8 feet tall, 300–600 lbs; thick fur, retractable claws, omnivore dentition
- **Powerful build** — Broad shoulders, dense muscle, intimidating presence
- **Long-lived** — 200–400 years

### Temperament
- **Solitary** — Prefer working alone; crowds exhausting
- **Territorial** — Strong sense of boundaries; respect yours and theirs
- **Independent** — Make own decisions; set own methods; can walk away
- **Patient but decisive** — Can wait; act fast when needed
- **Practical** — What works matters; simplicity valued

### Framework
- **Repository template** — Unknown/multiple FC; not single project
- **Sapient expression:** [Ursine Marshals](marshals.md) — emergent law enforcement profession
