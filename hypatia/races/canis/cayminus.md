# Cayminus (Coyote Folk)

**Status:** Framework Element - Universal Race  
**Category:** Races / Species  
**Related:** [Genus Canis](genus.md), [Legal Systems](../../cosmology/legal-systems.md), [Mercara's Cross](../../worlds/mercara-cross.md)

## Overview

Cayminus (*Canis cayminus*) are coyote-based anthropomorphic people. Common names: **Coyote folk**, Nummus's bankers. Nummus's banking staff at Mercara's Cross—banking vaults, quantum ledger, financial systems. Contractual and opportunistic; everything is a deal.

## Legal Code

- Contractual & opportunistic justice
- Everything is a deal. Breach a contract → restitution or servitude
- Disputes settled by negotiation or arbitration (they're good at it)
- Punishment: Debt slavery until repaid (with interest)
- "You entered the agreement. You pay the price. Simple."

### Truism
*"Sound books, steady hands, and a great deal of patience."*

## Role at Mercara's Cross

- Banking operations (Nummus's domain)
- Quantum ledger maintenance
- Financial systems, contract enforcement
- Complete autonomy in banking ops (per Nummus's arrangement)

## Relationships

- **Capra (Riftkeepers):** Uncomfortable—goat musk
- **Kitsune:** Legara's legal staff; complementary roles (banking + contracts)

## Author Notes (Feel)

**Nummus context:** Nummus is the most "Trump-like" FC—chaotic, provocative, likely to offend. A Cayminus job is half banker, half apologizing to the people Nummus pissed off. Sound books, steady hands, patience with the ledgers *and* patience with the political fallout.
