# Genus Canis

**Status:** Framework Element - Universal  
**Category:** Races / Genus Definition  
**Related:** [Genetic Frameworks](../genetic-frameworks.md), [Mercara's Cross](../../worlds/mercara-cross.md), [../README.md](../README.md)

## Definition

Genus Canis is defined by the canid-derived genetic template from the FC genetic repository. Coyote-derived expression serves as Nummus's banking staff at Mercara's Cross.

### Physical
- **Canid-derived** — Coyote, wolf, or dog framework
- **Agile, adaptable** — Suited to varied environments

### Temperament
- **Contractual** — Everything is a deal
- **Opportunistic** — Practical, transactional
- **Negotiation-focused** — Arbitration, settlement

### Framework
- **Non-sapient baseline:** Coyotes, wolves, dogs, hyenas (Earth template)
- **Sapient expression:** [Cayminus](cayminus.md) — coyote folk; Nummus's banking staff at Mercara's Cross
- **Sapient expression:** [Gnoll](gnoll.md) — hyena folk; chaos-souls; emergent; no designated role
