# Giff

**Status:** Framework Element - Universal Race  
**Category:** Races / Species  
**Related:** [Races README](README.md), [../FRAMEWORK.md](../../FRAMEWORK.md)

## Overview

Giff are a strong, hippo-like humanoid race known for their military discipline, love of explosions, and honorable conduct. They are larger and more physically powerful than humans, with thick hides, powerful builds, and a distinctive hippopotamus-like appearance. Giff are often characterized by their strict military organization, love of firearms and explosives, and strong sense of honor and duty.

## Universal Characteristics

### Physical Attributes
- **Lifespan:** Moderate to long (typically 80-120 years, varies by world)
- **Size:** Large and powerfully built (typically 7-8 feet tall)
- **Build:** Massive, muscular, with thick hides
- **Strength:** Exceptional physical strength and resilience
- **Appearance:** Hippopotamus-like features (broad snout, thick skin, powerful build)

### Cognitive Abilities
- **Intelligence:** Fully intelligent, often strategic and tactical
- **Discipline:** Strong mental discipline and focus
- **Learning:** Good learners, especially for military and technical skills
- **Memory:** Strong memory for tactics, history, and obligations

### Social Characteristics
- **Military:** Often organized into military units or mercenary companies
- **Honor:** Strong sense of honor, duty, and commitment
- **Community:** Close-knit unit bonds and loyalty
- **Reproduction:** Moderate population growth

## Framework Role

### Military and Discipline
- Represent military order, discipline, and honor
- Often serve as mercenaries, soldiers, or guardians
- Provide interesting contrasts with less organized races
- Create opportunities for military and tactical story elements

### Explosives and Technology
- Known for love of firearms, explosives, and technology
- May be master craftspeople of weapons and explosives
- Could have advanced technology or access to it
- Create interesting technological dynamics

## Variations Across Worlds

### Physical Variations
- Size and build vary slightly by world
- Some worlds: very large and imposing
- Other worlds: moderately larger than humans
- Appearance details (coloration, features) vary

### Cultural Variations
- Military-focused cultures, mercenary companies, honor-bound societies
- Some worlds: primarily military/mercenary
- Other worlds: may have civilian giff populations
- Values emphasize honor, duty, discipline, and explosions

### Technological Variations
- Some worlds: advanced firearms and explosives technology
- Other worlds: mastery of existing technology
- May have unique technological innovations
- Could interact with magic/quantum abilities through technology

## Honor and Duty
