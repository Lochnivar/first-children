# Djemari (Camel People)

**Status:** Framework Element - Universal Race  
**Category:** Races / Species  
**Related:** [Genus Camelus](genus.md), [Races README](../README.md), [Llamari](../lama/llamari.md), [../../FRAMEWORK.md](../../../FRAMEWORK.md)

## Overview

Djemari are camel-based anthropomorphic people created through FC panspermia. Water conservation, endurance, phlegmatic calm. Not a designated role or biome lock. Peak phlegmatic: nothing bothers them. Low-energy calm; biologically adapted to economical movement and minimal emotional expenditure.

## Universal Characteristics

### Physical Attributes
- 7-8 feet tall
- Humped backs (water/fat storage)
- Can go days without water
- Broad, padded feet
- Sandy brown to dark brown coloring
- Closable nostrils (particulate)

### Emergent Tendencies
- Water conservation, endurance, phlegmatic → long-haul work, low-resource efficiency
- Traits lead naturally to transport, hauling, steady-state operations

### Temperament: Peak Phlegmatic
- Nothing bothers them. Ever. At all.
- Unflappable (world ending? "Mm. Noted.")
- Minimal emotion (not suppressed, just don't have much)
- Deadpan delivery (everything in monotone)
- Economical movement (why waste energy?)
- Biologically adapted to low-energy state; panic wastes resources

### Truism
*"Noted."*

### Culture
- Nomadic routes (established circuits, not endless wandering)
- Trade caravans (seasonal routes)
- Water is sacred (never waste)
- Endurance competitions (pride)
- Oral storytelling tradition

## Story Potential

### Story Function
- Long-haul expertise
- Water transport, steady-state operations
- Deadpan comedy (ultimate calm vs. human panic)
- Endurance model

### Djemari + Llamari Dynamic
Djemari and Llamari often work together on long-haul routes. Ultimate calm meets ultimate anxiety—comedy gold through contrast. Both professionally competent despite appearances; mutual respect between specialists.

### Example Dialogue
- Human: "PEOPLE ARE DYING!"
- Djemari: "Yes. Happens when water runs out. I will get water."
- Human: "WHEN?!"
- Djemari: [already loading cargo, no urgency] "Now. Going now. See?"
- Human: "Can you go FASTER?!"
- Djemari: [long pause] "No."

### Example Character
Rashid the Red-Humped, 94 years old, deep brown, single large hump. Grumbles constantly but never fails.

## Variations Across Worlds

- Trade routes and cultural expressions vary
- Relationship to Llamari at handoff points may vary
