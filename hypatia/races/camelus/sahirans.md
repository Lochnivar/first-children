# Sahirans (Camel People)

**Status:** Framework Element - Universal Race  
**Category:** Races / Species  
**Related:** [Genus Camelus](genus.md), [Races README](../README.md), [Highwalkers](../lama/highwalkers.md), [../../FRAMEWORK.md](../../../FRAMEWORK.md)

## Overview

Sahirans are camel-based anthropomorphic people created through FC panspermia. They aren't restricted to desert—their traits make them suited to it. Water conservation, endurance, phlegmatic calm: ideal for desert hauling, but those same traits could suit long hauls anywhere. Not a designated role or biome lock. Peak phlegmatic: nothing bothers them. Low-energy calm is essential where panic means death—desert is one expression, not the only one.

## Universal Characteristics

### Physical Attributes
- 7-8 feet tall
- Humped backs (water/fat storage)
- Can go days without water
- Heat resistance
- Broad, padded feet
- Sandy brown to dark brown coloring
- Can close nostrils against sandstorms

### Emergent Tendencies
- Water conservation, endurance, phlegmatic → suited to desert (common expression, not restriction)
- Could suit long-haul work anywhere, low-resource environments—same traits, different biome
- Often desert transport, water hauling—because traits make them ideal for it

### Temperament: Peak Phlegmatic
- Nothing bothers them. Ever. At all.
- Unflappable (world ending? "Mm. Noted.")
- Minimal emotion (not suppressed, just don't have much)
- Deadpan delivery (everything in monotone)
- Economical movement (why waste energy?)
- Desert survival requires calm—panic = death; biologically adapted to low-energy state

### Culture
- Nomadic routes (established circuits, not endless wandering)
- Trade caravans (seasonal routes)
- Water is sacred (never waste)
- Endurance competitions (pride)
- Oral storytelling tradition

## Story Potential

### Story Function
- Desert survival expertise
- Water transport (life-or-death in desert regions)
- Deadpan comedy (ultimate calm vs. human panic)
- Endurance model

### Sahiran + Highwalker Dynamic
Sahirans and Highwalkers often hand off cargo at desert-mountain boundaries. Ultimate calm meets ultimate anxiety—comedy gold through contrast. Both professionally competent despite appearances; mutual respect between specialists.

### Example Dialogue
- Human: "PEOPLE ARE DYING!"
- Sahiran: "Yes. Happens when water runs out. I will get water."
- Human: "WHEN?!"
- Sahiran: [already loading cargo, no urgency] "Now. Going now. See?"
- Human: "Can you go FASTER?!"
- Sahiran: [long pause] "No."

### Example Character
Rashid the Red-Humped, 94 years old, deep brown, single large hump. Grumbles constantly but never fails.

## Variations Across Worlds

- Desert regions and trade routes vary
- Cultural expressions of phlegmatic temperament differ
- Relationship to Highwalkers at terrain boundaries may vary
