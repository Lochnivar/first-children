# Dwarves

**Status:** Framework Element - Universal Race  
**Category:** Races / Species  
**Related:** [Races README](../README.md), [../../FRAMEWORK.md](../../FRAMEWORK.md)

## Overview

Dwarves in Hypatia are First Children purpose-built engineers—they build and maintain FC infrastructure, create anchoring systems for worlds, and forge artifacts and tools. Sturdy, practical, and gruff, they view mortals as unskilled labor and respect FC authority as absolute. They fix actual bugs, not "features."

## Framework Role: FC Staff

### Origin
Created by First Children as purpose-built engineers for divine infrastructure.

### Role
- Build/maintain FC infrastructure
- Create anchoring systems for worlds
- Forge artifacts/tools
- Repair/sustain physical structures
- Mine/process raw materials

### Physical Traits
- Sturdy, resilient (work in harsh conditions)
- Skilled craftsmen
- Strong (physical labor)
- Resistance to harsh conditions

### Personality
- Practical, no-nonsense
- Pride in craftsmanship
- Respect hierarchy (FC authority absolute)
- Gruff but reliable
- View mortals as unskilled labor

### Story Function
- Technical exposition (how systems work)
- Infrastructure logic
- Engineering perspective
- Frustrating obstacles (try arguing with an engineer)

### Example Character
Grimnar Ironbrace, Gateway Maintenance Engineer—312 years old. "Functioning within parameters. User error. Read the instructions." Will fix actual bugs, not "features."

## Universal Characteristics

### Physical Attributes
- **Lifespan:** Long-lived (typically 200-400 years, varies by world)
- **Size:** Shorter than humans but broader and more robust
- **Build:** Strong, stocky, physically resilient
- **Stamina:** Exceptional endurance and physical toughness

### Cognitive Abilities
- **Craftsmanship:** Natural talent for making things
- **Engineering:** Strong understanding of construction and mechanics
- **Focus:** Can concentrate on detailed work for extended periods

## Variations Across Worlds

Worlds may present dwarves differently:
- **FC Staff (primary):** Engineers serving First Children infrastructure
- **Traditional:** Master craftspeople, miners, community-focused
- **Cultural:** Mountain dwarves, hill dwarves, city dwarves—different expressions based on world

Individual worlds can include, exclude, or modify this framework characterization.
