# Draconis sapiens

**Status:** Framework Element - Universal Race  
**Category:** Races / Species  
**Related:** [Genus Draconis](genus.md), [Races README](../README.md), [Genetic Frameworks](../genetic-frameworks.md), [../../FRAMEWORK.md](../../FRAMEWORK.md)

## Overview

*Draconis sapiens* are dragon-inspired humanoids created by First Children (Epsilon specifically). FC-created, but **not** FC staff—classifying the genus as staff would be one insult too far. Bipedal humanoids with dragon features—scales, claws, tail, fangs, horns—they are legitimate FC repository templates and the **only** actual members of Genus Draconis by FC taxonomy. Greater Dragons reject the genus; *Draconis sapiens* are stuck with a name that implies relationship to beings who deny it.

**Origin:** Created out of spite. Epsilon and Aurelion argued for millennia. Aurelion: "You're immature, cruel, don't understand consequences." Epsilon: "I'll show YOU—I'll make BETTER dragons!" Created to prove FC could "do dragons too" and to annoy Greater Dragons.

**Aurelion's assessment:** "You created mockeries of my children out of spite. This proves my point about your immaturity."

## Framework Role

### FC Creation (Spite-Built, Not Staff)

- Dragon-inspired humanoid (morphological, not genealogical)
- Not related to Greater Dragons, Lesser Dragons, or Kobolds
- Pale imitations of Lesser Dragons
- Their existence is taxonomic warfare—tools of historical revisionism (FC catalog dragons in same genus; structure implies FC creation)

---

## Universal Characteristics

### Physical Attributes

- **Form:** Bipedal humanoid with dragon features (scales, claws, tail, fangs, horns)
- **Lifespan:** 80–150 years (failed to replicate dragon longevity)
- **Strength:** Enhanced over *Homo*, inferior to dragons

### Capabilities

- **Breath weapon:** Weak (~1/20th Lesser Dragon power)
- **Combat:** Better than *Homo*, worse than dragons

---

## The Taxonomic Irony

- Greater Dragons reject genus classification (they are individuals, not species)
- *Draconis sapiens* are the only beings who fit the FC genus definition
- But they're the "fake" ones—created to insult the real dragons
- Named after beings who reject the name
- "We're the only real 'Draconis' but we're the FAKE ones"

---

## Identity

*Draconis sapiens* who learn their origin face an identity crisis: created to spite, used as propaganda, not related to the beings they're named after. "Our entire existence is taxonomic warfare. We're propaganda made flesh."
