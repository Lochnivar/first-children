# Corvani (Blackwings)

**Status:** Framework Element - Universal Race  
**Category:** Races / Species  
**Related:** [Genus Corvus](genus.md), [Races README](../README.md), [Earth Trial Mechanics](../../worlds/earth/trial-mechanics.md)

## Overview

Corvani (*Corvus morticus*) are crow-based anthropomorphic people. Self-designation: **Corvani** (or "the Corv" casually). Common names: **Crows**, **Blackwings**, Roof-Watchers (Manchester/Earth). Derogatory/affectionate: "The Death Birds," "Morticus." No designated role; traits lead to emergent healing and chain-mending. Passed trial by observing, remembering, and never being the first to act.

## Physical Characteristics

- **Height:** 5–6 feet when upright; often perch or lean forward
- **Feathers:** Jet-black with subtle iridescent sheen (blue-green-purple in direct light)
- **Eyes:** Large, intelligent black with faint silver rim
- **Beak:** Prominent, curved, long, strong, slightly hooked
- **Wings:** Powerful; flight capable; prefer gliding and short bursts
- **Hands/feet:** Taloned; grasping, climbing, delicate work
- **Clothing:** Minimal—dark cloaks or hooded wraps (weather, blending into night rooftops)
- **Presence:** Tall, dark, watchful; loom like omens when they descend—but help is real, quiet, reliable

## Species-Wide Trait: Hyper-Literal, Death-Framed

- Hyper-literal communication (say what you mean, expect same)
- Death-framed worldview (measure everything against mortality)
- Pattern recognition obsession (see patterns everywhere, must analyze)
- Brutal honesty (lying is inefficient and confusing)
- Detail fixation (notice and mention everything)
- Social obliviousness (miss emotional subtext entirely)
- Delayed emotional processing (feel deeply, but later, privately)

Not a disorder—species norm. Other species find it strange; corvids find other species strange.

## Natural Gravitation: Healers / Chain-Menders

**Why 60–70% become healers:**
- Death-framing → essential for triage, prognosis, treatment
- Brutal honesty → patients need accurate information
- Pattern recognition → diagnosis is pattern matching
- Detail obsession → symptoms are details; details save lives
- Emotional distance → can work on dying patients without breaking down
- Memory → remember every case, every treatment, every failure

"Medicine wants EXACTLY what I naturally do: truth, patterns, details, death awareness. So I became a healer. It's... efficient."

## Healing Methods

- **Chain-Mending:** Use recall to guide survivors through re-linking lost blocks. "Tell me what you remember. I saw it happen. Let me fill the gap."
- **Haze Filtration:** Beak ruff + herbal salves (Pachi-traded or scavenged) to reduce haze intake
- **Conversion Quarantine:** Can't reverse conversion, but can "quarantine" the monster-pattern and rebuild the original chain around it (leaves scars, preserves self)
- **Plague/Disease Response:** Spot outbreaks from above; deliver warnings or remedies via dropped packets
- **Emotional/Social Healing:** Mediators—memory ensures fair retellings of disputes ("I remember what was said. No lies.")

## Trial History & Earth Deployment

- Survived trial by observing, remembering, never being first to act
- Deploy to Earth during Prep Year via Platform 934 (Manchester) or smaller rifts as independent contractors (healing, chain-mending, observation)
- Gravitate to high ground: rooftops, overpasses, water towers, old mill chimneys

## On Earth (Manchester)

- Claim rooftops, overpasses, high medians as territory
- Known as "the Blackwings" or "the Roof-Watchers"
- Humans seek them for haze treatment or lost memories: "The Crows remember when no one else does"

## Agency & Inner Lives

- Full lives beyond healing: family roosts, storytelling rituals (recounting observations to reinforce chains), territorial disputes over prime perches
- Conflicts: Some resent the burden ("I carry too many ghosts"); others embrace it ("We are the ones who don't forget. That's our gift.")
- Insecurity: Unlike Pristails, they need **accuracy**, not crowds. A wrong memory is their haze: "If I forget, who will remember?"

## Story Role

Quiet, reliable healers and chain-menders. Hold the pieces humans can't hold themselves. Contrast with Iridari (validation-seeking, performative) and Bruin (enforcement, decisive). Corvani: observation, memory, truth.
