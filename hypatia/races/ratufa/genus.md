## Definition

Genus Ratufa is defined by the giant squirrel–derived genetic template from the FC genetic repository. Independent generalist species (like Chelonari, Pachi); no designated role. Origin unclear—natural evolution or panspermia-seeded; no FC claims credit. Passed trial via vertical refuge (canopy survival) and social cooperation.

### Physical
- **Squirrel-derived** — 4–5 feet tall, 60–90 lbs; lean, agile, climber's build
- **Flexible** — Can twist, contort; strong core for balance on branches and narrow ledges
- **Prehensile** — Hands and feet grasp; retractable claws for climbing

### Temperament
- **Social** — Need interaction; crave companionship; information-sharing instinct
- **Observational** — Notice everything; excellent memory; track relationships
- **Low aggression** — Prefer flight over fight; conflict resolution through social pressure
- **Height preference** — Ground = danger; high ground = safety (psychological comfort)
