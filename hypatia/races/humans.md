# Humans

**Status:** Framework Element - Universal Race  
**Category:** Races / Species  
**Related:** [Races README](README.md), [../FRAMEWORK.md](../../FRAMEWORK.md)

## Overview

Humans are the baseline intelligent species in Hypatia. They appear on many worlds and serve as the foundation for most mortal societies. While other races may have specialized abilities or longer lifespans, humans are characterized by their adaptability, diversity, and rapid population growth.

## Universal Characteristics

### Physical Attributes
- **Lifespan:** Typically 70-100 years (varies by world)
- **Size:** Medium build, average height
- **Adaptability:** Highly adaptable to different environments
- **Diversity:** Wide range of physical appearances, cultures, and capabilities

### Cognitive Abilities
- **Intelligence:** Average baseline intelligence, but wide variation
- **Learning:** Capable of rapid learning and adaptation
- **Creativity:** Known for innovation and problem-solving
- **Memory:** Standard memory capabilities

### Social Characteristics
- **Reproduction:** Relatively fast population growth
- **Community:** Form complex societies and civilizations
- **Culture:** Develop diverse cultural expressions
- **Conflict:** Capable of both cooperation and conflict

## Framework Role

### Universal Presence
- Humans appear on many worlds across Hypatia
- Often the dominant or one of the dominant species
- May interact with or coexist with other intelligent races
- Serve as baseline for comparison with other races

### Adaptability
- Can thrive in diverse environments
- Rapidly adapt to new situations
- Form varied cultural expressions
- May develop differently based on world conditions

## Variations Across Worlds

### World-Specific Adaptations
- Physical characteristics may vary by world
- Cultural expressions are highly variable
- May develop specialized abilities on some worlds
- Technology levels and social structures vary

### Interaction with Magic
- Humans may or may not have access to magic/quantum abilities
- On worlds with fragmented Quantumancy, humans may manifest abilities
- Magical capabilities vary by world rules
- May interact with magical systems differently than other races

## Story Implications

### As Protagonists
- Often serve as viewpoint characters
- Represent "baseline" perspective
- Allow exploration of other races through comparison
- Drive many stories through human ambition and conflict

### As Background
- Form the foundation of most civilizations
- Create the societies in which stories take place
- May be the majority population on many worlds
- Provide context for other races

## Iolinus Status

On Iolinus, humans appear to be the primary or only intelligent species. The various cultures (Astelli, Kitzengai, Llosca, Maneth, etc.) are all human cultures with different cultural expressions. Earth souls transferred to Iolinus inhabit human bodies.

## Notes

- Framework provides universal baseline characteristics
- Individual worlds may modify or expand on these basics
- Humans are flexible enough to fit diverse world settings
- Can serve multiple story functions across different worlds
