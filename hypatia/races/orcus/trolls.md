# Trolls (Orcus gigantus)

**Status:** Framework Element - Adversary  
**Category:** Races / Species  
**Related:** [Genus Orcus](genus.md), [Genetic Frameworks](../genetic-frameworks.md), [../../mechanics/trials.md](../../mechanics/trials.md)

## Overview

**WARNING: Adversary use only. Not for staff deployment.**

Trolls are Orcus gigantus—the elite/boss tier of the Orcus genus. Bridge guardians, area denial, near-immortal until someone learns fire. They live long enough (centuries) to get very smart. Know they're unkillable... until someone learns the weakness. Watch generation after generation die to the same pattern. Can't escape their nature. "We are eternal... until we're not. We guard... because we must."

## Framework Role: Trial Adversary

### Adversary Designation
Elite/boss enemy type

### Physical Traits
- 8-10 feet tall, massive build
- Enhanced regeneration (regrow limbs slowly over days/weeks)
- Extremely tough hide
- Green-brown skin, sometimes mossy growth
- Can eat almost anything

### Purpose in Trials
- Boss encounters (set piece battles)
- Bridge guardians (block progress)
- Area denial (control territory through presence)
- Teaching moment (learn exploitable weakness—fire/acid)

### Design Features
- Near-immortal (hard to kill permanently)
- Regenerate in place (territorial, can't leave)
- Slow but unstoppable
- Weakness to fire/acid (rewards knowledge)

### The Awareness
- Live long enough (centuries) to get very smart
- Know they're unkillable... until someone learns fire
- Watch generation after generation die to the same weakness
- Can't escape their nature
- "We are eternal... until we're not. We guard... because we must."

### Personality
- Slow-thinking, patient
- Solitary (don't need others)
- Territorial, simple but not stupid
- Calm until provoked (then very dangerous)
- Mournful, depressed (centuries of same role)

### Philosophy
- "Troll has guarded bridge for centuries. Different bridges, same purpose."
- "Troll dies. Troll regenerates. Troll guards again."
- "Troll does not hate you. Troll simply... IS."

### Expected Lifespan
Centuries (regenerative)

## Known Issues

Trolls develop depression (centuries of same role).

## Variations Across Worlds

- Bridge/territory assignments vary
- Regeneration rate and weakness specifics may differ
- Some worlds may have troll variants with different vulnerabilities
