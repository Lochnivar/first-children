# Ogres (Orcus magnus)

**Status:** Framework Element - Adversary  
**Category:** Races / Species  
**Related:** [Genus Orcus](genus.md), [Genetic Frameworks](../genetic-frameworks.md), [../../mechanics/trials.md](../../mechanics/trials.md)

## Overview

**WARNING: Adversary use only. Not for staff deployment.**

Ogres are Orcus magnus—the tank/siege tier of the Orcus genus. Heavy damage output, huge health pool, environmental hazards. Too stupid to fully understand their purpose. Just hungry, angry, fighting. "Ogre hungry. Ogre smash. Ogre die. Ogre... confused."

## Framework Role: Trial Adversary

### Adversary Designation
Heavy/siege enemy type

### Physical Traits
- 10-12 feet tall, enormously strong
- Proportionally massive
- Huge appetite (high caloric need)
- Yellowish-green to brown skin
- Not very bright (trade-off for size)
- Surprisingly fast when motivated

### Purpose in Trials
- Heavy damage output (teach dodging)
- Huge health pool (test sustained damage)
- Environmental hazards (can be tricked, redirected)
- Siege encounters (destroy fortifications)

### Design Features
- Maximum strength, demolition capability
- Slow (telegraphed attacks, learnable patterns)
- Stupid (exploitable, use environment against them)
- Food-motivated (can be distracted, comedic but dark)

### The Simplicity
- Too stupid to fully understand their purpose
- Just hungry, angry, fighting
- Die confused ("Why does food fight back?")
- "Ogre hungry. Ogre smash. Ogre die. Ogre... confused."

### Personality
- Simple, food-motivated
- Easily confused/angered
- Strong but lazy (need supervision)
- Childlike in some ways

### Expected Lifespan
5-10 years

## Known Issues

Ogres may refuse to fight if overfed. Hunger is primary motivator.

## Variations Across Worlds

- Siege and demolition roles may vary
- Food motivation and distractibility differ
- Some worlds may have ogre variants with different intelligence levels
