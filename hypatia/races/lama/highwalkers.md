# Highwalkers (Llama People)

**Status:** Framework Element - Universal Race  
**Category:** Races / Species  
**Related:** [Genus Lama](genus.md), [Races README](../README.md), [Sahirans](../camelus/sahirans.md), [../../FRAMEWORK.md](../../../FRAMEWORK.md)

## Overview

Highwalkers are llama-based anthropomorphic people created through FC panspermia. They aren't restricted to mountains—their traits make them suited to it. Efficient oxygen usage, sure-footedness, hypervigilance: ideal for high terrain, but that efficient O2 could suit divers (if they had fins). Not a designated role or biome lock. Their temperament is anxiety incarnate: perpetually worried about everything. Vigilance keeps them alive—and makes them incredibly capable despite (or because of) their worry.

## Universal Characteristics

### Physical Attributes
- 5-6 feet tall (shorter, stockier)
- Thick, woolly coats (natural insulation)
- White, brown, black, spotted patterns
- Sure-footed (split hooves, perfect balance)
- Large lungs (efficient oxygen usage—suits thin air, could suit diving in other forms)

### Emergent Tendencies
- Efficient O2, sure-footed, hypervigilant → suited to high terrain (common expression, not restriction)
- Could suit diving, cold, low-oxygen niches—same traits, different biome
- Often high-altitude transport, mountain guides—because traits make them ideal for it

### Temperament: Anxiety Incarnate
- Perpetually worried about EVERYTHING
- Nervous energy (constant fidgeting)
- Hypervigilant (scanning for dangers constantly)
- Worry spirals (what if, what if, what if)
- Catastrophize everything (small problem = disaster thinking)
- Need to CHECK things repeatedly
- Verbal processing (talk through anxieties constantly)
- Mountain survival requires paranoia—one mistake = death; prey animal instincts; checking everything = actually keeps them alive

### Culture
- Vertical settlements (embrace altitude)
- Slow, methodical movement (safety first)
- Communal care (guard each other)
- Meditation practices (mountain silence)
- Weaving traditions (use their own wool)

### Personality
- Calm demeanor when not actively worrying
- Cautious and careful
- Gentle protectiveness
- Quiet confidence (despite anxiety)
- Zen-like until threatened (then: aggressive spitting)
- Actually SUPER capable—anxiety doesn't stop them, enhances vigilance

## Story Potential

### Story Function
- Mountain survival expertise
- Vertical terrain solutions
- Anxiety comedy (constantly worried but incredibly competent)
- Calm-under-pressure contrast (worried entire time but never fails)
- Perfect foil to Sahiran phlegmatic calm

### Sahiran + Highwalker Dynamic
Highwalkers and Sahirans often hand off cargo at mountain-desert boundaries. Ultimate anxiety meets ultimate calm—comedy gold through contrast. Both professionally competent despite appearances.

### Example Dialogue
- Human: "We need to reach the peak settlement."
- Highwalker: "The peak! Yes! Okay! I can— wait, which peak? The north peak or— no, you said THE peak, so probably the main peak, but what if you meant— okay, assuming main peak, we need to— oh no, is it going to rain? It looks like it might rain. Rain on this route is very dangerous because the rocks get slippery and then we could FALL and—"
- Human: "Are you... okay?"
- Highwalker: "What? Yes! Fine! Totally fine! Just making sure we don't DIE. Because we could die! So many ways to die on a mountain!"

### Example Character
Mara Gentlestep, 67 years old, white wool, serene expression. "I can retrieve them. The mountain provides holds where you cannot see them. Trust the mountain. Trust me."

## Variations Across Worlds

- Mountain terrain and altitude vary
- Cultural expressions of anxiety differ
- Relationship to Sahirans at terrain boundaries may vary
