# Llamari (Llama People)

**Status:** Framework Element - Universal Race  
**Category:** Races / Species  
**Related:** [Genus Lama](genus.md), [Races README](../README.md), [Djemari](../camelus/djemari.md), [../../FRAMEWORK.md](../../../FRAMEWORK.md)

## Overview

Llamari are llama-based anthropomorphic people created through FC panspermia. Efficient oxygen usage, sure-footedness, hypervigilance. Not a designated role or biome lock. Their temperament is anxiety incarnate: perpetually worried about everything. Almost if OCD were given a personification—the checking, the worry spirals, the need to account for every possibility. Vigilance keeps them alive—and makes them incredibly capable despite (or because of) their worry.

## Universal Characteristics

### Physical Attributes
- 5-6 feet tall (shorter, stockier)
- Thick, woolly coats (natural insulation)
- White, brown, black, spotted patterns
- Sure-footed (split hooves, perfect balance)
- Large lungs (efficient oxygen usage)

### Emergent Tendencies
- Efficient O2, sure-footed, hypervigilant → transport, logistics, precision work
- Traits lead naturally to roles requiring vigilance and careful execution

### Temperament: Anxiety Incarnate
- Perpetually worried about EVERYTHING
- Nervous energy (constant fidgeting)
- Hypervigilant (scanning for dangers constantly)
- Worry spirals (what if, what if, what if)
- Catastrophize everything (small problem = disaster thinking)
- Need to CHECK things repeatedly
- Verbal processing (talk through anxieties constantly)
- Prey animal instincts; one mistake = death; checking everything = actually keeps them alive

### Culture
- Slow, methodical movement (safety first)
- Communal care (guard each other)
- Meditation practices (quiet focus)
- Weaving traditions (use their own wool)

### Truism
*"There's always time for one last check."*

### Personality
- Calm demeanor when not actively worrying
- Cautious and careful
- Gentle protectiveness
- Quiet confidence (despite anxiety)
- Zen-like until threatened (then: aggressive spitting)
- Actually SUPER capable—anxiety doesn't stop them, enhances vigilance

## Story Potential

### Story Function
- Logistics expertise, precision work
- Anxiety comedy (constantly worried but incredibly competent)
- Calm-under-pressure contrast (worried entire time but never fails)
- Perfect foil to Djemari phlegmatic calm

### Djemari + Llamari Dynamic
Llamari and Djemari often work together on long-haul routes. Ultimate anxiety meets ultimate calm—comedy gold through contrast. Both professionally competent despite appearances.

### Example Dialogue
- Human: "We need to complete the delivery."
- Llamari: "The delivery! Yes! Okay! I can— wait, which route? The north route or— no, you said THE delivery, so probably the main route, but what if you meant— okay, assuming main route, we need to— oh no, did we check the cargo? We should check the cargo. What if something shifted? Shifting cargo could cause a cascade failure and then we could LOSE EVERYTHING and—"
- Human: "Are you... okay?"
- Llamari: "What? Yes! Fine! Totally fine! Just making sure we don't FAIL. Because we could fail! So many ways for a delivery to go wrong!"

### Example Character
Mara Gentlestep, 67 years old, white wool, serene expression. "I can retrieve them. The route has holds where you cannot see them. Trust the route. Trust me."

## Variations Across Worlds

- Cultural expressions of anxiety differ
- Relationship to Djemari at handoff points may vary
