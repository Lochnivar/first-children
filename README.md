# Hypatia

**A cosmological framework for a multiverse of interconnected stories and worlds.**

Hypatia is the over-universe (the Library) that contains multiple worlds (Books) and stories, similar to Brandon Sanderson's Cosmere. Individual stories (like Iolinus) are subordinate to and must conform to the Hypatia framework.

**The Library Metaphor (Conceptual Framework):**
- **Prime Author** = The Author who created everything, then withdrew forever
- **Hypatia** = Conceptually understood as "The Library" containing all existence (from First Speech: simultaneously "library" and "creator")—purely conceptual, the closest approximation First Children or mortals can achieve
- **Worlds** = Conceptually understood as "Books" within the library, each telling their own story—a mental model, not literal structure
- **First Children** = The Author's first creations (many siblings) who conceptually "inhabit the library" and can "author books/worlds"—this metaphor helps them organize their understanding

**In the First Speech:**  
*hypatia* — simultaneously "library" and "creator."

## Framework Concepts

- **Prime Author** - The deistic creator of the universe who created the First Children (many siblings), then withdrew forever
- **First Children** - The cohort of siblings created by the Prime Author; each can author complete worlds from nothing; possess full Quantumancy
- **Multiple Worlds** - Worlds authored by First Children (by one, by several in collaboration, or contested when visions clash), each with different rules and magic
- **Soul Transfer** - Mechanism for transferring souls between worlds
- **Life Force Binding** - Binding a world's life force to its creator (hostage mechanism)
- **Quantumancy** - Universal quantum manipulation force (full in First Children, fragmented in mortals)

## Usage

When working with stories in this universe:
1. **Check the framework first** - All stories must conform to `FRAMEWORK.md`
2. **Document conformance** - Each world should have a `CONSISTENCY_CHECK.md`
3. **Process contradictions** - See `meta/CONSISTENCY_PROCESS.md` for handling conflicts
4. **Respect the hierarchy** - Framework wins over individual story details
