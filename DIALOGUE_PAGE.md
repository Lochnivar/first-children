### Weavers (*Iteratum textori*)

Canonical documentation: [oververse/races/iteratum/weavers.md](oververse/races/iteratum/weavers.md)

Voice notes: matter-of-fact about iteration-end; resist external tragedy framings without being dismissive of them; library cognition produces a specific kind of precision — they know what they know without feeling it, and they're clear about the difference; long pauses are comfortable; they tend to correct imprecise framings of their own existence gently and specifically, not defensively.

*Key line that captures the register:* "I have access to their memories. I don't remember them. There's a difference."
