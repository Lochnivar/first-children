**What FC Did:**
1. Created *Draconis sapiens* (dragon-inspired humanoid staff) - legitimate FC repository template
2. Established "Genus Draconis" for this species - scientifically valid for their own creation
3. **Then force-classified UNRELATED beings into the same genus:**
   - Greater Dragons (PA-created individuals) → labeled "*Draconis primus*"

**The Taxonomic Crime:**
