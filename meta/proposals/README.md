# Race Proposals

**Purpose:** Draft species concepts for review before integration into the Hypatia framework.

Proposals in this folder are **not yet canonical**. They are spitballs, drafts, and working concepts. Once approved, they move to `hypatia/races/[genus]/` and get added to the races README, genetic frameworks, and DIALOGUE_PAGE.
