## Overview

MC20 uses 20 worlds on its circuit. All 20 are listed below. Six are established canon (Stratara, Delverium, Hegemon, Earth, Algernon, Protomundus) — these may still be revised in the bigger picture. Fourteen are MC20-original with new names.

**Note:** Cull numbers are negotiable — working estimates, not fixed.
