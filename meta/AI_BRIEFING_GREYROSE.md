## Executive Summary

**Greyroses** are the Iolinus-specific mechanism for soul transfer, created by Nala as her interface to Earth souls and as marks of Apostasy membership. They are actual roses embedded in flesh (NOT tattoos) that enable soul transfer, blood purification, and contain a suicide mechanism. Discovery by the Church of Malachor is a death sentence.

**Key Points:**
- Iolinus-specific implementation of universal soul transfer mechanics
- Created by Nala after the Shattering to enable liberation operations
- Two-stage process: native volunteers become 'rosed (cached), then Earth souls transfer in
- Soul stack architecture: physical layer (hardware) vs. soul layer (operating system)
- Operational security: suicide mechanism, placement variations, information hierarchy

## Technical Function: Soul Stack Architecture

### The Metaphor

Think of it as **hardware vs. operating system**:

**Physical Layer (Hardware):**
- The actual brain tissue
- Neural pathways
- Muscle memory
- Procedural knowledge
- All of Laila's physical training/skills
- **This stays intact**

**Soul Layer (Operating System):**
- Consciousness/self
- Personality
- Episodic memories (personal experiences)
- Identity/sense of self
- **THIS is what gets swapped**

**Greyrose (Interface/Cache):**
- Nala's technology for soul manipulation
- Caches the native soul (Laila's consciousness)
- Provides interface for Earth soul (Gabby's consciousness)
- Enables the swap while preserving hardware

### How It Works

**Stage 1: Native Volunteer Becomes 'Rosed**
1. Native Iolinian volunteers
2. Greyrose embedded in flesh
3. Becomes 'rosed
4. Joins waiting pool
5. Native soul is **cached** in the greyrose
6. Can happen to anyone willing

**Stage 2: Soul Transfer (When Needed)**
1. Earther soul dies on Earth
2. Nala captures it
3. Chooses host from pool
4. Performs actual Ceremony
5. Earther installed, native cached
6. Only when Earther available

**The Gap:**
- Could be years between stages
- Or immediate if Earther ready
- Some die waiting (never host)
- Some host quickly
- Unpredictable timing

### What Gets Preserved

**Physical Layer (Preserved):**
- All of Laila's physical training
- Muscle memory
- Procedural knowledge
- Neural pathways
- Body's learned skills

**Soul Layer (Replaced):**
- Consciousness (Gabby's, not Laila's)
- Personality (Gabby's)
- Episodic memories (Gabby's Earth memories)
- Identity (Gabby's sense of self)

**Result:**
- Gabby has all of Laila's memories and skills that are in the "hardware" of the brain
- What is replaced is basically the operating system/soul
- Gabby can access Laila's training but with Gabby's consciousness
