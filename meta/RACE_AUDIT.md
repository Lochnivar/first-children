## WORKING NOTES

**Relationship sections are provisional.** Any relationship note referencing a NEEDS WORK or STUB species should be treated as a placeholder until that species is finalized. When a species moves to SOLID, revisit any SOLID entries that reference it and verify the relationship descriptions still hold.

**Naming conventions require author approval before finalizing.** Draft naming systems during species development but confirm with the author before treating them as canonical. This applies to new entries and rebuilds alike.

---

## COMMON GAPS ACROSS THE ROSTER

Nearly every entry has at least one of these missing:

- **Naming conventions** — absent or stubbed in roughly 70% of entries
- **Author's Narrative** — the most commonly skipped section
- **Trial History** — absent in most entries outside the SOLID tier
- **Theology** — nearly universally empty
- **Example characters** — absent in approximately half the roster

---

## DEVELOPMENT PRIORITY SUGGESTIONS

*For discussion — not a fixed order.*

1. **Otter-folk** — Strong concept, needs the Bandari treatment.
2. **Hobgoblins** — "Banality of evil" bureaucrat has real potential.
3. **Llamari** — Anxiety mechanic needs development; Djemari/Llamari dynamic can't fully land until both are realized.
4. **Pachi** — Philosophical wanderlust needs depth.
5. **Quedari** — Passive philosophy needs expansion to be narratively useful.
6. **Stubs (Aubrei, Dwarves, Elves, Humans)** — Lower priority unless story requires them.
