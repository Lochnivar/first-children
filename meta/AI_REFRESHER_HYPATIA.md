## Framework Overview

**Hypatia** is the over-universe framework (conceptually "The Library") containing all worlds (conceptually "Books"). The framework establishes universal cosmological rules, metaphysics, mechanics, and principles that govern all worlds within Hypatia.

**Key Principle:** The framework is the PRIMARY source of truth. Individual worlds (like Iolinus) are SUBORDINATE and must conform to framework rules, though they may expand or provide exceptions for world-specific elements.

**Document Hierarchy:**
1. `FRAMEWORK.md` (Root) = PRIMARY SOURCE OF TRUTH
2. `hypatia/` (Directory) = Detailed supplementary documentation
3. `hypatia/worlds/[world]/` = World-specific exceptions/expansions

## Core Cosmology

### The Deepest Truth

**Everything is energy. Everything is quantum.**

This is the absolute bedrock of Hypatia's cosmology. There's no "other" realm. No separate spirit world. No elemental planes. No divine essence outside the rules. It's all one vast, indifferent quantum sea.

- **Mortal bodies?** Coherent energy patterns bound tightly by forces, pretending to be solid for a lifetime.
- **Ghosts and spirits?** The same patterns, just looser—echoes that haven't fully dissipated.
- **Rift spawns?** Raw quantum collapses, some stable enough to persist, most unraveling like half-remembered dreams.
- **Magic, technology, souls, gods themselves?** All manipulations or expressions of the same underlying quantum layer.
- **Even the First Children**—for all their godlike spark—are just more complex, self-sustaining waveforms dancing on the same substrate.

The First Children are the only ones who can swim in the quantum sea without drowning (most of the time).

### The Prime Author

**Nature:**
- The true, deistic creator of the entire universe (formerly called "Big 'G' God")
- Created existence and a cohort of First Children (many siblings), then withdrew permanently and will not return
- Not actively involved in world events—withdrawal is permanent and final (will not return)
- Established the fundamental **physical laws** of reality (immutable constants of existence)
- Does not establish moralistic rules—these are First Children constructs
- Source of natural laws and the ultimate quantum foundation

**Terminology Note:** While framework documentation uses "Prime Author" for the actual being, First Children in-world may refer to this concept as "The Author" in their cosmology. Different worlds/mortals may have completely different conceptions, or none at all.

**Role in Stories:**
- Background presence, not an active character—withdrew forever after creation
- Represents the "true" creator versus the First Children who came after
- Source of the physical laws that First Children cannot violate
- Provides the cosmological "why" behind conflicts, but is absent from them

### The First Children (The Siblings / Little 'g' Gods)

**What They Are:**
- The first children/creations of the Prime Author—many siblings (the exact number is intentionally left as a gray area)
- They refer to the Prime Author as "The Author" in their cosmology
- Glorified waveforms (patterns of coherent energy at the quantum layer) that are sentient and godlike—cannot be truly destroyed but can be reformed (effectively the same thing)
- Possess full **Quantumancy** - the complete spectrum of quantum manipulation abilities
- Essentially powerful beings (aliens/dimensional entities) who assume deistic identities

**Background Detail:**
- The "what they really are" (waveforms/energy patterns) is background world-building only
- For story purposes, they are simply gods with godly powers
- Their true nature/origin is out of scope for individual stories
- The framework allows for many First Children, not just those encountered in specific worlds

**Powers: Quantumancy**
- All quantum abilities combined in one being:
  1. Quantumancers - Manipulate quantum reality
  2. Readers - Read quantum states (past/present/future truth)
  3. Weavers - Manipulate quantum threads (reality weaving, health assessment)
  4. Echoes - Create quantum echoes (view past events)
  5. Nullwraiths - Nullify quantum effects (anti-magic, invisible to quantum perception)
- Only First Children possess full Quantumancy
- Mortals may manifest fragments of these abilities (persecuted on many worlds)

**Characteristics:**
- **Immortal and Ancient:** Cannot be killed through normal means (no traditional death). Have existed since the early universe as persistent coherent energy patterns. Can be reformed beyond recognition or functionality (practical destruction).
- **Personality Variation:** Range from wise and compassionate to immature and cruel. Each First Child has their own motivations and goals.
- **Relationships:** All First Children are technically siblings (they all share the same creator, the Prime Author). There's a nominal equality between them, but some are "more equal than others" (power/hierarchy differences). Social orders are still undetermined, will be developed as needed for stories.
- **Limitations:** Bound by **physical laws** (from Prime Author) and **moralistic rules** (First Children constructs). Cannot violate physical laws (these are immutable constants of existence). May violate moralistic rules, but face peer judgment and enforcement—First Children are responsible for violations and enforcement.

**Mortal Awareness:**
- First Children ultimately control what mortals know on their creations
- Some worlds are open about the cosmology, some are closed, and some actively encourage belief that the creator is the only god
- Mortal awareness varies widely by world and by design

### First Speech

**What It Is:**
- The original conceptual framework/terminology from the Prime Author
- Provides foundational vocabulary for First Children
- Purely conceptual, the closest approximation First Children or mortals can achieve

**Key Term:**
- *hypatia* — simultaneously "library" and "creator" (from First Speech)

### The Library Metaphor

**Note:** The Library metaphor is **purely conceptual**—it's not a literal description of Hypatia's structure. However, it's the closest that First Children or any mortal mind is going to approach understanding the true nature of Hypatia.

**Conceptual Framework:**
- **Prime Author** = The actual being who created everything, then withdrew permanently
- **"The Author"** = First Children's term/concept for the Prime Author (in-world terminology)
- **Hypatia** = Conceptually understood as "The Library" containing all existence
- **Worlds** = Conceptually understood as "Books" within the library, each telling their own story
- **First Children** = Conceptually "inhabit the library" and can "author new books/worlds"

This metaphor provides a useful conceptual framework for understanding Hypatia, even though it's an approximation, not literal truth.

## Universal Metaphysics

### Quantum Foundation

**The Deepest Truth:** Everything is energy. Everything is quantum.

All existence operates at the quantum layer:
- Physical matter is coherent energy patterns bound by forces
- Ghosts and spirits are looser coherent patterns
- Magic is quantum manipulation
- Technology that appears "magical" is quantum manipulation through different interfaces
- Souls are coherent quantum patterns
- First Children are complex, self-sustaining waveforms

**No Separate Realms:**
- No spirit world separate from physical reality
- No elemental planes
- No divine essence outside quantum rules
- Everything exists within the same quantum substrate

### Coherent Energy Patterns

**Fundamental Nature:**
- All existence—mortal bodies, ghosts, spirits, rift spawns, even seemingly solid matter—is ultimately **patterns of coherent energy** sustained at the quantum layer
- Physical bodies are tightly bound patterns stabilized by electromagnetic, strong, and weak forces (plus gravity)
- Ghosts/spirits are looser, less dense coherent patterns that persist without a full physical substrate
- They are quantum "echoes" or self-sustaining waveforms that retain identity, memory, or intent

**How They Form:**
- **Death Echoes:** Strong emotional or quantum imprint at death can leave a coherent pattern (classic "ghost")
- **Rift Spawns:** Some stable rift outputs manifest as spectral entities—energy patterns that never solidify into matter
- **Deliberate Creation:** A First Child or advanced mortal practitioner can shape coherent energy patterns intentionally
- **Cultural Magic Interfaces:** Many "spirit magic" traditions are simply schools for perceiving, communicating with, or stabilizing these patterns

**Compatibility:**
- Ghosts/spirits are fully compatible with the unified quantum-manipulation foundation
- Require no exception to the "all magic is quantum" rule
- They are just another culturally interpreted expression: mortal minds wrapping comprehensible stories around raw coherent waveforms

## Universal Magic Systems

### Quantumancy

**Definition:** The complete spectrum of quantum manipulation abilities. Only First Children possess full Quantumancy.

**Core Limitation:**
- Requires constant enforcement without an anchor
- Without an anchor, quantum effects require persistent mental/quantum focus
- Mortals attempting quantum manipulation without anchors experience "haze" (madness, loss of self)
- Anchors are stable constructs that offload persistence, allowing quantum effects to persist without constant enforcement

**The Five Abilities (Full Quantumancy):**
1. **Quantumancers** - Manipulate quantum reality directly
2. **Readers** - Read quantum states (past/present/future truth)
3. **Weavers** - Manipulate quantum threads (reality weaving, health assessment)
4. **Echoes** - Create quantum echoes (view past events)
5. **Nullwraiths** - Nullify quantum effects (anti-magic, invisible to quantum perception)

**Mortals and Fragments:**
- Mortals may manifest fragments of these abilities (if they manifest at all)
- The specific split/fragmentation is world-specific
- Mortals with quantum abilities are often persecuted on many worlds
- Mortals require anchors to use quantum abilities safely (without "haze")

**All Magic is Quantum:**
- All "magic" in Hypatia ultimately boils down to quantum manipulation
- "Magic" systems are schools and methods that let the mortal mind comprehend something way above their natural abilities
- Different worlds may have different cultural interpretations and methods, but they all manipulate the same quantum substrate

## Universal Mechanics

### World Creation

**Who Can Create:**
- Each First Child *can* author complete worlds from nothing (but not all do—some First Children may never create worlds)
- Worlds may be shaped by one sibling alone (most common), by several in collaboration (possible but not necessarily common—raises questions of ownership/control, "whose world is it?"—potential fuel for conflicts), or become contested when visions clash

**Legality:**
- World creation legality is a **peer judgment** system among First Children
- Not explicit Prime Author prohibitions
- First Children judge whether a sibling is too immature, dangerous, or otherwise unfit to create
- Creating against peer judgment is "against prohibition" (violates First Children moralistic rules)
- First Children are responsible for violations and enforcement

**Collaboration:**
- Multiple First Children can collaborate on world creation
- Collaboration is possible but not necessarily common
- Raises ownership questions ("whose world is it?")—potential fuel for conflicts
- No personality stereotypes should be established for First Children

### Notable First Children — Mercara (Quick Guide)
- **Core:** Puzzle-obsessed station-keeper of Mercara's Cross; focuses on optimizing transit schedules, gating intervals, and quantum stability.
- **Voice & Tone:** Detail-obsessed, methodical, warmly whimsical, and gently sarcastic in public announcements.
- **Mannerisms:** Often disguises themself as a timetable clerk, tea vendor, or conductor to observe and make micro-adjustments.
- **Portrayal guidance for AI:**
  - **Do:** Use Mercara for system/puzzle-focused scenes, logistics dilemmas, and witty timetable announcements. When asked to generate content in their voice, keep messages short, mechanically precise, and lightly teasing.
  - **Don't:** Make Mercara omnipotent or place them above other First Children; do not invent world-core narrative prose unless explicitly requested (see `ai-rules/golden-rule-no-original-text.md`).
- **Signature quote:** “Where we’re going, we don’t need platforms.”

### Soul Transfer

**Mechanism:**
- Requires specific mechanisms (greyrose or equivalent) to transfer souls between worlds
- Not all worlds have soul transfer capabilities
- Used for liberation operations and other purposes

**Purpose:**
- Liberation operations (bringing souls from one world to another to aid in liberation)
- Other purposes as needed for stories

### Liberation Operations

**Pattern:**
- A First Child observes another First Child's abuse of their creation
- The observing First Child initiates liberation operations
- Often involves bringing souls from other worlds (like Earth) to aid in liberation
- Liberation is complex and often requires hidden operations
- Liberation is morally complex—not simple good vs. evil

**Narrative Framework:**
- Framework supports diverse story types
- Allows for standalone or interconnected narratives
- Focus on character-driven plots

## Universal Rules and Guidelines

### Cosmological Constants

**What Must Remain Consistent:**
- Prime Author is the true creator (created reality and First Children, then withdrew permanently and will not return)
- First Children have Quantumancy (full quantum manipulation)
- Mortals may have fragmented quantum abilities (if they manifest at all, split is world-specific)
- All "magic" is quantum manipulation
- Quantumancy's core limitation: requires constant enforcement without an anchor, leading to "haze" for mortals
- Anchors are stable constructs that offload persistence
- Soul transfer requires mechanisms (greyrose or equivalent)
- Creating against peer judgment is "against prohibition"
- The deepest truth: Everything is energy. Everything is quantum
- Ghosts, spirits, and non-corporeal entities exist as coherent energy patterns at the quantum layer
- First Children are glorified waveforms that cannot be truly destroyed but can be reformed (effectively the same thing)

### Physical Laws vs. Moralistic Rules

**Physical Laws (Prime Author):**
- Established by the Prime Author before withdrawal
- Immutable constants of existence
- Cannot be violated by anyone, including First Children
- Examples: Fundamental forces, quantum mechanics, causality

**Moralistic Rules (First Children):**
- Constructs created by First Children
- Enforced by First Children peer judgment
- Can be violated (though violators face consequences)
- Examples: World creation prohibitions, rules about intervention

**Responsibility:**
- First Children are responsible for violations and enforcement of moralistic rules
- Physical laws are simply immutable—no enforcement needed

### Expansion Guidelines

**When Creating New Worlds:**
- Must conform to cosmological constants
- Can expand on universal elements
- Can provide world-specific exceptions (documented in world files)
- Must respect physical laws (cannot violate)
- Can interpret moralistic rules differently (but still subject to peer judgment)

**Essential Questions:**
- How does this world demonstrate framework concepts?
- What world-specific expansions or exceptions does it provide?
- How do mortals perceive the cosmology? (Open, closed, or actively encouraged creator-as-only-god belief)
- What quantum abilities (if any) do mortals have? (World-specific fragmentation)
- How does soul transfer work? (If applicable)
- What liberation operations exist? (If applicable)

## Document Organization

### Single Point of Truth Hierarchy

1. **`FRAMEWORK.md` (Root) = PRIMARY SOURCE OF TRUTH**
   - Comprehensive overview of all universal framework elements
   - Single authoritative reference for framework rules and cosmology
   - Contains all essential framework information in one place
   - **Purpose:** To be the definitive reference—anyone reading this should understand the complete framework

2. **`hypatia/` (Directory) = DETAILED SUPPLEMENTARY DOCUMENTATION**
   - Detailed, expanded explanations of framework elements
   - Cross-referenced navigation and organized categorization by topic
   - Provides depth and context beyond what is presented in `FRAMEWORK.md`
   - **Purpose:** To serve as a detailed reference, cross-reference hub, and organized categorization for specific topics
   - **Relationship to `FRAMEWORK.md`:** These files expand upon but **never contradict** `FRAMEWORK.md`

3. **`hypatia/worlds/[world]/` (Directory) = WORLD-SPECIFIC EXCEPTIONS/EXPANSIONS**
   - Documentation for individual worlds
   - Details world-specific elements that either **expand** upon or provide **exceptions** to the universal framework rules
   - Each world's documentation must conform to the framework but can add unique, world-specific depth
   - **Purpose:** To show how individual worlds work within the framework, demonstrating its flexibility

### Key Principles of Information Flow

- **Framework Rules:** Defined authoritatively in `FRAMEWORK.md` (primary), then detailed and expanded upon in `hypatia/` files (supplementary)
- **World Details:** World-specific expansions or exceptions are documented within `hypatia/worlds/[world]/` (subordinate)
- **Updates:** Any changes to core framework rules must first be made in `FRAMEWORK.md`, and then reflected in relevant `hypatia/` files
- **No Duplicate Authoritative Statements:** Only `FRAMEWORK.md` states universal rules authoritatively. `hypatia/` files elaborate, and world files demonstrate application or exceptions

## Framework vs. World Details

**The Framework = General/Universal Rules**

This framework contains the **"all or general"** things that apply universally across Hypatia:
- Cosmological constants (Prime Author, First Children, quantum foundation)
- Universal mechanics (soul transfer, world creation)
- Fundamental principles (physical laws from Prime Author; moralistic rules are First Children constructs, enforced by them; story patterns)
- Universal elements (races, themes, character types)

**World Details = Expansions or Exceptions**

Individual world documentation contains world-specific details that are either:

- **Expansions:** Adding more depth and specificity **within** the framework rules
  - Example: Framework says "mortals may have fragmented quantum abilities" → World expands this with specific abilities and their detailed rules

- **Exceptions:** World-specific variations that work **within or modify** general rules
  - Example: Framework establishes universal principles → World may have unique cultural interpretations of those principles

**Key Principle:** World details must respect framework constants but can expand or provide exceptions for world-specific elements. The framework provides the foundation; worlds build upon it with their unique details.

## Common Pitfalls to Avoid

1. **Don't confuse framework with world-specific details**
   - Framework = universal rules
   - World-specific = expansions/exceptions documented in world files

2. **Don't violate cosmological constants**
   - Prime Author withdrew permanently (will not return)
   - First Children are waveforms (background detail, not story focus)
   - Everything is quantum (no separate realms)

3. **Don't establish personality stereotypes for First Children**
   - They vary widely in personality and motivations
   - No "typical" First Child behavior

4. **Don't forget the deepest truth**
   - Everything is energy. Everything is quantum.
   - This applies to ALL existence, including ghosts, spirits, magic, technology, souls, and First Children themselves

5. **Don't confuse physical laws with moralistic rules**
   - Physical laws = immutable (Prime Author)
   - Moralistic rules = First Children constructs (can be violated, but face consequences)

6. **Don't assume all worlds are the same**
   - Mortal awareness varies by world
   - Quantum ability fragmentation is world-specific
   - Cultural interpretations vary widely
