~ Both.  Humans were created with the intention/design of being FC staff.  They are also candidates in the sense that all races have to go through trials now as candidates for finalization into the FC genetic repo.


### Q6: FC designer — singular or plural?

~ First Children is a proper name, so it could be both.  For the purposes of Earth, I'm thinking one designer.
